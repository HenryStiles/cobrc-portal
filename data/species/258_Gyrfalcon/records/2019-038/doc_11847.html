Description of the Bird

Bird was perched on a power pole for the entire duration of the observation. Large raptor similar in size and shape of a Peregrine Falcon or Gyrfalcon.  Head coloration more uniform than expected on a PEFA; lack of distict helmet or mustache. Dark streaking on white breast transitioned to barring on belly. Tail extension well past wing tips with some distinct barring on tail. Back had a silver gray color. Light/white underwing coloration when opening wings while perched.

Similar Species Discussion

Distinct falcon specie. Peregrine Falcon is the common specie for the area. Head coloration, light underwing coloration, long tail extension, and strong barring on tail eliminated PEFA. Other falcon species are relatively much smaller.
