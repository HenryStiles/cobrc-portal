Similar Species Discussion

Prairie, Peregrine were easy to rule out based on plumage details and the size of the bird (and it's long tail). 

Saker and Lanner Falcons are actually tricky alternatives to consider. Saker wouldn't look this dark gray and the streaking on the underparts looks off for that species. They also rarely show the grayish legs of this bird, and as far as I know rarely if ever show the broad diffuse gray on the side of the face, and instead have more of a distinct moustache in front of a more distinct pale cheak, and don't look this dark headed. I think Lanner Falcon can be similarly ruled out by the darker diffuse head and low-constrast underwing, long tail. Geography also comes into play ruling these two out.
