Description of the Bird

A small hummingbird with purple on the throat.  Please see photos, all taken by me on 2 Decemer 2013.  The bird came into the feeders three times, and stayed for nice looks and photos.  The bird was found on 1 November 2013 by the caretakers of the private property.  The bird was seen by a few birders on 27 November 2013 (Bill Maynard, Jeannie Mitchell, and Mark Peterson).  A few more birders also saw the bird on 3 December 2013.  The bird came into the feeders again on 4 December 2013.  I think this was the last date the bird was seen in the wild, before the arctic cold front came. 

Similar Species Discussion

Purple in the gorget and head, elimnate all other species.
