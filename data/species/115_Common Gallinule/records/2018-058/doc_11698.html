Description of the Bird


Continuing bird previously reported for a number of days. Stayed out of sight for many hours and several visits before observed. 


Came in via reeds. Stayed in the reeds partially visible most of the time, but did enter small (30-35' wide) water area back in the marsh for full views. This bird seemed very shy overall, considering how seldom it was visible.


No calls were heard.

Similar Species Discussion


American Coot - this bird had a white flank-stripe and a red shield on its forehead, with a brightly yellow-tipped bill.

Resources Used


Sibley.
