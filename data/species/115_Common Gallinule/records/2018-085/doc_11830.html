Description of the Bird

Mostly dark chicken-like marsh bird.  Most of the body was black with the back a dark brown.  The beak and shield were red with a yellow tip on the bill.  There was a white horizontal line on the flanks. 

Similar Species Discussion

American Coot has a white will and no horizontal line on the flanks.
