Description of the Bird

Bird found by Tanja Britton and reported on WhatsApp for county birders. When I arrived the bird was working along the reeds on the north edge of the pond. It was plain to see the birght red bill with yellow time, the bright yellow legs, the brown back and white strip down the side. It is an adult bird in breeding plumage. I have seen many common gallinules in Florida, Texas, and Arizona, so I am familiar with them. A great bird for El Paso county.

Similar Species Discussion

The only remotely similar bird to the gallinule in the area is the American Coot. Other that similar shape and activities, the birds are not alike.
