Description of the Bird

Size and shape of adjacent American Coot , Red shield , yellow tip to bill , white in tail and white flank line , long greenish yellow legs , otherwise black body .Foraging along northern shore in and out of reeds .See photos .Not Purple Gallinule as body black not purple , legs greenish yellow not bright yellow .
