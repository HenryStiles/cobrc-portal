Description of the Bird

Mostly black chicken-like marsh bird similar to American Coot.  Obvious facial shield and beak with a yellow tip.  Most of the bird was black with a white horizontal stripe on the flanks, and a dark brown back.  The legs were yellow/greenish. 

Similar Species Discussion

American Coot typically has a white bill and also is typically swimming rather than walking in the reeds.
