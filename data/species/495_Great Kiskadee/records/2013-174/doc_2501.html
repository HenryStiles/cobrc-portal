Description of the Bird

-a little larger and stockier than a Western Kingbird
-striking striped head pattern (is what caught my attention)
-black crown with strong white line below
-strong black stripe from bill to back of head below the white stripe
-fairly heavy bill
-white throat
-yellow belly
-plain rufous back with tail and wings more reddish
Behavior: -stood on the rocks by the water feature
-stood in the very very shallow water where water moves over rocks between two ponds
-fed on insects? from water
-have some photos of food items in mouth that will be sent to David Leatherman to determine what it might be
- the entire time was spent in this location
Call: never heard
Plumage: adult
