Description of the Bird

Medium sized warbler with blackish brown bill. Dark eye. Some chestnut on crown mixed with some olive. Crown also slightly streaked black. Buffy split eye-ring. Gray face. Buffy neck patch. Upperparts gray with some olive and black streaking. Two obvious white wingbars. Wings blackish-gray with pale gray edges. Light chestnut on sides. Tail grayish black with gray feather edges.
Behaviors: When first seen, the bird was in the hand after being mist netted. After being banded, I saw the bird at least 3 more times actively foraging away from the banding station.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult
