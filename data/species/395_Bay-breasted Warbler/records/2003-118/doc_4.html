Description of the Bird

A richly colored warbler, with deep chestnut flanks, throat, and cap. Pale yellow nape and belly were also visible. Dark wings with two white wing bars. Black mask. Dark warbler-shaped bill.
Behaviors: The bird was mainly trying to keep out of the wind, hopping along on the ground or in low branches.
Call: not heard
Plumage: Adult Alternate

Similar Species Discussion

I'm not familiar with other warblers with the rich chestnut colored cap and throat combined with the dark mask and yellow nape.
