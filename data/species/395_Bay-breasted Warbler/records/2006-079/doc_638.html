Description of the Bird

A colorful warbler dark throat, peach colored ear patch, two white wing bars, buffy underparts, short tail. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying in the tall trees.
Call: singing quite a lot, high pitched, not sure how it describe it.
Plumage: first spring male

Similar Species Discussion
