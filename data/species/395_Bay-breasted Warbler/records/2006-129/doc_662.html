Description of the Bird

Greenish head, bright white wing bars, dark legs, green back, lack of streaking on the back, light dark chestnut color on sides, long tail, yellow buffy throat, cremy white under parts. Small bird with pointed bill, moving around a lot, catching bugs.
Behaviors: I first spotted it up in the spruce trees. Later it put on a show for 20 minutes flying back and forth in front of us.
Call: a high chip note
Plumage: first fall male

Similar Species Discussion

The color on the sides elimnates Blackpoll Warbler, as does the long tail. No other warbler looks like this bird.
