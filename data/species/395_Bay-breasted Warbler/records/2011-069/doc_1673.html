Description of the Bird

Rufous-crowned large warbler moving sluggishly 10 feet above ground. Rufous flanks. Whitish belly. 2 large whitish wingbars. Buffy just behind dark blackish auriculars. Dark streaks on back.
Behaviors: Foraging slowly in plains cottonwood, ignoring miller moths in favor of other smaller prey items.
Call: none heard
Plumage: breeding
