Description of the Bird

First year male Summer Tanager.  Splotched plumage with red on head, splotched red and yellow on breast, red splotches on olivey-green wings.  Feeding on suet.  Not easily intimidated by Pine Siskins and Cassin's Finches also feeding in area.  Ignored the oranges offered.

Previous Experience

I have seen this species in Texas and New Mexico.  I've seen Western Tanagers in Colorado.  Scarlet Tanagers in the east and High Island, Tx during migration.  Hepatic Tanager in SE Arizona and New Mexico
