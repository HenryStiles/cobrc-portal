Description of the Bird

medium sized woodpecker with black back, black bib and white underparts with black streaks. White rump, black tail, bright red patch on back of head, black around white eye with white on forehead, cheeks & chin giving bird the "clownish appearance" it's known for. Long black bill. In flight, white rump and wing patch clearly visible.
Behaviors: Bird was perched high in a tree, occasionally pecking half-heartedly at the bark - not excavating holes. After 15 minutes or so, the bird climbed to a higher perch and then flew off in a westerly direction, presumably to forage.
