Description of the Bird

My favorite woodpecker - looks like a clown. Black-backed, white chest/belly with some black streaking. Heavy black bill topped with white/yellow across the "bridge of the nose". Black face with a bold red skullcap. Eye pale with a dark iris. White wing patch.
Behaviors: Sitting in a tree looking miserable following the snowsquall that had just ended. Rather fluffed out.
