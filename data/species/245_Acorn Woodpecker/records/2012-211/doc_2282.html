Description of the Bird

Woodpecker with glossy black back , showing white rump and white wing patches in flight , white and black facial pattern , red crown , white iris , black streaking on upper breast .
Behaviors: Repeatedly taking peanuts from a feeder and placing them in the main trunk of an adjacent cottonwood. Residential area.
Call: Load weka weka call matching that on my Bird Jam
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other woodpecker has this combination of features.
