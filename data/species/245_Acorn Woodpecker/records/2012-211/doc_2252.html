Description of the Bird

Dark backed woodpecker with bright white wing patches in flight. Red cap did not extend to the white above the bill. Yellowish white on throat. Black streaked breast turning to white underneath.
Behaviors: Often froze on tree branches at times aggressively defended the area around the nest from starlings.
Call: Gave some rattling calls when agitated
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Very unique looking woodpecker.
