Description of the Bird

A medium-sized woodpecker with distinctive plumage: red crown patch separated from white forehead by band of black; white of forehead continuing posteriorily between black around and under eye and then isolating black on chin/throat; black around eye and on auriculars extending around nape and down neck and onto and across chest, with bottom edge of black on chest extending in variable streaking; underparts otherwise off white; white basal-primary patch on black wings
Behaviors: perched and calling from tree, then clinging to trunk
Call: distinctive, gravelly, somewhat rattling calls that I find otherwise undescribable
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing really similar; White-headed has similar wing pattern, but very different head pattern
