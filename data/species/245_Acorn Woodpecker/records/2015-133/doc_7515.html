Description of the Bird

This Acorn Woodpecker has been observed many times at this location since being found by Marty Wolf in December 2012.  Distinctive characteristics are black back, white patch at base of primaries, white rump, black streaks on undersides, red cap, and clownish face.  It is identified as female by the partial red cap.

Similar Species Discussion

The characteristics described previously make this species easily identifiable.
