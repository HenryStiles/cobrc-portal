Description of the Bird

Heard only bird at parking stop one of the western part of Tamarack state wildlife area, west of highway 55.  The calls were coming from the northwest direction at an undetermined distance.  I heard the call 6 to 8 times over a few minutes.

The call heard was the distinct, 3-part pee-a-wee of an Eastern Wood-Pewee.  While this bird was occasionally calling, a family group of Western Wood-Pewees was very active in my immediate area making their expected, but distinctly different, array of sighs and "breerrts".

Similar Species Discussion

Pee-a-wee call distinctive for Eastern Wood-Pewee and the call was not part of a sequence that a mimic, like a Brown Thrasher, might make.
