Description of the Bird

First heard and then seen atop some snags just north of overgrown road. It gave about three typical "pee-ah-wee" songs and a couple of calls before flying off. A pewee with very light underparts and largely orange lower mandible. This is a continuing bird that has been well photographed and observed by many.

Similar Species Discussion

Western Wood Pewee eliminated mostly by voice and secondarily by lighter coloration of this bird and brighter and more extensive orange lower mandible.
