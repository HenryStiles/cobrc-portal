Description of the Bird

A wood-pewee with a mostly pale lower mandible singing a typical EAWP song repeatedly.
Behaviors: Flycatching from high perches. Occasionally chasing other pewees. Singing.
Call: peee-a-weee peeeeer
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Western Wood-Pewee looks nearly identical. Bird was singing typical EAWP song. Coupled with pale lower mandible I was able to eliminate WEWP.

Resources Used

Guide to North American Birds V3, audio portion.

Previous Experience

I have seen and heard EAWP many times in other states. WEWP is abundant on front range and is encountered regularly.
