Description of the Bird

A wood-pewee with all orange lower mandible, clear white front (no hint of vest), and white unmarked under tail coverts. See photos. Bird was heard singing by Bill Maynard the day before in the exact location.
Behaviors: Perched in trees
Call: none, heard the day before singing
