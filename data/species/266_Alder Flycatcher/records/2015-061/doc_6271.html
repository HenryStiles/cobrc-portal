Description of the Bird

 
Two birds, one also seen on 5/24/15 by David Dowell.  Greenish flycatcher with thin eyering, creamy colored wingbars and edging on secondaries, broad and all-yellow mandible, "pip" call heard multiple times from each bird; "rreeBEEa" song heard twice.

Similar Species Discussion

Other empids eliminated by call and song, color and eye-ring.

Resources Used
