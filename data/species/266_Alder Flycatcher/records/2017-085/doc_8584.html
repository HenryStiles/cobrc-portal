Description of the Bird

Largish Empidonax with green upperparts including crown. Wings quite contrasty. Tertials black centered with white edges. Wing coverts black centered with bright, white or near-white edging (wingbars). Secondaries edged with yellow-green. Throat white, contrasting sharply with gray-green auriculars. Weak round eyering. Rounded head throughout observation. Moderately long primaries. Bill smallish c/w eastern WIFL. Tail moderate length, relatively broad, square-ended. 

Similar Species Discussion

w WIFL should be duller and shorter winged.
Eastern WIFL typically would have different head shape (more crested), larger billed, darker and duller above
