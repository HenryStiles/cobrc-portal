Description of the Bird

This notoriously hard ID was made in hand after running through the Pyle Alder/Willow differentiation formula. This bird fell well within the range of ALFL. unfortunately those measurements are in CO right now and I am in TX. Thay can be accessed at a later date. Visually this bird was strikingly greenish-colored on the back. Much more green tones than the typical olive-gray of willow flycatchers. The crown had some dark centers to the feathers another ALFL character.

Similar Species Discussion

WIFL/ALFL can be diffrentiated by a formula in Pyle. We did the calculations and this bird was decidedly ALFL. Those measurements are in CO and I am in TX. The greenish coloration as opposed to olive was also distinctive.
