Description of the Bird

An empidonax flycatcher based on size, structure, color and tail-bobbing. It had an olive back and head, whitish throat, pale yellow belly, yellowish wingbars and a thin complete eyering. The bird had moderate primary projection which appeared too long for WIFL. This was confirmed when I compared photos of a WIFL taken the same day. The bird gave the classic feBEEo call of ALFL twice which confirmed what I suspected.
Behaviors: flycatching over a small pond
Call: rrrrrBEEo repeated twice
Plumage: 1st winter
