Description of the Bird

Empid with very narrow white eyering (narrower than LEFL, HAFL, DUFL etc). Lores diffusely gray. Back bright green (which was what originally attracted us to bird). Long primary extension. Wings blackish with very contrastingly white edged secondaries and tertials. Wingbars white. Underparts dull yellowish on belly/vent, dull grayish sides to chest. Throat white. Green of back extends thru nape onto crown, which were a bit darker than back; cheeks olive, with border between auriculars and white throat being marked. Bill orange/pink-orange below and black above. Moderate size. Tail longer/broader than that of Least. Crown rather flat. Call sharp peek.

Similar Species Discussion

Eastern Willow Flyatcher: Not as green on back nor crown. Eyering less distinct. Larger bill (though hard to be certain of such in field) Call rather different.
Least Flycatcher: Smaller bill, shorter primaries, different call. 
Acadian Flycatcher: Even longer primaries, larger bill.
