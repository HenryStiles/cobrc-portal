Description of the Bird

A large greenish backed empid with a typical traill's type shape. Things that point toward ALFL were the very green tones of the back and the eye-ring. The most important ID feature was the voice.
Behaviors: Sitting in Russian Olives 2-5 feet up making ocassional sallies for insects.
Call: pik or peek
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Willow Fly is the only similar species. The only way to conclusively eliminate this sp is through vocal ID. The greenish back of this bird also points to ALFL as does the small eyering
