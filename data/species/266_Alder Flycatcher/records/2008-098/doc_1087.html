Description of the Bird

A large Traill's type Empid. I noticed that it was noticeably greener overall than the other Willw Flycatchers I had been catching over the previous week. The bird was banded and the following measurements obtained. The combination of the measurements and the greener back eliminate all Willow Flycatcher subspecies. All measurements in mm. Primary Extension=16.7, Wing-Tail=18, Longest P-P6=7, P10-P5=1.5, P6-P10=2.4, P9-P5=13.1, bill=8.2. I followed the Pyle WIFL-ALFL seperation formula and the result 3.5 was well out of the range of WIFL and well in the range of ALFL.
