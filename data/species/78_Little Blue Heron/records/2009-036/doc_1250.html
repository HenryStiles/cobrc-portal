Description of the Bird

Medium sized heron, obviously molting with white and blue feathers over all feathered areas giving a kind of "calico" appearance. Long bi-colored bill with thick gray base and dark tip. Gray lores. I did not see the legs and feet well due to distance and heat waves.
Behaviors: Slowly foraging in a small marshy pond.
Call: not heard
Plumage: molting immature
