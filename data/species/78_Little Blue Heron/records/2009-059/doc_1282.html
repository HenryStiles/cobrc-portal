Description of the Bird

The size of the heron was similar to Snowy Egret. It had a pale bill, yellow legs, and mottled with blue/gray patches.
Behaviors: The bird was only observed in flight trailing Snowy Egrets.
Call: none
Plumage: 1st Spring

Similar Species Discussion

Snowy Egret was eliminated because of bill color, leg color and mottled plumage of the LBEH.
