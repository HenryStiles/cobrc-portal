Description of the Bird

A medium-sized white heron/egret like bird. Which the feathers were off white in color, with two toned bill. Black on the tip of the bill and grayish blue on the base of the bill. The bird had yellow above the eyes. The bird had dark tips to wings. Though, it was a whitish bird. The legs were greenish yellow.
Behaviors: The bird was wading in the water.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Snowy Egret - don't have two tone bill, don't have green-yellow legs.
