Description of the Bird

A large Myiarchus flycatcher. The first thing that struck me about this bird was the bright yellow belly and the dark bluish-gray upper breast. In flight the bird showed significant rufous in the wing and tail. I never saw the head well but the bright yeloow of the belly and gray of the breast should eliminate all other possible Myiarchus. The bird showed reddish in wings and tail which would eliminate All yellow-bellied kingbirds
