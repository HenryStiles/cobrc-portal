Description of the Bird

Large bulky accipiter, immature Northern Goshawk plummage, strong white supercilium, wingtips do not cover long tail , wide wavy  banded brown and black tail with thin white outline, pale yellow and brown streaked nape, bright white spots on brown back.  Somewhat pointy wings. Flew from juniper hedgerow to Cottonwood tree.  Close to second much smaller Coopers hawk for size comparison. 

Similar Species Discussion

Immature Red Tailed Hawk, head  shape and color not correct, tail too long, bands too wavy and spaced out.
