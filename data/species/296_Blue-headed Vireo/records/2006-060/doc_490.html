Description of the Bird

It had bright yellowish green sides and flanks. It had a greenish yellow back. It had two white wing bars. It had a dark gray head with a white lores and eye ring. It was a strong contrast between the head and the back of the bird. It also had a strong contrast between the gray head a bright white throat. It had a grayish green tail with white edges to the outer most tail feathers. The secondaries are the same color as the back with a few whiter feathers.
Behaviors: When we released the bird it flew up into a large Cottonwood and started working its way up the tree.
Call: Didn't hear it.
Plumage: Unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo - Not as much of a gray contrast to a gray head and grayish green back. It also has a weak contrast between a light gray head and a white throat. It shouldn't have as much, if any, white in the outer part of the tail. Not as much and not as bright of yellowish green sides. Greenish edges to the secondaries.
Plumbeous Vireo No yellow on the sides of the bird. The back and head is all gray. Short gray tail. Gray edges on secondaries.
