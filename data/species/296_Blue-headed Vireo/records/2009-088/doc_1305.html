Description of the Bird

Vireo with dark head, bright white throat, spectacles around each eye. Greenish color back and flanks. Contrast between the dark head and greenish black. See photos.
Behaviors: moving around the trees with other birds.
Call: none
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo -- no contrast between head and back, usually doesn't have bright white throat.
