head blue with bright white spectacles; chin and throat bright white, contrasting strongly with blue auriculars

nape and back greenish, contrasting with blue head

wings edged bright green; R6 with distinct white edging, R1-R5 green-edged

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo has a grayish head and weak contrast between throat and auriculars. It has paler yellow-olive wash on flanks and R6 is not edged white.

Plumbeous Vireo is a larger bird with longer wing chord (77-85 mm) and larger bill (exposed culmen 11.2-12.2 mm). It has a gray head and back and gray-edged remiges.
