Description of the Bird

Strikingly bright solitary vireo with lemon yellow sides lightly washed with some olive, deep blue-gray head, emerald green back. Big bold wingbars washed lightly in yellow (not visible in photos for some reason). Sharp contrast between nape and back, and between auriculars and throat.
Behaviors: Feeding slowly in junipers.
Call: not heard
Plumage: not determined

Similar Species Discussion

Colors brighter than Cassin's Vireo and contrast between auriculars/snowy white throat and between nape and back too sharp for Cassin's Vireo
