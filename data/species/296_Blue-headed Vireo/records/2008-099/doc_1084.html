Description of the Bird

A solitary vireo with a steel blue head, green back and yellow flanks. White underneath with sharp delineation between with throat and blue sides of head/neck.
Behaviors: foraging in the middle of Cottonwoods
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo - this species has less abrubt delineation between throat and head/neck, and has a more uniform colored head and back.
