Description of the Bird

Pishing when a bright Solitary Vireo came in close, above eye level, but not high up, approaching as close as 15-20 feet, never more than 30 feet during couple minutes of direct observation time. Blue gray head with bright white spectacles contrasting sharply with bright deep green back and snowy white throat. Most of underparts white, except sides washed in yellow with some olive. Some yellow on vent. Big bright wingbars, white tinged yellow. Bright yellow-green edgings to secondaries. 

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo should not be so bright on back and head and should lack this degree of contrast between head and back, auriculars and throat; Cassin's also more olive on sides.
