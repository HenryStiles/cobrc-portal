Description of the Bird

A vireo with blue head, green back, white throat, greenish/yellow flanks, white belly and under tail coverts. Two white wing bars. Thick bill. See photos.
Behaviors: With a flock of other birds, including Cassin's, Plumbeous, and Warbling Vireos, Townsend's Warbler, Black-capped Chickadees, and other birds.
Call: none
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo: This species doesn't have the bright white throat, that contrasts with the blue head and cheeks.

Previous Experience

Seen many in the east, and in Colorado in recent years. This species seems not to be that rare in Colorado.
