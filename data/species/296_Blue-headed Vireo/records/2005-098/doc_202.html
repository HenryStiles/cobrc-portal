Description of the Bird

Obvious Solitary Vireo by size, shape, bill shape and white spectacles. Green back, yellow flanks, bright white belly, center of breast, throat and wing-bars, blue-gray head, face and sides of neck. Head and neck color contrasted sharply with with throat, with sharp delineation.
Behaviors: flittin around, acting like a vireo, responded to PLVI tape by coming in closer
Call: none
Plumage: adult looking
