Description of the Bird

A vireo with blueish head, white spectacles, green back and wings, greenish yellow flanks, bright white throat, white belly. Thick bill. See photos. I don't think this is the same bird as the one from October 5, as it doesn't appear to be as bright as that bird.
Behaviors: With a flock of Black-capped Chickadees, Yellow-rumped Warblers, a Plumbeous Vireo.
Call: none
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Vireo - This bird has greenish head and back, without the bright white throat, contrasting less than this bird.
