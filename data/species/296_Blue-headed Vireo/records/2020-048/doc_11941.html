Description of the Bird

 this bird had noticeablly bright plumage overall. White "spectacle" from base of dark vireo-type bill around dark eye; bright blue gray head with clear demarcation between the cheek and clean white chin and throat; coloration of head transitioned at the nape into greenish upper back; wing had two wing bars - white with yellow wash to the bars. bright yellow wash down the flank below folded wing on otherwise white underside.  Bird was actively feeding on the insects hatching from the creek (midges and beatis, I think) that were on the willow leaves. 

Similar Species Discussion

 this individual was obviously brighter than plumbeous vireo. Separated from Cassin's by bright green upper back and clear demarcation between bright blue-gray side of head and clean white chin/throat. What caught my attention was how this individuals's color really popped with brightness.
