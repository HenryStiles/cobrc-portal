Description of the Bird

1 - Bright white spectacles
2 - Blue head contrasted with white throat
3 - and with green back
4 - 2 white wing bars
5 - yellowish flanks
6 - white belly
7 - overall size about 5.5 inches
8 - stout bill
Behaviors: -stayed in fairly high branches overhead while we were near
-feeding on something amongst the leaves - probably insects
Call: did not call
Plumage: bright adult

Similar Species Discussion

We eliminated both Plumbeous and Cassin's Vireo because of the strong contrast between the blue auriculars and white throat, and the contrast between the blue head and the green back. The bird was very bright overall and had very clean sharp lines between contrasting areas.
