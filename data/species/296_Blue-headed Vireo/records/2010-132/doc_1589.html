Description of the Bird

I was looking a bunch of Yellow-rumped Warblers and then spotted this bird. A vireo with blueish head, bright white throat, white spectacles, yellow flanks, white belly. See photos.
Behaviors: With a bunch of Yellow-rumped Warblers.
Call: none
Plumage: unknown

Previous Experience

Seen in the Eastern US in the Summer, also seen in Colorado in spring and fall. Have seen several this fall in Colorado, maybe not so rare.
