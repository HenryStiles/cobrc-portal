Description of the Bird

A waterthrush, with bright white eyeline, flaring in the back. Brownish on the upper parts, head, back, and tail. Bright white throat, breast, belly, with cinnamon colored flanks, with some streaking on the sides. Bright pink legs, large thin warbler like bill. The tail pumping was in a U shape, not up and down like Northern Waterthrush. See my lousy photos.
Behaviors: Feeding along the moving stream, catching insects.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Northern Waterthrush -- tail bobbing in more up and down, bright white eyeline, flaring at the end, brighter white throat, and underparts, pinkish legs, and cinnamon flanks.
