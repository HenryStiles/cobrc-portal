Description of the Bird

Warbler size yet primarily stayed along the stream going in and out of the folage and dissappearing for several minutes. Dark brown solid uper and brown streaked below on a white belly. Bright white supercilium that got broader and bolder on the rear. Bright pink legs and no spots on the white throat.
Behaviors: Very secreative along the stream banks. Going in and out of the folage along the back. Obviously, a warbler that liked to be next to the water.
Call: no
Plumage: basic

Similar Species Discussion

Northern Waterthrush is very close, but the broad white supercilium at the rear, lack of spotting on the throat, bright pink legs, all added up to a Louisiana.
