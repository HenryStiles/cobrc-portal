Description of the Bird

See photos. Greenish back, wings, tail. No wing bars. Gray hood with blackish lores (better defined, darker than bird earlier in day) and no markings around eye. Lower end of bib solid black. Some olive extending on to nape (first year male??). Underparts bright yellow

I don't know if Glenn is submitting report. See a photo of his at http://ebird.org/ebird/view/checklist/S37056306

Similar Species Discussion

MacG with white crescents around eye, more prominent black lores, typically more extensive green on sides.
