Description of the Bird

Secretive warbler moving about near stream, mostly hidden by vegetation but occasionally out in the open.  Dark blueish-gray hood with darker patch on breast.  Olive upperparts without wingbars.  Yellow underparts.  No calls heard.

Similar Species Discussion

Knowing that hybrids with the similar MacGillivray's Warbler exist, I looked closely for evidence of white eye arcs.  I did not see any.

Resources Used

Previous Experience

I have seen about half a dozen Mourning Warblers.  I frequently observe MacGillivray's Warblers in Colorado during spring, summer, and fall.
