Description of the Bird

Kara spotted a warbler, that she quickly identified as a Mourning Warbler. I looked at the bird and agreed. The bird had a gray head with no hint of any white eye arches or eye ring. It also had a black band on the lower breast, above the all yellow underparts. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying very low and in the shade.
Call: It made a chip, it sounded a bit different then MacGillivray's Warbler, though I'm not sure how to describe it.
Plumage: adult alternate plumage
