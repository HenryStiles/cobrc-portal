Description of the Bird

I was birding at Brett Gray Ranch with other birders, when I spotted this male Mourning Warbler. The lack of eye archs or eyering, on the gray head, made this a Mourning Warbler. See photos.
Behavior: Staying low most of the time, then quite high up in the trees.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
