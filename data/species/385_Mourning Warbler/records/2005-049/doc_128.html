Description of the Bird

A warbler with a greenish back and yellow lower breast, belly and vent. The head, throat and upper breast was gray with a darker gray lower edge to this hood.
Behaviors: foraging in willows and Russian olives usually staying low but occasionally up to 10 feet high.
Call: none noted
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Connecticut Warbler has a prominent white eye ring. MacGillivray's Warbler has bright white eye arcs above and below the eye.
