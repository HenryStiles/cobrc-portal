Description of the Bird

A large warbler olive-green above. Bright yellow below from the lower breast to the undertail coverts. The head and upper breast was hooded in dark gray. Blaskish around the eye with some blackish mottling on the upper breast
Behaviors: foraging in low willows around a small pond
