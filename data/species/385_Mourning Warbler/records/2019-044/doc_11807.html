Description of the Bird

I was birding alone, when I first spotted this bird.  I saw the gray head, black on the lower head, greenish back, bright yellow under parts.  I looked carefully at both sides of the eyes, and there were no eye archs or eye ring, which elimnates the other possible warblers.

Similar Species Discussion

MacGillivray's Warbler - has the white eye archs, which this bird didn't have.

Resources Used

None

Previous Experience

Mourning Warbler is a somewhat regular migrant through Colorado in May, it seems, I've seen many times over the years in Colorado.  I've seen them in North Dakota and other eastern states as well.
