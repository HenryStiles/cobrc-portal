Description of the Bird

I was walking around the house at Van Truan's yard, at 1901 Court Street in Pueblo.  A warbler shaped bird and warbler sized bird flushed up from the Golden Currents into a small tree.  The bird was in clear view, dark gray head , black eyes with black in front of eye, no hint of any eye arches or eye ring, olive green back, bright yellow underparts, below the throat through the entire belly and undertail coverts.  The legs were pink.  The bill was thin and pale.  The bird made loud chip, and flew off to neighors yard.  I didn't see it again.  I will attach Van's photos taken on the same day, 27 May 2014.  Van heard the bird singing on both May 26 and May 27.
