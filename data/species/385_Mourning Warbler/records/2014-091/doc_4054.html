Description of the Bird

Warbler, superficially like a MAWA.

Gray head, neck, nape.  Lemon yellow belly.  Solid olive-green back and tail.  Chest sporting a black band separating the gray of the neck/throat/upper-chest with the yellow of the lower-chest/belly.  Black lores/bandits mask. No eyering/eye-arcs.

Similar Species Discussion

MAWA - lack of eye arcs.  The black lores/bandit mask and the strong black chest-band are not consistent with MAWA.

CONW - structurally wrong.  Too dark in tones.  Lack of round eyering.
