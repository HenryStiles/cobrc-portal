Description of the Bird

This large dove was pale gray with warmer tones on the back and wings. The head was gray and the nape was marked with a distinct black crescent that extended to a point at the sides of the neck. The primaries were dark gray. The tail was moderately long and square. The undertail showed extensive white for more than half its length and the uppertail showed large white outer patches when spread for landing. Undertail coverts were gray. The bill was dark and the legs were reddish-pink.
Behaviors: Perched on utility lines and flying to ground
Call: Not heard
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Light color, black neck crescent, gray undertail coverts, and tail all clearly identify this species.
