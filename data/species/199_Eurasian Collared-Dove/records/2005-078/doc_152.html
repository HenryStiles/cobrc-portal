Description of the Bird

A large light colored dove was sitting on a sign at the west end of the town of Gunnison. It was noticably larger than a White-winged or Mourning Dove. It had a black band on the back of the neck. It had a long square tail with very little black. The black was towards the undertail coverts while the rest of the underpart of the tail was white. The Dove had dark primaries.
Behaviors: The bird was sitting on top of a sign. I never saw it fly.
Call: Didn't hear it
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Ringed Turtle Dove - Have light brown or gray primaries and ECDO has dark brown or black primaries. The undertail coverts are light with light and the edges are white. They are also slightly smaller than a Eurasian Collared-Dove.
That I am aware of, there are no other doves that have a dark band on the back of the neck.
