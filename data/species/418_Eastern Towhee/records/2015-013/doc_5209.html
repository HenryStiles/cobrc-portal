Description of the Bird

Urling first noticed the bird perched in willows. She first noticed lack of spotting on back. Then she saw brown head, brownish-black body without spotting in wings except for a white patch at the fore edge of the wings. Rusty sides, white breast and belly. And it called with a different note than Spotted Towhees.

Similar Species Discussion

Spotted Towhee: This bird lacked white spots, and had definitive brown head and back. Has different call.
