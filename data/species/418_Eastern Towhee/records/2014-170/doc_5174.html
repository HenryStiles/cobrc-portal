Description of the Bird

Typical Towhee behavior, foraging through leaves.  He was there when I arrived, and after a slow walk around Lake Mary that took nearly an hour, he was in the exact same spot when I returned. 

Similar Species Discussion

Spotted Towhee was considered, but this bird clearly had no white spots on scapulars and had the white patch at base of primaries.  Bird was photographed.
