Description of the Bird

solid black back, rufous sides, white belly, red eye, white outer tail feathers showing when bird quickly fans tail while sitting.
Behaviors: Eating seeds on ground that have fallen from feeder above on front porch of my house. Also seen in backyard under another seed feeder.
Call: no call or song recorded
Plumage: winter

Similar Species Discussion

Spotted Towhee. I just moved here from Berkeley and we have Spotted and California Towhee there. I knew this was not a Spotted Towhee because it was lacking the spots on the back.
