Description of the Bird

SPTO-sized, shaped and colored bird. 
Dark (almost jet black) tail,  back, head, face, throat and onto upper chest.
Rufous sides.
White  extending from chest to belly.
Red eye.
Black bill.
White on tertial  edges and at the base of the primaries in a squarish shape.
Call notes raspy  turwheets and similar.  Some reminiscent of SPTO call.

Similar Species Discussion

SPTO - Eliminated by complete lack of white spotting on the back, coverts or scapulars.
