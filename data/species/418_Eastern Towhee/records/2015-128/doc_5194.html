Description of the Bird

See attached photo.  Head and upperparts plain brown, flanks rufous, belly white.  Small white patch at base of primaries.  Long tail – outer rectrices with white spots.  Dark conical bill.

Similar Species Discussion

The similar species Spotted Towhee has white spots on the scapulars and wing coverts.

Previous Experience

I have seen many Eastern Towhees in the decades when I lived and worked in the East and have seen one previously at Chico Basin Ranch in 2006.  I have seen and banded many Spotted Towhees in Colorado and Nebraska.
