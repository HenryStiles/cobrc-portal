Description of the Bird

A chocolate brown towhee sized bird, brown head and breast, white belly and chestnut sides and flanks. A small white spot at the base of he primaries. No white spots on scapulars or coverts.
Behaviors: perched in a shrub, seemingly alarmed... then flew off
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Spotted Towhee is similar but is covered in white spots on coverts and scapulars and usually has a red iris.
