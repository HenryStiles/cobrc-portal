Description of the Bird

Mallard-type duck with very dark body, contrasting with paler tan head and neck. Blackish cap and quite dark line through eye. Underparts rather uniform with only a hint of wamer brown on chest. Dark through vent. Tail dusky brown without white. When exposed, speculum blue with narrow posterior border. Normal anterior border.  No curled tail feathers. Bill seemed to be yellow- orange-olive. Size of male Mallard. Some internal markings on uppertail coverts and scaps noted.

Similar Species Discussion

Mallard x Mexican hybrids should show curled tail feather, some white on tail, paler body coloration, green on head... at least one or two of these traits.
Mottled Duck without white borders to speculum, more contrasting head
Am Black Duck darker. American Black Duck x Mallard would likely show some green on head, curled tail feather, some white on tail ....
