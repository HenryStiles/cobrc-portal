Description of the Bird

Male. Very dark body for Mexi = darkest I've seen. Breast same color as sides (no chestnut hues). Undertail coverts same color as remainder of underparts. Internal paler markings on scaps eliminates Am Black Duck. Contrastingly paler (tan) neck and head. Some streaking on cheek and no "grin" near bill eliminates Mottled. Crown and eyeline blackish. Bill bright yellow with some olive tinge. Tail all brown. Legs orange-red

Similar Species Discussion

See description. Too dark for hybrdi Mexi x Mallard. Additionally, solid brown tail, no curled upper tail feathers, no browner chest, no paler undertail coverts to indicate any Mallard.
