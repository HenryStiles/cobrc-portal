Description of the Bird

Female Mallard-type duck with substantially darker body that contrasted with neck and head; Head and neck markings darker than that typical of Mallard. Bill orange with olive saddle. In flight, tan colored tail, narrow edges to speculum which looked teal/blue colored. Underparts solid dark beneath in flight, with no paling towards undertail coverts
Behaviors: swimming about with Mallards
Call: not heard
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Female Mallard, or intergraded Mallard x Mexican Duck, should not be so dark, should show more Mallard like tail and speculum and should be paler on vent
