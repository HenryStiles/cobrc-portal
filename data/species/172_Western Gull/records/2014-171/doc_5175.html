Description of the Bird

 Mark Peterson and myself were looking at a 1 st cycle Thayers Gull in the middle of the reservoir through spotting scopes when a larger darker mantled gull came and landed near it . A cold front had just passed through , temperature dropping from the 60's to high 30's in 10 minutes . this larger gull had a darker mantle than an adjacent CAGU but a tad lighter than a LBBG ( we looked at Sibley after the bird flew away to compare mantle color ) . Sitting in the water , I observed all white head with no streaking on head or neck , light eye , all yellow bill with a red gonydeal spot . In flight showed dark black primaries with just one obvious mirror on P10 , broad white trailing edge on secondaries and bubble gum pink legs . Bird flew off in a north easterly direction and although we were joined by Bill Maynard bird never returned before we left at 4:30 pm.

Similar Species Discussion

 Adult Slaty backed would have darker mantle , streaking on head and neck ,dark streak through eye, mirrors on P8 -10 . WEGUXGWGU hybrid would have paler mantle , streaking or smudging on neck and head , dark eye .
