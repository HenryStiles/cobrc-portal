Description of the Bird

Kara Lewantowicz and I went on the shore on the north side of Lake Cheraw, when she spotted a pale backed plover. I quickly looked at it, and figured out it was a Snowy Plover. The date was early, which it turns out might well be the earliest one to be seen in Colorado. The bird had dark legs, which would elimnate Piping Plover.
Behaviors: The bird was walking on the shore at Lake Cheraw.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
