Description of the Bird

I was with Larry Semo, Tony Leukering, Brian Gibbons, and Mymm Ackley on the evening of May 20th, when a Whip-poor-will started to sing, just after sunset along the road near the banding station at Chico Basin Ranch. The bird gave a typical 'whip-poor-will' 'whip-poor-will' song many times. Early the morning of May 21st, I also heard the bird sing a couple of times with Kara Lewantowicz and Bill Maynard.
Behaviors: Singing from Russian olive grove, near the banding station at Chico Basin Ranch, after sunset and before sunrise.
Call: 'whip-poor-will' 'whip-poor-will' song heard many times
Plumage: singing
