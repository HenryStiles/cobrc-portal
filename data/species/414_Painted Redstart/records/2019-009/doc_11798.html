Description of the Bird

The bird was first seen on a branch in a sidewise position so could see bird was all black with a white wing patch.  Bill was black.  The bird then flew and we could view the black back and tail in which center middle taile feathers were black with white side feathers.  He then flew to a tree where he faced us on a limb and we viewd the black head and throat and dark red breast.  Size was about 6 inches.
