Description of the Bird

black with white wing bars and outside tail feathers, and with a prominent scarlet red belly extending part way up the breast ... warbler bill ... similar in size to a goldfinch
Behaviors: perched on metal railing under my feeder for about 30 seconds and watched the goldfinches and chickadees feed before flying off up the valley to the north
Call: did not hear the bird
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

There is no other small black bird with white wing bars and a bright red belly/breast in my bird books.
