Description of the Bird

Smaller warbler sized bird, largely black on back with bright red chest and belly. White partial eye-ring lower portion of eye. Single large white wing bar. Quick moving frequent change of position as it hunted several neighboring trees.

Similar Species Discussion

none

Resources Used

Originally reported by Cathy Sheeter earlier in the day. Verified by Stokes and National Geographic sixth edition, other birders on site at the time of my visit.

Previous Experience

I saw another one during a visit the previous Christmas (2013) at the Aransas National Wildlife Refuge in Texas. It was also considered a rarity for that part of Texas.
