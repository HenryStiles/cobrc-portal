Description of the Bird

The bird was calling repeatedly while bouncing around in the trees to the west of farm equipment display. 

Similar Species Discussion

Not many species have that combination of a black/charcoal gray head/body, bright red breast and white patch on the wing.  I suppose you could consider red-winged blackbird, but the size was too small and the red was on the breast, not the wing.  I snapped several photos to confirm later.
