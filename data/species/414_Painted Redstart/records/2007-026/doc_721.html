Description of the Bird

Wabler sized bird with black head and upperparts. White crescent below dark eye. Small, pointed, slender black bill. Large white wing patch, white outer tail feathers. Bright red lower breast and belly. Black and white mottled underparts.
Behaviors: Flying in small loop from tree to tree, feeding and calling repeatedly.
Call: "chee"
Plumage: adult
