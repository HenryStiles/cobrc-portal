Description of the Bird

A small warbler shaped bird, with black upper parts, white wing bar, red belly, long tail, with white. Small thin bill. Dark eyes. See photos.
Behaviors: Moving around the trees catching insects.
Call: a loud call given often.
Plumage: perhaps 2nd year
