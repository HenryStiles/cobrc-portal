Description of the Bird

Gave distinct chips. Was foraging for insects on side of canyon. Came very close to me, red chest, white under eyes, white barring on back. 

Similar Species Discussion

American redstart was considered. The bird had no orange on it however and had the distinct red and white. 

Resources Used

I used the ebird field guide and the Merlin ID application. 

Previous Experience

I have been with American Redstarts a lot but a painted redstart is a new one for me. He came very close and I got many looks at him.
