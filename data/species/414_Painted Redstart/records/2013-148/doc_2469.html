Description of the Bird

Warbler-sized bird with longish black and white fanned tail (white outer tail feathers), black head and upperparts with broad white panel in wings, black chest, crimson red belly; small thin black bill.
Behavior: Flitting about branches high in a Ponderosa Pine with wings hanging down from body and tail fanned.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
