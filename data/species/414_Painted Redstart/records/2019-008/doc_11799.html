Description of the Bird

A small bird with black head, back, wings, sides, and center of tail; white teardrop below eye, red down middle of underparts from chest to just above legs, white lower belly and under-tail covs, white median and greater secondary coverts, and white outer rectrices.

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing else very similar; Slate-throated Redstart ruled out by white in wings, white teardrop, and more-extensive white in tail
