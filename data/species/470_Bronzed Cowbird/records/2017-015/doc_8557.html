Description of the Bird

He was very interested in my feeders with both sunflower seed in one and mixed millet, corn, sunflower seed mixture.  I didn’t see him retrieve anything from the feeders.  He looked around at other birds, jumped on a couple of perches we provide.  Hung out for a few minutes and then took off.

Similar Species Discussion

I first considered Common Grackle… maybe a juvenile,  I also looked up juvenile European Starlings, but beak is wrong.  I also looked up Brown Headed Cowbird, but they were all eliminated due to the red eyes of this bird.  I posted this bird on What’s This Bird, Facebook, and they made the ID.
