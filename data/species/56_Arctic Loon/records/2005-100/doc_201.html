Description of the Bird

An obvious Arctic/Pacific type loon by plumage, size and shape. Noticeable white patches on flanks extending above waterline no matter how low the bird was riding. Head shape was slightly blocked with "knot" above eye giving the head a shape more in line with COLO than PALO. Bill was held at an angle off horizontal. No vent strap was seen in the preening bird.
Behaviors: Swam and dove continually. Occasionally preened allowing view of ventral surface.
Call: none
Plumage: breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Pacific Loon eliminated due to head shape, bill size, and obvious white patches on flanks
