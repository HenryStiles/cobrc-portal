Description of the Bird

Warbler foraging very actively, mostly within several feet of the ground.  Dark blueish-gray upperparts without wing bars.  Yellow underparts.  Spectacles consisting of yellow supraloral and bicolored eye ring (yellow upper front, white lower rear).  Blackish necklace of dense streaks.  No calls heard.

Similar Species Discussion

The necklace and distinctive face pattern make Canada Warbler instantly recognizable.  The bicolored eye ring is diagnostic, eliminating other warbler species.
