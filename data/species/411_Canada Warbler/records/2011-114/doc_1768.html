Description of the Bird

On 5 September, Joey Kellner and I pulled up to the Last Chance rest stop a couple minutes after the more speedy Glenn Walbek ;o)

Glenn excitedly said, "Canada Warbler, right here." Though Joey and I failed to achieve binoculation at that moment, we called the bird back, and it provided lovely views. The blue-gray upperparts, bright eyering, yellow underparts (except undertail coverts and perhaps part of vent), and pale gray "necklace" gave the ID away
Behaviors: Bird came in at mid height (mostly) in agitated fashion to taped Eastern Screech Owl and BC Chickadee calls/songs.
Call: not heard
Plumage: likely immature
