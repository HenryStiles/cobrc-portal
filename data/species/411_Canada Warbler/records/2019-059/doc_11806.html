Description of the Bird

I was birding alone, when I saw a warbler that was all yellow undernearh, with black streaks on the neck area.  The back was gray, and the bird had an eyering.  See excellent photos taken by other birders this morning.

Similar Species Discussion

The gray back without wing bars, elimnates Magnolia Warbler.

Previous Experience

I've seen Canada Warblers in the spring in Colorado, two other times, also in the fall two times.  This is only my 5th one for Colorado.  I've seen Canada Warblers in Kansas in fall migration, and in the Eastern States.
