Description of the Bird

Bright plumage of adult male Bullock's Oriole. Photo available on Flickr in ebird Rarities Group. or on Facebook for Cree Allison Bol

Bird continued to eat crab apples throughout sighting. Other birds in same tree included House Finches and American Robins.
Behaviors: Eating crabapples in a tree about 20 ft from ground. Tree was located 15 ft from a residential street. Neither pedestrian or car traffic seemed to impact bird. Stayed on same two limbs. Ate apples for 90 minutes.
Call: No sounds or calls
Plumage: bright yellow plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Black cap on head suggested Bullock's not Hooded Oriole. Confirmed sighting in Sibley's field guide
