Description of the Bird


A very distintive goose, black front, grayish back.  See photo.  The bird showed up at the same lake, as the Pink-footed Goose.  There were thousands of Cackling Geese, as well as Canada, Snow, Ross's, and Greater White-fronted Geese present as well.  I have no idea, if this bird is wild or not.  It was fun to see it, I missed it three times the week before.  Last year, in early January one was photographed at Aurora Reservoir, and a few winters ago, there was one at Maxwell NWR in northern NM.  Are these birds coming from Greenland or private collections, I have no idea.
