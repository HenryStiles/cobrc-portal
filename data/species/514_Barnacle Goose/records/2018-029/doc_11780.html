Description of the Bird

Small goose, smaller than nearby Richy Cackling Geese swimming a 100 meters or farther out on Aurora Reservoir. Black chest, neck and head except white face (but face with black loral stripe that ended at eye -- i.e, did not reach crown). Sides whitish-gray, with bright white vent and largely black tail. Back barred gray, black and white. Wing coverts similarly patterned. Tertials largely gray. Folded primaries, as could be determined, black. Bill short and petite, black in color. Watched bird swim among Cacklers for 5-10 minutes before disappeared.

Similar Species Discussion

There really are no similar species. Unclear on age. Facial pattern more consistent with adult, back pattern perhaps more like first cycle
