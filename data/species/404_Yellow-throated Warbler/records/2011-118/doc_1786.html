Description of the Bird

Warbler which appeared to be a little larger and more elongated than adjacent Townsend's Warbler .Had a long white supercilium , blackish auriculars , white neck spot , gray back and wings with two white wing bars and a yellow throat extending down to the upper breast . remaining underparts white with black flank streaking. Did not see any yellow on lores so this would make it the albilora subspecies.
Behaviors: Initially feeding in Russian Olive , then flew to cottonwood where proceeded to glean for food on trunk of tree like a nuthatch.
Call: None heard
Plumage: fall adult ?

Similar Species Discussion

Nuthatch behaviour and yellow throat and black and white facial pattern unique. Grace's has a yellow supercilium and no white neck spot , Black and White doesn't have a yellow throat.
