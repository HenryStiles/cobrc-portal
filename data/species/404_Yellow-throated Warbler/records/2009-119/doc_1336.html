Description of the Bird

Mid-sized warbler. Gray back, black crown, black face with large white eyebrow stripe and large white patch on side of neck. Small halfmoon patch of white under each eye. Bright yellow throat and upper breast. White underparts, black streaking on sides. White wing-bars.
Behaviors: Actively searching for food.
Call: did not hear
Plumage: adult
