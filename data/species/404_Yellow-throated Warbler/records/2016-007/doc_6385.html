Description of the Bird

**Stakeout. We arrived and the bird was at the backyard feeders coming to both seed and suet, and also feeding below the feeder on fallen seed. Appeared to be a first-year bird given pointed RR, and slightly duller overall appearance including less boldly contrasting plumage. 


Photos show bird well.

Similar Species Discussion

No other species really very similer. Grace's perhaps most similar, but lacks boldly contrasting black and white face pattern.
