Description of the Bird

The size of the bird was the same size as the Western Bluebirds in the same flock. I first noticed the orange on the side of the neck. Next I saw the white throat and belly.
Behaviors: The Eastern Bluebird was in a flock of Western Bluebirds. When I first spotted the flock they were working the fence line flying down to the ground and then back up to a perch. The then moved further into the field moving from shrub to yucca(?) with the same behavior of landing on the ground, then flying to a perch.
Call: None
Plumage: Unknown

Similar Species Discussion

I ruled out female Mountain Bluebird because of the orange color on the flanks and Western Bluebird because of the white underparts and orange color on the side of the neck.
