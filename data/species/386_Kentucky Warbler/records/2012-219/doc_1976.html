Description of the Bird

See photos. Warbler with bright yellow underneath, black side burns on the face. Nothing looks like a Kentucky Warbler really.
Behaviors: Low to the ground, near the willows.
Call: I heard it chipping, giving the loud Fox Sparrow like chip.
