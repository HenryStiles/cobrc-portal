Description of the Bird

A warbler with bright yellow underparts. Black side burns. Olive/green back. Yellow semi-circle going behind the eyes. Pink legs. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying low to the ground.
Call: call was a loud "chuck" reminding me of Fox Sparrow. They both sound very similar.
Plumage: adult
