Habitat

Brushy area under taller trees near ranch house. Somewhat damper than general area due to lawn and flowers around ranch house that are watered at times.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Canon 30D camera with 100-400 IS lens
Distance:  estimated to be 30 feet.
Light:  taken in open shade on clear day.

Description of the Bird

Small bird with bright yellow breast, and black outlining its yellow "spectacles". Pale legs. Olive back.
Behaviors: Bird was sitting on branch ~4 feet from ground. Responded calmly to my presence about 30 feet away with tripod, long lens on camera. Fluffed up its feathers, then organized them again. After about 5 minutes, it dropped to ground and began looking for something, pecking at things and running a short distance, perhaps scratching a bit. Then moving again. I was not able to get clear shots on the ground because of the underbrush where the bird was.
Call: Not observed
Plumage: breeding
