Description of the Bird

Kara spotted a tanager, it was very green with dark wings and no wing bars. The bird was bigger than a Painted Bunting which is also green. See photo.
Behaviors: Feeding on the ground, which was odd.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Western Tanager -- has wing bars

Painted Bunting -- much smaller, doesn't have black wings
