Description of the Bird

Male Scarlet Tanager that wintered at a feeder in Boulder Co., CO in 1993-1994. A photo is on Flickr taken by David Ely on 2/18/1994. Many birders saw this bird.
No special behavior. It was eating at feeders.

I am doing this report due to a request from Rachel after Tony saw my report on eBird.
You really should contact David Ely for details.
