Description of the Bird

The same bird I saw on 10/19/2011. I saw greenish yellow tanager, with thin wing bar. The overall color was wrong for Summer Tanager and Hepatic Tanager, and lack of two obvious wing bars eliminated Western Tanager. See photo from today and from 10/19/2011.
Behaviors: Seen in grape tree.
Call: none
Plumage: fall plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Summer and Hepatic Tanager's aren't greenish yellow.

Western Tanager has two wing bars.
