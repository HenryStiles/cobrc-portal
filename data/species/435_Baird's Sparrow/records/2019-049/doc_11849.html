Description of the Bird

These sparrows were typically identified by their musical 'te-da-da-deeeeeeee' song.  They had necklaces and olive/buffy heads that were patterned.  We found one pair with fledged young in Larimer County in mid-June, and an active nest in Weld County in early August.  We color banded 11 individual birds between June and July.
