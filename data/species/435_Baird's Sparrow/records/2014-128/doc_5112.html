Description of the Bird

Ammodramus Sparrow , large bill , buffy face with dark throat stripe and moustachal stripe, eyering ,two dark spots on auriculars which in some individuals is joined by another dark line , scaly mantle black feathers edged in white , some rufous showing in secondaries and median coverts , pale median crown stripe , streaky lateral crown stripes , some dark streaking on upper chest . Seen in association with other sparrows perching on fence wires for brief periods of time before flying down into long grass and be lost to sight . On two occaisons seen along roadside . No calls heard .    

Similar Species Discussion

Juvenile GRSP which has no strong malar or moustachial stripes , doesn't have a buffy face , doesn't show rufous in secondaries or median coverts . From Henslow's which lacks buffy nape with fine streaks , has an olive green face , and has buffy flanks not pale .
