Description of the Bird

Sparrow sized. Dark spot behind the cheek with a second dark mark also behind the cheek. Dark malar strip and mustache strip. Ochre face, but I'm not sure if that uis the right label for the color; see the attached photos. Upper breast streaks further down on the breast sides. Ochre center crown strip, visible in photo 2217. Dark back feathers bordered by white, maybe a juvunile?
