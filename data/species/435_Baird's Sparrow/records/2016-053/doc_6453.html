Description of the Bird

A sparrow similar in size/structure to GRSP with the flat head and the large bill.  Streaky.

Face buffy.  Lower back side of auricular patch has two dark spots.  No eyestripe.  Crown ochre toned with dark bown streaks.  No median stripe on the crown.  Throat white with double mustache lines. Back of neck buffy with dark brown streaks..

Undersides grayish white with upper chest streaked. 

Song distinctive - a trilly deet-deet-dee-dee - downward slurred.

Similar Species Discussion

Separated from the other ammos (GRSP, HESP, NESP, LESP) as well as SAVS by song.
