Description of the Bird

Sparrow sized bird, similar to a junco with gray head and chest and paler under parts, brown and streaked back and wings, distinct black around will and chin and a bright pinkish bill.
Behaviors: I saw the bird perched at the end of a twig of a juniper.
Call: none that I heard
Plumage: adult breeding

Similar Species Discussion

No junco has a black chin, no other sparrow has such a junco-like plumage
