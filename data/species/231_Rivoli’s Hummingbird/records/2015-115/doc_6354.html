Description of the Bird

This hummingbird came to a Fuchsia hanging basket on my porch. The first thing I noticed was the low pitched hum of her wings; then her size; and finally the white postocular patch.  She was timid when approached by the Broad-tailed Hummingbirds.  She would give way to their "bullying" but return to the feeders and the Fuchsia, which seemed to be her favorite. 

Similar Species Discussion

Broad-tailed, Rufous, and Calliope Hummingbirds are common here and this girl was the size of a Pine Siskin.
