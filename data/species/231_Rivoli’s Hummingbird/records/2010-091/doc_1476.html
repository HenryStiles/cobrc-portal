Description of the Bird

A large very dark green hummingbird. Looked twice the size of the female and juvenile Broad-tailed that were visiting the feeder. Bill long and straight. Wings slightly beyond tip of tail. Grayish below with hints of gray-dot striping on throat.
Behaviors: Came into a hummingbird feeder hanging in a ponderosa pine tree. Not aggressive, easily chased off by other hummers. Fed at feeder several times. Was seen about 4-5 times during a one and a half hour time period.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: Adult Female

Similar Species Discussion

This bird was gigantic compared to the other hummingbirds present. (Broad-tailed, Rufous, Calliope)
