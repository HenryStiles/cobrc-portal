Description of the Bird

 Large Hummingbird , green crown , back and rump , white elongated spot behind eye , dark auriculars , long bill , small dark spots on throat becoming larger green spots on breast belly and flanks . White edges seen on tips of tail when bird approached feeder .When perched in pine tree before approaching feeder , often gave a flat chip call.

Similar Species Discussion

Other large hummingbird to be considered would be Blue-throated which has a smaller bill , elongated white supercilium extending behind the eye , bronzy rump , gray underparts not spotted and larger white spots on tail edges .
