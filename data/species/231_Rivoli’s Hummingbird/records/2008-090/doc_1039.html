Description of the Bird

Large green hummingbird. With small white coming down behind the ears, not like Blue-throated Hummingbird. See photos.
Behaviors: Coming to hummingbird feeders.
Call: no
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Blue-throated Hummingbird - nearly the same size, with long white ear lines, behind the ears. More grayish underneath.
