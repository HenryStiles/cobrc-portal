Description of the Bird

This warbler was one I had never seen, yellow eye mask and black hood with black bill. Yellow breast and under parts with olive colored wings. I generally obsered him in the evenings around 5-6pm. I observed short flights from low branches to the groudn where he spent much time foraging.

Similar Species Discussion

other warblers, such as the yellow rumped, yellow throated warbler, townsends warbler, goldfinche etc. but the distinct yellow eye mask and black hood did not match any other warblers when compared to.
