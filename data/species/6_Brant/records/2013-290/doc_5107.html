Description of the Bird

A smaller goose was feeding on the lawn with a flock of about 100 Canada Geese. The observed bird lacked the white cheek patch of a Canada Goose. It's head and beak were black and it had a half ring of white on the nape. It's feet were also dark black. The wings were very dark; black and dark brown while the belly was grey and vent area white.

Similar Species Discussion

Cackling Goose was considered because of size but was lacking white cheek patch.
