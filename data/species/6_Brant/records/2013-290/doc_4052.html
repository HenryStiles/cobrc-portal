Description of the Bird

Bird was with Canada geese on soccer field. Smaller that Canada geese. Black head, eye, bill, and feet. White necklace. Paler belly, definite line from neck to belly, white under tail coverts .No white wing bars which would lead me to a juvenile. Medium brown winds, black primaries. Resembles mostly the picture of a pale-bellied Adult, Sibleys Guide to Birds, pg 76. I did not hear any calls. It was plucking and eating grass like the Canada geese .

Similar Species Discussion

The size and distinctive colors and white necklace made this bird stand out. Black head and feet are also indicative of a Brant.

Resources Used
