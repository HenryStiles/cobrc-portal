Description of the Bird

I noticed a goose that was smaller than nearby Canada geese. Then I noticed it had a dark head and a white collar. It was walking around on ice on a frozen reservoir. It seemed to drink from nearby water.

Similar Species Discussion

I have seen Brants a few times before, so it was immediately recognized as such. My photos were also quickly approved by the eBird reviewer.
