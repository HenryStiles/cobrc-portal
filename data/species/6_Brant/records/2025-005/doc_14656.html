Description of the Bird

Small mostly black goose (smaller than cackling) with all black head and white necklace. Dark breast with some lighter baring lower beast/side. White rump with dark tail feathers. On the outskirts of a group of Cackling Geese at the edge of open water on Thomas Reservoir. Occassionally resting, preening and eating snow/ice. Appeared to get bullied by Cackling Geese occassionally. 

Similar Species Discussion

Smaller than the Cackling Geese that are present. Pretty unique species.
