Description of the Bird

A small gull, with short black bill, small black dot behind eyes, white head, white underparts that were tinged with pink. Gray mantle and wings. It flight it had a white tail that was wedge shaped. The under wing color was dark gray, though not black like in Little Gull. See photos.
Behaviors: Flying and sitting on the water at Cherry Creek Reservoir.
Call: none
Plumage: adult basic plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Little Gull -- under wings are black and tail shape is not wedged

none needed.

Previous Experience

I saw one in this exact plumage, and looked exactly the same, except I couldn't see any pink color underneath. It was at John Martin Reservoir, Bent County, Colorado, two weeks before (the same bird?) showed up in Boulder County.
