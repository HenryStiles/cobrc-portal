Description of the Bird

large orangish thrush with gray back, orange supercilium, orange chin and throat, dusky breast band, two orangish wingbars on grayish wing.

Dark eye and bill.
Behaviors: Somewhat secretive coming to various feeders, and also raking bill back and forth in still unfrozen dirt
Call: none heard
Plumage: probably 1st basic

Similar Species Discussion

American Robin
Has the same size and shape, but with out the dark mask with orange super, orangish wingbars, and dusky breast band.
