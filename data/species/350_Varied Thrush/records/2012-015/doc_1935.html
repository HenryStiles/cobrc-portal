Description of the Bird

Thrush sized and shaped bird with blue-gray rump, back, nape, and crown. Orange breast and throat. V-shaped black breast-band. Orange supercilium and wing-bars. Bold black eye-stripe. Gray tail.
Behaviors: Picking on ground, easily disturbed and very flightly.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
