Description of the Bird

Adult male Varied thrush, Robin sized.
Orange throat, with a black breast band. Orange eyebrows. Longish curved bill.
Behaviors: Eating on my flat tray bird feeder. (millet I think) Non-aggressive to other birds that came to the feeder. (black capped chickadees, juncos) Shy to neighbors movements. Flew to pine trees but then came back to the feeder.
Call: No song heard
