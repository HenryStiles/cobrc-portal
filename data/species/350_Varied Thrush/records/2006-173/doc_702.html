Description of the Bird

It was a robin sized bird. It had a dark to black face mask, head, and breast band. It also had dark gray back, tail, and parts of the wings. Orange throat, supercillium, upper belly and breast (excluding the dark breastband), and flanks. It had a white undertail covert and lower belly. Yellow to orange legs. Dark bill.
Behaviors: I first saw it on the ground beside a camper, then it flushed low into the nearest tree, where it sat in the same spot for a while. Then it started hoping up the branches until it got about half way up the tree into thick branches where it sat the rest of the time while I watched it.
Call: I didn't hear it call or sing
