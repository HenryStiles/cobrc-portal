Description of the Bird

Robin sized bird with gray back, nape and crown, with buffy orange eyebrow, wing bars and secondary stripe; brown wings and tail, brick orange underparts fading to whitish on vent; broad black chest band and eye mask; tail shorter than that of American Robin; dark bill more slender than that of American Robin, similar in length.
Behaviors: Foraging in small trees and on ground with other birds (e.g. starlings).
Call: none
Plumage: first basic

Similar Species Discussion

American Robin lacks black breast band and wingbars
