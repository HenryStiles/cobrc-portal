Description of the Bird

Seen with a flock of American Robins. Similar shaped and size of nearby American Robins. Dark head, orange stripe from the eye down to the start of the nape. Black area from the bill to the nape. Orange chin and throat, thick black band on the chest. The belly was orange, white under tail. Grayish back, with two orange wing bars on each wing. Bill mostly dark, eyes dark, legs orange.
Behaviors: on the ground with other American Robins.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
