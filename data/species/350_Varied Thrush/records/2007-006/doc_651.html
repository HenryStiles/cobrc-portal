Description of the Bird

Eye line very conspicuous even in dense shade under blue spruce tree where bird first seen; breast band could be seen well; all diagnostic field marks, including slender bill, seen well with 6x35 binoculars when bird moved into sun; photographed with 10x Kodak digital camera.
Behaviors: Feeding on ground, perhaps on cracked corn and sunflower hearts; watering at snow level heated bath; sunning in chokecherry tree.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: Male

Similar Species Discussion
