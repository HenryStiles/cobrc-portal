Description of the Bird

A Robin-like bird with orange supercillium, gray breastband, unique orange markings on wings, whitish undertail coverts and flesh-tone legs. The bird was deemed a female by the gray color of the breastband and auriculars.
Behaviors: skulking around on the ground in wooded area with small pond.
Call: none
Plumage: adult female

Similar Species Discussion

Unique orange markings on wings make ID straightforward for this species
