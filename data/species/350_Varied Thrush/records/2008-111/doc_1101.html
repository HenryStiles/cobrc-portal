Description of the Bird

It was about robin sized. It had orange on the throat and upper breast, the upper belly, the flanks. It had an orange supercillium and some orange on the wings. It had dark gray to black head, face mask, and lower breast band. It was also gray on the back, tail, and most of the wings. It had a white undertail covert and lower belly. It also had yellow to orange legs and a dark bill.
Behaviors: It was on the ground right by a camper. Then it flushed low into a tree, where is sat for awhile. Then it started going up higher into the tree and into thicker branches, where it stayed the rest of the time I watched it.
Call: I didn't hear it call or sing
Plumage: First Basic

Similar Species Discussion

The orange on the upper belly, throat, supercillium, and wings eliminates American Robin. Also, the dark breast band and the leg color eliminates American Robin.
