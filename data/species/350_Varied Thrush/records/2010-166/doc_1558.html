Description of the Bird

Gray back with orange wing stripes. It has a orange belly and throat with a black breast band. It had white undertail coverts. The head is mostly black with a orange stripe above the eye.
Behaviors: It was mostly on the ground feeding on anything it could find with a large group of American Robins. The size of the bird was just slightly smaller than the nearby robins.
Call: It was giving a chip note similar to that of a Hermit Thrush, it was a chup note, but a little harder than a HETH.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

The orange belly eliminates everything but American Robin. The orange wing stripes and orange stripe above the eye and the black breast band eliminates American Robin and everything else.
