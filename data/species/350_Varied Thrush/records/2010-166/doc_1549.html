Description of the Bird

I observed an American Robin sized thrush with a blueish back and orange belly with black auriculars and black chest band. The wings had orange spots.
Behaviors: I observed the bird in a front yard of an suburban neighborhood foraging for cranberries close to shrubs in front of the house.
Call: possibly heard the short whistle like call
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

I ruled out all other thrushes by the black chest band and blue on orange color of the body.
