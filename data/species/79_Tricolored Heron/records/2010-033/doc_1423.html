Description of the Bird

This was a heron based on the long legs and long, spear-like bill and long neck.

The bill was very long for a heron, mostly horn colored,. The head and very long, curved neck was gray blue on the dorsal parts and sides but white along a narrow stripe on the ventral surface. The back was the same color blue as the dorsal surface of the neck. The belly was white and contrasted with both the back and the sides of the neck. The long legs were dull greenish.
Behaviors: Mostly stationary. It did walk along the shore for about 100 feet.
Call: none heard
Plumage: non-breeding adult

Similar Species Discussion

Only heron in the U.S./Canada with white belly contasting with blue back and head/neck.
