Description of the Bird

a medium sized heron (larger than the four Snowy Egrets it was standing near. Long, curved neck with three colors visible if seen well, gray-blue on the backside and continuing up to top of bill and down to wing tips and tail. Whtie chin belly and much of under wing (visible in flight see photo). On neck between grayblue and white is a thin band of brown see well when the bird faces the observer. Long bill the was dark on top and lighter underneath. Leg color not observed.
Behavior: Seen both perched at edge of water and in flight but not feeding.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other medium-sized heron with the three colors on neck. Great Blue much bigger and without the white on the underwing.
