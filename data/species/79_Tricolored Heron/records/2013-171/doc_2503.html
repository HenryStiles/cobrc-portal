Description of the Bird

Heron with long legs and neck in comparison with adjacent snowy egret but about half the size of a Great Blue. Dark gray blue back and neck , white belly , in flight shows white underwing coverts and belly.
Behavior: Feeding and preening along lake shoreline with Snowy Egrets . When disturbed flew to other side of lake.
Call: None heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other heron has this combination of gray and white.
