Description of the Bird

blue cap on top of head, red check and back of neck, also red stripe down the front of the neck. Blue and red wings and back. White belly and sides of neck. Yellow legs, eyes, and lores. Pink upper mandible and dark upper mandible, with the tip darker than the rest.
Behaviors: It was acting like an Egretta heron, dancing around while feeding with wings open, but not as long as Reddish Egret. Other wise it they would just either stand or walk through the water.
Call: didn't hear them
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

This bird had relatively long neck and legs for a fairly compact body, which is different than other herons. It had a pale belly which other Egretta herons usually do not have. It was about medium sized for an Egretta heron.
