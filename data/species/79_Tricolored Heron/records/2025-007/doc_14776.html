Description of the Bird

  • Much smaller than a Great Blue Heron 
  • The bill is longer and thinner than that of a Great Blue Heron and pale yellow
  • Reddish on grey neck.
  • Reddish eye
  • Buffy plumes on lower back
  • Remained on water for over an hour not hunting 

Similar Species Discussion
