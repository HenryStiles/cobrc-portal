Description of the Bird

Medium sized heron. Seemed a bit larger than SNEG. Much smaller than GBHE. Neck, back and wings bluish except pale buff plumes on mid back and hint of purple on head and neck. Underparts white, though white on anterior neck more of a stripe, with sides of neck bluish/purple-blue. Bill pale towards base and dusky towards tip. Lores yellow. Legs pale (seemingly yellow or yellow-green). Two short white nuchal plumes.
