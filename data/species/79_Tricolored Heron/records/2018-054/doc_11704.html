Description of the Bird

Medium sized heron. Neck blue gray turning to chestnut on chest. Remainder of underparts white. Head blue-gray with some orange on chin. Short white plume from either side of head. Legs greenish. Wings and back blue gray except some brownish (plumes?) on lower back
