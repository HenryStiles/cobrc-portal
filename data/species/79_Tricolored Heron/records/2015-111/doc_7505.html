Description of the Bird

Mid-sized heron with a white stomach, yellow-green legs, long straight pointed bill that was black on the tip/upper mandible and yellow towards the base/lower mandible.  The back was blue gray with a noticeable white spot on the shoulder, the throat was white, and the top of the back and back of the neck were brownish gray.  The neck was long and thin.

Similar Species Discussion

Great Blue Heron would be larger, have darker legs, a darker stomach, and more white in the face.

Little Blue Heron would not have a white stomach.
