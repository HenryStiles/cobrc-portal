Description of the Bird

Heron about half the size of an adjacent Great Blue , dark blue gray breast contrasting with white belly , dark back and neck , light area under chin , long straight gray bill makes this an easy ID for Tricolored .
Behavior: Perched on an exposed log in the water , sharing with other herons , ducks and DC Cormorants. Preening from time to time .
Call: None Heard
Plumage: adult
