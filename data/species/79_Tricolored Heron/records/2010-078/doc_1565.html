Description of the Bird

It has a long thin gray bill with a black tip. A dark blue and purplish neck and head with a white chin and a yellow front of neck. It had a red eye. The color on the back was the same color as the head and neck. It had a white belly and lower breast. It also had long pinkish gray legs.
Behaviors: When I saw it the bird was on the far end of the cattails. For more of the time that I watched it was actively feeding, including the dashing that is does to feed and a few times it did the dancing that Egretta occasionally does, mostly REEG. It then disappeared into the cattails.
Call: Didn’t hear it
Plumage: Adult Breeding

Similar Species Discussion

With the pinkish gray legs that eliminates all the other herons. Also, with the dark blue/purple neck and body that eliminates all the herons other than Little Blue Heron and Reddish Egret, but the white belly then eliminates those two.
