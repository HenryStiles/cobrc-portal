Description of the Bird

Very large sparrow. Brown spotted head with white chin triangle. Pink Bill, white belly. Small inverted well defined spotted "V" on breast. White wing bars.
Behaviors: Very active. Behavior similar to Spotted Towhee. Actively scratching the ground and dashing back under cover.
Call: none heard
Plumage: First year

Similar Species Discussion

in with Song Sparrow, Spotted Towhees, Dark-eyed Juncos.
Made size comparison easy..so distinctive from Song Sparrow due to 1) Size 2) Pink Bill 3) Bold Markings 4) Bright White belly
