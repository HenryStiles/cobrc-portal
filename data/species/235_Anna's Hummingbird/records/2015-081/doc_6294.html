Description of the Bird

Mark Peterson and I went to see this bird.  When we arrive, we quickly saw the bird, and saw it for nearly an hour.  Getting nice looks and photos, of a hummingbird with pink head and throat, and very gray underparts.  See photos.  Nothing looks like an adult male Anna's Hummingbird.

Similar Species Discussion

The pink head and throat elminates the more common Broad-tailed Hummingbird and all other hummingbirds.

Previous Experience

I've seen lots of adult male Anna's Hummingbirds, mostly in Arizona and California.  This was my first adult male in Colorado.
