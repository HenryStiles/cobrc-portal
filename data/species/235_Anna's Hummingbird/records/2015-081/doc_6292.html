Description of the Bird

 Hummingbird with red throat and red crown , straight bill , greenish wash on breast , green back , tail longer than primary tips at rest , pale eye ring . aggressively defending feeders (2 ) against Broasd-tailed , Black-chinned and Rufous Hummingbirds . Perched close to feeders in adjacent aspen tree . First reported by home owner , Paul Tennery prvious day .
