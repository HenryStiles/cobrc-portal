Description of the Bird

I counted various amount of White-throated Swifts from 3 Dec 2015 to 17 Jan 2016.  I counted 33 at the dam at Pueblo Reservoir, on the Pueblo Reservoir CBC.  I think other people counted larger numbers during this time.  The last time, I saw two on 17 Jan 2016 with Stephany McNew.  Margie Joy saw one on 28 Jan 2016, which might be the last date.  See photos, large swift, with white throat.  Photos taken on 3 Dec 2015, which watching them with Steve Mlodinow. Edited: I saw ten flying west of Valco Ponds parking lot, on 16 Feb 2016, with Margie Joy and Barbara Lochard.

Previous Experience

Seen lots of these in CO, and other western states.  It is surprising to have a wintering group, perhaps up to 150 birds, around Pueblo in the winter.
