Description of the Bird

Oriole chatter; typically for orioles, the bird was visiting a nectar feeder and an orange feeder.

Similar Species Discussion

Bird is too orange for first cycle orchard oriole; head and facial pattern wrong for Bullock's; black "hood" (actually more of a facial patch) more extensive than in Altamira or streaked-back oriole.
