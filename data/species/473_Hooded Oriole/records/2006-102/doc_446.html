Habitat

A yard with elms and trumpet vine.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  8x42 leica binoculars, 20 x 60 zoom spotting scope.
Distance:  20 to 30 meters.
Light:  Sunny and windy on 6/19. Sunny, not windy on 6/23.

Description of the Bird

A large oriole with long tail. Orange/Yellow head, black throat, two white wing bars, black back, yellowish rump, yellow belly and under tail coverts, long black tail. Curved bill.
Behaviors: Working the elm trees and Trumpet Vine in the yard.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing has the orange/yellow head, black back, and black throat like a male Hooded Oriole.
