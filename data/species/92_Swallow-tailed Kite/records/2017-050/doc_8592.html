Description of the Bird

Previously reported bird from near this spot.  First seen flying in the distance, no wing flap, long dark forked tail on raptor with white head, white body and white inner parts of dark wings when flying. When perched as seen from mulitple angles, top of white head has parallel buffy streaks; white breast has buffy feather edges. Back in poor light looked silverey but in better light was medium dark gray with dark gray at bases of wings where they folded into the body. Bill with hooked beak.  Long and dark forked tail when perched noticeable but not like it was seen spread while bird was flying.  Silent. Surprisingly spent the entire time preening different parts of its body.

Similar Species Discussion

There are no similar species. Coloration wise similar to Osprey but that is where the resemblance ends.
