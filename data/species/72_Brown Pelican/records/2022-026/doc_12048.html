Description of the Bird

This bird was originally found at Cherry Creek State Park in Arapahoe County on 4/15 by Buzz Schaumberg. It continued at Cherry Creek on 4/16 in the morning, but took off to the west by late morning. This morning (4/17) I found the bird in the bay off Kingfisher lot. Renee Cassias was also present at the time, and more observers showed up after we got word out. It eventually moved east, where it was obscured by trees. However, viewing was still decent from the wildlife viewing platform in Douglas County. 

Large, brown waterbird with large bill and whitish head. MUCH smaller than American White Pelicans it was associating with. 

Similar Species Discussion

Not really many species similar to pelicans. American white eliminated by the much smaller size and overall brown color. 

Resources Used

Nothing specific. Very familiar with species and had just seen the bird the day prior at Cherry Creek SP.

Previous Experience

Have lived in San Diego and have spent a lot of time along Texas coast and east coast, where I’ve seen probably thousands of Brown Pelicans. Have also lived in Colorado for several years where American White Pelicans are regular. Plus I’ve occasionally seen both Pelican species as vagrants in a few different states (including Colorado on 4/16/2022 at Cherry Creek SP).
