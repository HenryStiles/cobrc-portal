Description of the Bird

Large predominantly gray bird with a large very elongated triangular bill with a bulbous tip.

Head mostly white with a funky rusty-brown tuft at the top of the back of the head.  Neck dark gray.  Bill dark gray with white patches near the back of the bill.  Orbital patch a fleshy color.

Appears to be the Atlantic race of BRPE in breeding plumage.
