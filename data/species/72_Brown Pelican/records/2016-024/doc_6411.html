Description of the Bird

I arrived at Lake Beckwith in Colorado City to see Dave Silverman's Brown Pelican.  When I drove to the parking lot near the dam, I saw the bird swimming on the far shore.  I also saw the bird flying around and plunge dive.  The bird was a brown colored pelican shaped bird.  See photos.
