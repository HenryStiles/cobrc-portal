Description of the Bird

A smaller pelican that was brown in color, sitting on rock, below John Martin Reservoir dam.  See photo.  This bird was found on 14 April 2016 at this location, and last seen on 24 April 2016 at this location.  People have better closer photos.  It appears it isn't the same bird from Lake Beckiwith, earlier in the month.
