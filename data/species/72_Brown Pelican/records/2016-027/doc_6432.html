Description of the Bird

Large gray-brown pelican hanging out with the AMWPs (see picture).  Initially seen surfing and fishing in the JMR outflow, later refound on Lake Hasty. 

Having seen the Lake Beckwith, Pueblo County bird, I feel that this is a second BRPE for the state in 2016.  This one appears to be in far more basic plumage than the Beckwith bird.

The date of 04/24 was the last date the bird was reported in eBird.
