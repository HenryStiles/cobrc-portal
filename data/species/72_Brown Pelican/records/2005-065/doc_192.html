Description of the Bird

Clearly a pelican, with long bill atop pouch. Distinctly smaller and darker than nearby American White Pelicans. Body upperparts silvery gray, lower body parts mostly dark brown. Long neck with chestnut brown nape and white on throat. Most of head white. Pouch greenish yellow, bill grayish with reddish orange tip. Pinkinsh ring around pale eye.
Behaviors: Most of the time I saw the bird it was preening or sleeping on a half submerged log.
Call: not heard
Plumage: probable 3rd year

Similar Species Discussion

Too small and coloration wrong for American White Pelican.
