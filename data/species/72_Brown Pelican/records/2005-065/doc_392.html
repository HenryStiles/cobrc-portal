Description of the Bird

Large pelican with white forehead, crown and back of neck. Long grayish bill, tipped with light red. Throat pouch yellow green. Some white extending from the top of the head down the side of the neck. Some chestnut color also on sides of neck. Grayish brown upperparts with slightly lighter gray, speckled, underparts. Large, grayish black legs and feet.
Behaviors: The bird was resting on a log in the middle of a lake.
Call: none heard
Plumage: 3rd cycle
