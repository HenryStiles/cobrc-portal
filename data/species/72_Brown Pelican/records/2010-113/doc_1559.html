Description of the Bird

Brown body with a lighter brown back. Dark brown feathers going up the back of the birds neck and on the breast. White head and upper throat. Black bill with a yellow tip with a little bit of red near the yellow tip, it also has a yellow-olive pouch. It also has a brown eye. The bill is very large and thr bird overall was pretty large, but smaller than American White Pelican.
Behaviors: Mostly just swimming around along the dam, but occasionally flying around and actively feeding, which was diving to try to catch its prey.
Call: Didn’t hear it
Plumage: Subadult Pacific

Similar Species Discussion

With the large bill with a pouch eliminated everything except other pelicans. With the bird being brown with the brown going up the back of the neck eliminates American White Pelican even those with oil stained bodies. The yellow-olive pouch also eliminates Atlantic Brown Pelican.
