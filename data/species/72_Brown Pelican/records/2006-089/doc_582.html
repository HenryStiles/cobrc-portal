Description of the Bird

Bill proportions and pouch unique to pelicans. Brown pelican by smaller size compared to nearby AWPEs, brown coloration on dorsal surfaces, dark bill. Aged as juvenile by white belly.
Behaviors: Sleeping and preening on a floating tire.
Call: not heard
Plumage: Juvenile
