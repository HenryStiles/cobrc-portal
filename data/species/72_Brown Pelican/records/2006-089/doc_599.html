Description of the Bird

This is a large brownish bird...
A pelican
Whose bill holds more than its belly can.
Food for a week
It stores in its beak.
But I don't know how the hell he can.

Huge bill with gular pouch.

Short stout gray brown legs with large webbed feet.
Behaviors: Slept the entire time.
Call: none
Plumage: 1st or 2nd year
