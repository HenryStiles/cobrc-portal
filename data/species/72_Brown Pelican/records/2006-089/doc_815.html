Description of the Bird

Brown colored pelican. Associating with and smaller than American White Pelicans.
Behaviors: Resting with a group of American White Pelicans
Call: None
Plumage: juvenal

Similar Species Discussion

American White Pelican was ruled out because of the size of the bird relative to nearby American White Pelicans and the overall plumage.
