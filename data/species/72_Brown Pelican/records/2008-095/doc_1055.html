Habitat

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  10X42 bins, 30x spotting scope, Sony DSC-7 digital camera used for digiscoped photo.
Distance:  Approximately 100 yards
Light:  Sun with fog burning off, resulting in some glare, but not enough to affect the identification.

Description of the Bird

Dark billed, dark upper-plumaged pelican with a pale belly. It is smaller than the White Pelicans with which it is associating.
Behaviors: Resting within a large flock of White Pelicans on a mud bar a hundred yards or so from shore.
Call: n/a
Plumage: Juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

White Pelicans behind and beside the bird afforded a excellent comparison.
