Description of the Bird

A large, swimming waterbird generally brown; dark brown on head, neck, back, wings, and tail, with paler brown on chest gradually becoming whitish on belly; massive dark bill at least as long as neck, dark eyes

Similar Species Discussion

No other species really similar; bird smaller and much darker than American White Pelican
