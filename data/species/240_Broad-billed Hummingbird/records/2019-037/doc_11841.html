Description of the Bird


The bird came in to the feeder, first landing on nearby branches, then by feeding. It did not stay on the feeder particularly long. It came in twice while we watched.

Similar Species Discussion

This bird was distinctive in its throat pattern and bill coloration, entirely different from the expected species in Colorado.

Resources Used
