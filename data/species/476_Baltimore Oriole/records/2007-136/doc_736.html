Description of the Bird

Full black hood of the male with the orange upper wing bar and white 2nd wing bar and complete orange body. I also saw the female with all lighter orange body, paler face, no hood, 2 white wing bar. The size of a Bullock's Oriole but both male and female plumage is very different than Bullock's. Photos are attached of both. The female photo is poor quality. Male was chashing and staying close to female. Food may have been passed once. They are a couple. Could not find the nest.
Behaviors: See above. Obvious pair.
Call: call a 2 note whistle
Plumage: breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Size of Bullock's but completely different plumage as discribed above.
