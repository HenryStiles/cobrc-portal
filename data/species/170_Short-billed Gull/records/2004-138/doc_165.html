Description of the Bird

Compared to nearby Ring-billed Gulls, thinner bill. Dark eye. White crescents visible on back. Brownish around head and neck.
Behaviors: Walking on ice very close to north edge of Sheldon Lake.
Call: Did not call.
Plumage: Adult nonbreeding

Similar Species Discussion

Compared to Ring-billed Gulls present. Referenced Sibley.

Resources Used

Sibley, Kaufmann.

Previous Experience

Moderate only. Have seen this species in California and Ireland previously, total life sightings however only 4.
