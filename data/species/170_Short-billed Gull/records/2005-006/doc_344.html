Description of the Bird

A small gull. The bird had a white head with some brown streaking. Dark eyes. Dark gray mantle and wings, with white cresents. Black tail, with more bigger white mirrors. An all yellow small thin bill. The underparts were mostly white. Leg color wasn't seen, since the bird was only seen swimming.
Behaviors: Swimming on pond.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull - adults have ring around bill, light gray mantle and wings, no cresents.
