Description of the Bird

A gull that was a little smaller with darker gray mantle then nearby Ring-billed Gulls. All yellow bill, thin yellow legs, and dark eyes. See photos.
Behaviors: Standing on the ice.
Call: none
Plumage: adult basic

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gulls are bigger, with ring on bill, lighter gray mantle, yellow eyes, thicker legs.
