Description of the Bird

small gull slightly smaller and darker-mantled than the common Ring-billed Gulls with which is was sitting on the ice. Noticeable thin all-yellow bill. Mantle with both a white scapular and tertial crescent. Head and hind-neck very heavily streaked with brown. Legs and feet, yellow. Head rounded and bill petite.
Behaviors: Sat on ice only. With about 40 RBGUs.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult basic

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull comes closest but that taxon is slightly larger, lighter gray on back, less round-headed and without strong contrasting white scapular and tertial crescents. It's bill has a noticeable dark ring at the distal end and is both longer and thicker.
