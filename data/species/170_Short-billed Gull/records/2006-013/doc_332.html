Description of the Bird

A small gull, smaller than the Ring-billed Gulls, with thinner bill. Darker mantle, and more brown on the head, chin, and throat.
Behaviors: Sitting on the southeastern shore of Pueblo Reservoir with many other gulls, including Ring-billed, California, Herring, Lesser Black-backed, and Glaucous.
Call: none
Plumage: first year

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull - bigger, thick bill, not as rounded head.
