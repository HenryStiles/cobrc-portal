Viewing Conditions

Light:  getting dark on Feb 10, 2005. Seen in fairly cloudy conditions on Feb 18, 2005.

Description of the Bird

A gull smaller than the nearby Ring-billed Gulls, with dark eyes, darker gray mantle, and an all yellow small bill. Photo taken and submitted.
Behaviors: The bird was observed at Pueblo Reservoir on Feb 10th, 2005 and then along the Arkansas River, below Pueblo Reservoir dam on Feb 18, 2005 (photo taken at that time).
Call: none
Plumage: adult winter

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull - this species is bigger, lighter mantled, has ring around bill, has light colored eyes.
