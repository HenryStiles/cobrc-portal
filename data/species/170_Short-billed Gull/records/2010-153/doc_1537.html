Description of the Bird

In comparison with adjacent Ring-billed Gulls , a little smaller in size , darker mantle , broad white secondaries , black primaries with white spots , fine dark streaking on back of white head with a darker patch around eye ( also dark ) and an unmarked yellow-green bill . Bird was swimming with a large group of Ring-billed's and looked smaller headed , shorter necked .
Behaviors: Swimming with Ring-billed Gulls . Size and shape differences characteristic. did not observe in flight.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: adult non breeding

Similar Species Discussion

A white-headed adult gull in non-breeding plumage , slightly smaller than adjacent Ring-billed Gulls , shape of unmarked yellow-green bill , from Laughing Gull .
