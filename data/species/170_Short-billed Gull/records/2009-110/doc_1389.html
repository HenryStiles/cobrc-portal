Description of the Bird

A gull a little smaller than nearby Ring-billed Gulls. All yellow bill, dark eyes, darker gray mantle, brownish feathers on the head/neck. White underparts, yellow legs. See photos.
Behaviors: Standing with other gulls.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull - has ring around the bill, yellow eyes, lighter gray mantle.
