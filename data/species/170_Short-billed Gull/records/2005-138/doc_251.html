Description of the Bird

A Ring-billed Gull sized Larus gull, with a slightly darker mantle, a smaller un-marked bill, smaller head, large tertial crescent and dark iris.
Behaviors: standing on a log in the water
Call: none
Plumage: definitive basic

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull is similar, photos show differences mainly in bill, head size and mantle color
