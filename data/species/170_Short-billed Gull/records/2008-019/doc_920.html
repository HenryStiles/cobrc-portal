Description of the Bird

A small Ring-billed type gull, thin bill, more brown overall, with darker gray mantle. Dark eyes, round head. Thin pink legs.

See photos (bird swimming in the water and standing on dock). Also, Van Truan's flight photo.
Behaviors: Seen on the tires & dock, swimming in the water, and flying around the North Shore Marina at Pueblo Reservoir.
Call: none
Plumage: first basic

Similar Species Discussion

Ring-billed Gull - larger, thicker bill, thicker legs, not as brown overall, lighter gray mantle.
