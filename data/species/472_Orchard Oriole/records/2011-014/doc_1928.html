Description of the Bird

This is a courtesy follow up for documentation of a sighting in Routt County around 31 May 2011. The bird was coming to a feeder of Nancy Merrill of Hayden.
Behaviors: Coming to jelly feeder. Not associating with Bullock's orioles.
Call: Not available
Plumage: first year

Similar Species Discussion

Bullock's oriole. Spring/fresh Bullock's much brighter, males with impressive orange. This bird quite dull, mixing of greenish under feathers not typical of mid-molt Bullocks.
