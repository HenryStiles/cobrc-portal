Description of the Bird

Bird was silent.  Large bright yellow bird with oversized dark bill compatible with grosbeak, but larger and bulkier than nearby Black-headed Grosbeaks. Upper mandible black, lower steely gray. Very dark wings with 2 prominent wing bars. White vent. Darker streakiness on head, back, and flanks apparently favors immature male. Bird originally found, identified and reported on Ebird by homeowner, Gip Rokich. During most of our observation was feeding at a black oil sunflower seed feeder.
