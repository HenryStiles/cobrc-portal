Description of the Bird

Gib Rokich found this bird at his feeders.  He posted the sighting to e-bird and the CFO Facebook page.  Since I know him, I sent him a message, asking if I could come see it and get more photos.  He agreed.  I picked up uncle Van Truan in Pueblo, and we arrived at 2:30pm.  Saw the bird from 2:39pm to 2:55pm in the cottonwoods and then at the feeders.  We took many photos and got nice looks.  The bird was a large grosbeak, that was very yellow.  See photos.
