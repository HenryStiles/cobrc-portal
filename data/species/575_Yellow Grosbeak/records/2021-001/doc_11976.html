Description of the Bird

Bright yellow bird with a huge black bill.  Mostly black back with some white flecks, and black wings with two white wingbars.  White undertail coverts.  Prominent black eye and dark legs.  Bird had some dark streaks in the eyeline and on top of the head, which made me think it was possibly a first summer male.

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing else like it in North America: certainly not a Black-headed, Rose-breasted, Blue or Crimson-collared Grosbeak.

Resources Used

Sibley and National Geographic field guides.

Previous Experience

I have seen this species only 2 or 3 times before in west Mexico.  Very familiar with Black-headed, Rose-breasted and Blue Grosbeaks.  Have only seen one female Crimson-collared Grosbeak.
