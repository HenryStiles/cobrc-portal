Description of the Bird


The bird did appear to open its bill and act as though calling, though I cannot say for certain if I heard a call.


It was sleeping a bit more than the surrounding Canada Geese. It eventually flew with a group of Canada Geese (leading them in fact) a quarter of a mile away after about 90 minutes of observation.
