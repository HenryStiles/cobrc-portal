Description of the Bird

Sapsucker with white chin and red throat, and red on the nape. See photo.
Behaviors: On pine tree.
Call: none
Plumage: adult female

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow-bellied Sapsucker - females have white chin and throat, not a white chin with red throat. Usually they lack red on the nape, and the back pattern is different.
