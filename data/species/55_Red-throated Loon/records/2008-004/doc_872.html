Description of the Bird

Small loon, white face, gray hind-neck, small up turned bill. See photos.
Behaviors: swimming and diving, seen with Western and Clark's Grebes, and a Red-necked Grebe.
Call: none
Plumage: basic adult

Similar Species Discussion

Pacific Loon - straighter bill, w/o white face.
Arctic Loon - lack of white face, thicker bill.
