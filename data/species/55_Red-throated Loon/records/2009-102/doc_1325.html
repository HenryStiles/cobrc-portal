Description of the Bird

The bird had a thin up-turned bill, white face, gray neck, and was a small loon. The shape of the bird was typical for a Red-throated Loon. See photos.
Behaviors: Swimming and diving in the water.
Call: none
Plumage: basic plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Pacific Loon -- straight, thicker bill.
Arctic Loon -- thicker bill
Common and Yellow-billed Loons -- way bigger
Grebes -- none have up-turned thin bills

none

Previous Experience

Seen lots of Red-throated Loons, Pacific Loons, Commons, and a few Yellow-billed and Arctic Loons. Seen all the grebes in North America.
