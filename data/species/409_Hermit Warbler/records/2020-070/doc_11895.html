Description of the Bird

Plain bright yellow face and crown, black throat . Dark black eye.  No eyering.  Yellow lores.  Plain white breast, sides, flanks, belly, UTC and tail with no streaking.  Black mantle and upperparts.  Two white wingbars.  White edging seen when wings folded on black primaries and secondaries.   

Similar Species Discussion

Black-throated Green Warbler eliminated based upon plain yellow face lacking auricular patch or dark lores.  Townsend's Warbler eliminated baded upon plain yellow face and lack of black face patch, dark lores and side streaking.  Golden-Cheeked Warbler eliminated based upon plain yellow face, lack of black eyeline and side streaking and its extremely limited range.
