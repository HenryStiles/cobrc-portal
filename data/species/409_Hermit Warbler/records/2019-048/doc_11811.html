Description of the Bird

Parulid in size and shape. Head and face bright yellow, throat black, some black in the nape, some black spotting on crown, back grayish spotted and streaked with black, underside mostly white, bill sharp and black. Bird was actively feeding in a Mulberry tree with many other warblers. No sound was ever heard. 

Similar Species Discussion

Black-throated Green and Townsend's are greener birds with streaked sides and not clean white underneath.
