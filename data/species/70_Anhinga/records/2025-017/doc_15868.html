Description of the Bird

similar size and shape as Double-crested Cormorant, however the head and neck were light tan, the wings had white highlights and the tail was longer. The neck was folded in a typical posture for the species, and the bill was long, straight and yellow/orange.

Similar Species Discussion

Compared with nearby Double-crested Cormorants, the tan head and neck, small head, long yellow-orange bill, white on the wings and long tail were distinctive.
