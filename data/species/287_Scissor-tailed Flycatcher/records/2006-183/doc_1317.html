Pasture fields along both sides of the road.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Nikon coolpix 8500 through Swarovski scope
Distance:  Approximately 20-30 feet
Light:  Low light in evening

Description of the Bird

Light gray with long tail. Observed the divided tail when he flew. Tail was grayish with black tips, reddish to salmon colored axillaries, and salmon wing linings. Bill and legs appeared black to dark gray. Wings were dark gray to black.
Behaviors: Sat on barbed wire fence. Flew to both sides of road. Flew down into pasture and back to wire.
Call: none heard
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing like this bird! Long tail and coloring.
