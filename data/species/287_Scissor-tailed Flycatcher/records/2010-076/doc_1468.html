Description of the Bird

A gray-headed, dark-winged flycatcher with pink sides and a long, dark forked tail.
Behaviors: Fly catching in the surrounding fields, occasionally perching on fence wires.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Western Kingbird is similar but is yellow with a tail about 1/4 length... Fork-tailed Flycatcher is structurally very similar but is black and white and hasn't occured in CO (yet).
