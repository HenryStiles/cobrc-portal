Description of the Bird

Unmistakeable flycatcher with extremely long dark tail. I have described it as in "breeding" plumage based on the white patch in the tail visible in photos.


NOTE: This species does not require documentation. However, I thought that a sighting in Chaffee County might be sufficiently significant to report it.

Similar Species Discussion

There is really no other species with which this bird can be confused.
