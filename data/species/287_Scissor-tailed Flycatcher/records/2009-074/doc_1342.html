Description of the Bird

Largish flycatcher with gray head/back. They had very long narrow dark/white tails and dark wings. There was also a pinkish hue to the belly of one bird.
Behaviors: 1) Sitting on barbed wire fence
2) Sitting on exposed dead branch
3) "Flycatching" from both perches
4) Chasing each other
Call: N/A - I did not hear them
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Western Kingbird. There were nesting WEKIs in this same tree. WEKI/STFL seen chasing each other. WEKI was eliminated due to lack of yellow tones and much longer tail.
