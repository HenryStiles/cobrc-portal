Description of the Bird

The bird was never seen.  At about 8:40 it started calling from some distance, and I couldn't hear it.  Finally, at 9:05 pm it came rather close and sang vigorosly for 5 minutes.  I could hear it excellently, even with my diminished hearing.  Listening to the songs on ebird, the bird sounded just like Mexican, and not Eastern, Whip-poor-will.

Similar Species Discussion

Eastern Whip-poor-will by the song, which goes for all the other goatsuckers as well. Poorwill with its two note call was heard in the same location before the Whip-poor-will.

Resources Used

My ears and the two songs for Whip-poor-will on ebird.

Previous Experience

Hardly any.  I saw one in SE Arizona about 40 years ago.  Familiar with Poorwill song.
