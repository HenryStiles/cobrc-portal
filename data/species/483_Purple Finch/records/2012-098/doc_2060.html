Description of the Bird

A brown and white streaked finch with dark auriculars and no eyering and curved culmin. See picture.
Behaviors: feeding with other birds in deciduous and juniper trees
Call: none
Plumage: female

Similar Species Discussion

House and Cassin's finch are similar. House finch lacks the dark face pattern (auriculars of Cassin's and Purple Finch). Cassin's Finch normally shows at least a partial pale eyering.
