Description of the Bird

The rasberry purple of the male and the bold face pattern of the female made the ID of the birds straight forward. Bold short fat streaks on the female breast and notched tails of both birds were also diagnostic. Got some good pictures which are attached. Was a state bird for me.
Behaviors: Eating cedar berrys as the photos show. In a small flock of 10-15.
Call: None
Plumage: N/A

Similar Species Discussion

House Finch was ruled out because of the male's color and the females face pattern.
