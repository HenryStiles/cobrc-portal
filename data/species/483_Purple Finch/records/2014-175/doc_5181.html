Description of the Bird

Heard first. Pik, Pik, Pik call. Flew over my head, a relatively long tailed finch, perhaps not as long in tail as HOFI. Landed in bare branches over narrow riparian area (I was standing on street nearby). Only upper half of bird seen. Head reddish with a little brown on face in area of auriculars. Nape reddish. Upper back with red. Chest and throat reddish without streaking. Bill a bit straighter than HOFI. No eyering and not as pinkish as CAFI. Overall color of head and chest pretty uniform except for brown on face. Color pinkish red, more so than HOFI, not as much so as CAFI. Flew off to south giving Pik call again

Similar Species Discussion

See description. Cassin's Finch wiht more pointed bill, eye ring, color paler, call different. House Finch deeper red. Should have seen brown nape, back should lack red, should have seen streaking on sides.
