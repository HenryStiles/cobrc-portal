Description of the Bird

I was counting birds at Rocky Ford SWA, when I saw some House Finches feeding in a tree near the Arkansas River.  One of the finches was clearly a female plumage bird, either Purple or Cassin's Finch.  The face marking made me think, it was a Purple Finch.  I wasn't very close to it, so I rthought I attempt to get photos of it, and then try to get closer, and hopefully see it better and get closer photos.  I tried to get closer and re-found the House Finches, though couldn't find the other finch.

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's Finch has different face pattern.
