Previous Experience

I grew up back east, where House Finch v. Purple Finch was a conundrum. Before I lived in Colorado, I lived in w. Nevada, where we had to deal with western/Pacific Purple Finch, Cassin's Finch, and House Finch. Here in Colorado, House Finch and Cassin's Finch are of course frequently encountered, and I'm aware of the basic issues with regard to vagrant Eastern Purple Finches in e. Colorado in winter.
