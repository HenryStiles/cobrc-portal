Description of the Bird

Bold brown and white pattern on head with triangular bill.
White underparts with distinctive brown streaks.
No streaks on undertail coverts.

The bird was actively feeding at the feeder station.

Similar Species Discussion

Cassin's finch is similar but is not as heavily streaked, has a longer tail, a more pointed bill and has a less bold facial pattern.
