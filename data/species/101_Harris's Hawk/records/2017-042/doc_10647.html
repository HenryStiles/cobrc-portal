Description of the Bird

"Lanky" very dark hawk.  Seen distantly in flight, reminicent of dark buteo, then seen perched facing me head-on.  Key features include yellow face, dark eye, nearly black breast and belly, reddish thighs, white vent, and black tail with broad white tip.  Hints of reddish shoulders seen but not well at the angle I was viewing.

Similar Species Discussion

Briefly considered dark Ferruginous & Red-tailed Hawk when seen at a distance in flight but once I found the bird and scoped it the yellow face jumped out at me along with the other field marks that are unique to Harris's Hawk.
