Description of the Bird

First observed the bird from a distance of approximately 100 yards perched on a wooden structure within a farm corral. It remained in the same spot for the whole observation and we eventually got to about 20 yards from the bird by slowly approaching using the car as a blind. The bird wasn't bothered by the observers as we approached nor did it move when another car drove past.

Very dark colored hawk overall with rufous on the shoulders, pale UTC's, white base to long tail. Faint white tip on the tail and sparse white flecks on the body, so possibly an immature.

Originally reported by Doug Faulkner on COBirds on 4/17/2023.
