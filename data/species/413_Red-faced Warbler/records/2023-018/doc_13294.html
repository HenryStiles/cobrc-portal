Description of the Bird

found by Steve Mlodinow 20 minutes before my arrival. Gray warbler with red face and throat, black cap and auriculars, associating with many other warblers while feeding 10 - 15 ft above ground in leafless mullberry trees, occasionally dropping into currants on ground. No calls were detected, bird well photographed.
