Description of the Bird

observed the bird from my office window at eye level in aspen trees. i assumed the bird was a house finch but it moved like a warbler: rapid gleaning of branches and catkins. i grabbed my camera to investigate, while cathy got binoculars. 

Similar Species Discussion

housefinch.  photos and many clear views shows it is RFW.
