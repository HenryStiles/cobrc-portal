Habitat

Bird was seen in large backyard with many trees and feeders. Large pine tree is right beside bird bath where bird was seen at each sighting.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  None - Too close. Did not need.
Distance:  4 ft to 8 ft
Light:  Sunny day. Sun was at normal position relative to time of day of sightings. Observer was inside of house looking out window. Photos were taken at 12:30 pm sighting.

Description of the Bird

Small warbler size and shape. Head, back and wings grayish blue. Two white wing bars. Greenish yellow spot on back. Breast was yellow with rusty splotches. White eye ring. Yellow throat. Light colored belly. It was overall bright and colorful.
Behaviors: Bird was fluttering very rapidly right next to window for a few seconds. It then lit on the bird bath and then on the deck railing. It did not seem too shy. It left after about 5 minutes and came back an hour later and went back to the bird bath and deck railing. It came back a third time but when I tried to sneak outside for better pictures, it flew and I never saw it again.
Call: Did not hear. Observing through window.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

I do not know of another warbler that has the greenish yellow spot on the back. The yellow on the breast with the rusty splotches. I have seen several pictues of this bird. When I checked the field guides there were no birds except for the Tropical Parula that looked similiar to this bird.
