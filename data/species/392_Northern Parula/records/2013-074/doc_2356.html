Description of the Bird

When I looked up to see what was chipping in the sycamore tree, I noticed this Northern Parula. The reason I'm submitting this sighting, is because of the rare winter date. See photos taken on December 5, 2012, the only day I saw this bird.
Behaviors: Staying high up in the sycamore tree.
Call: heard a warbler chip, which didn't sound like the Yellow-throated or Pine Warbler, that had been around.
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Tropical Parula -- lacks eye arches, that you can clearly see in the photos.
