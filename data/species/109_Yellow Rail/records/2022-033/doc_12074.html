Description of the Bird

When Van Truan and I arrived an hour before sunrise at 4:55am, we heard a Yellow Rail, about 600 yards west of a parking lot.  When we arrived at the parking lot just a few mintues later, a Yellow Rail was also calling there. Later we heard two calling birds at the same time.

Here is a recording I made from the parking lot location, when it was dark and no other birds were singing yet.
