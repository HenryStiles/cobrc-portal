Description of the Bird

An oriole with black hood extending down to upper breast and upper back , yellow not orange underparts , two white wingbars on gray blackish wings and a yellow shoulder stripe/bar.
Behaviors: Singing perched on top of a juniper bush .
Call: Rich fluty song , matching that on my I-phone ( Stokes )
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow underparts and black hood etc rules out Bullock's and Baltimore , black hood and upperbreast /back rules out Hooded and Audubon's , leaving Black-vented which has a reddish tinge at the lower black breast band ( N.Mexico subspecies ) and of course a black vent. I did not consider Black- vented until afterwards so can't honestly say whether I looked hard for the presence or absence of the vent coloration.
