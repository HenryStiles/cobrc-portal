Description of the Bird

Oriole based on bill - head, face and back rich black. Back adn wings dark grayish with upper white wingbar wide with lower white wingbar narrow. Breast and belly rich yellow. Tail black with yellow rectangles on both sides of the upper tail. Bill dark with a lighish blue to the lower mandible base. Undertail and undertail coverts yellow.

Similar Species Discussion

Black-vented Oriole does not have wingbars and has yellow shoulders, and has all black tail and black undertail coverts


Audubon's Oriole has a greenish wash to its back. The tail is all black. Has one thin white wingbar. The blac of the head stops and is replacec by a yellow collar. The black of the head does not extend as low onto the breast as in SCOR.
