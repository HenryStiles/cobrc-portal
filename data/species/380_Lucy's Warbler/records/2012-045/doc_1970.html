Description of the Bird

All white underneath warbler. All gray on top. Red cap, pointy thin bill. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying high up in the trees.
Call: Loud chip note, similar sounding to a Virginia's Warbler.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The only warbler with no color, except for the red cap.

Previous Experience
