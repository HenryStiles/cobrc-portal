Description of the Bird

Van Truan and I were birding below Two Buttes Reservoir dam, and saw a Lucy's Warbler.  All white underneath warbler, and gray on-top.  Van Truan was able to get a couple of photos.  I believe this could be a first sighting for Baca County.
