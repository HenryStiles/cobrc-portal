Description of the Bird

Seen briefly on the ground, bird was buffy tan with dark streaks down the sides of the breast and a plain face with large prominent dark eye.
Behaviors: The bird fly in and landed next to a wet playa surrounded by ag lands walked around for a half minute then flew across the rd and dropped like a rock on folded wings into ankle high native grass.
Call: Squeet Squeet or Skeet skeet, often doubled sometimes single
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

AMPI is the only other similar pipit in central NA. Eliminated by the plain face and dark eye and the voice. The voice is the most important factor in ID
