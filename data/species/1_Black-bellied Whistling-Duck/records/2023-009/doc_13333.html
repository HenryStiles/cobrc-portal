Description of the Bird

Seven adult breeding plumage. Large orange bill with a white thumb nail at the end.  Cinnamon brown strip from top of head down the back of the neck.  The rest of head and face were gray with a bold white eye ring.  Cinnamon brown back, shoulder, breast down to the start of the belly.  Black belly to undertail coverts.  Wing was grayish white with a white stripe at the bottom.Long pink legs and feet.  A broad white wing patch extending half the length of the mostly black wing was observed when one bird stretched its wing while at rest.

Similar Species Discussion

The only expected duck with similar structure is the Fuvous Whistling-Duck which has different bill color and color markings.
