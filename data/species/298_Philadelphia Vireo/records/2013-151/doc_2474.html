Description of the Bird

Smaller Vireo with grayish back and wings. No wing bars. Crown was a darker gray, white supercillium and dark eye line extending through the lores. Throat was yellow. Breast was yellow, darker in the center, pale toward the wing shoulders. Belly and under tail coverts also yellow.
Behavior: Bird was observed gleening insects from new emerging leave buds
Call: none heard
Plumage: basic

Similar Species Discussion

Similar species Warbling V. Less colorful overall, lighter crown, pale eye line that does not extend into the lores. Breast which is more yellow at the wing shoulders than the center of the breast.
Red-Eyed V. much 'greener' back, red eye, much longer bill and white breast.
