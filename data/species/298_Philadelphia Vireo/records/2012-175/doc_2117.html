Description of the Bird

A small vireo, yellow below and greenish-gray above, brightest yellow on the breast. A dark gray cap, dark eyeline extending to the lores. The bird was small billed and round headed compared to Warbling Vireo.
Behaviors: flitting around in a small flock of chickadees and warblers
Call: none
Plumage: fall

Similar Species Discussion

Warbling Vireo can be similar, but lacks the deep yellow throat and breast and usually has some white below. Also, Warbling Vireo has a larger bill and head that is square-ish by comparison.
