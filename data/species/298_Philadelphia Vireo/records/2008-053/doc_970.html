Description of the Bird

A small Vireo without wingbars. Olive above, whitish below with a pale yellow throat. Dark eyeline and dark lores. Pale supercilium. gray legs
Behaviors: feeding methodically in cottonwood, willow and Russian Olive
Call: none noted
Plumage: definitive adult
