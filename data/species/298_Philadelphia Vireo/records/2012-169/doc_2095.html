Description of the Bird

A small vireo, yellow underneath, darkest at throat, greenish-gray above with dark gray cap, dark eyeline including lores and diry-pale supercilliam... see photo
Behaviors: fliting around with a Warbler Vireo, offering excellent comparison.
Call: none
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Warblering Vireo can be similar, PHVI is brightest yellow in throat, WAVI in flanks. Head and bill appeared smaller and head rounded compared to adjacent WAVI
