Description of the Bird

Green backed, pale gray crowned vireo with entirely yellow underparts, brightest on throat and chest. No wingbars. Pale supercilium, Dark line through eye, so lores dusky. Flight feathers contrastingly darker than wingcoverts.
Behaviors: Came into pishing and scolded loudly
Call: scold note, similar to that on iBirdPRO
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Too yellow for any other North American vireo other than YG Vireo, which has more prominent facial pattern, much larger bill. Dark lores also eliminates WAVI
