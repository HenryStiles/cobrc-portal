Similar Species Discussion

Warbling Vireo has crown and back mostly uniform in color, face with less contrast overall - less distinct supercilium, paler grayish eyeline and lore; whitish throat.

WAVI also has primary 10 present and longer than primary coverts (vs. absent or present and shorter in PHVI).
