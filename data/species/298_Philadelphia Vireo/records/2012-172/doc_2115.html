Description of the Bird

Vireo that was totally bright yellow beneath, with gray cap, pale supercilium, dark transocular stripe (so lores dark) and small pale arc below eye. Short tail. Small billed for vireo, but thicker billed than "Vermivora."
Behaviors: scolding at me while I was pishing
Call: irritated scold note.
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow beneath rules out other North American vireos, other than YG Vireo, which has much larger bill. Bigger bill, more robust build than Tennessee or OC Warblers.
