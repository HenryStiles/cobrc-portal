Description of the Bird

Small short tailed vireo with a dark stubby hooked bill. Yellow on throat and belly extending to vent area. Gray cap , whitish supercilium , dark line through eye and lores. Upper back and wings dark green-olive , no wings bars , although wing coverts had some paler fringes from fresh feathering .
Behaviors: Actively feeding in Russian olives , subsequently moved south and observed in willow .
Call: None heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

WAVI does not have yellow throat or dark eye line , REVI does not have yellow throat and has white supercilium bounded by two dark black lines.
