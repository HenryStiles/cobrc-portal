Description of the Bird

vireo-type bill; no wing bars; white crescents below eyes; black eye line with white above and some contrast with crown above that, although contrast not as sharp as shown in Sibley (2000/2011); flight feathers darker than wing coverts; dark lore; yellow throat, white belly, yellow undertail coverts
Behaviors: The bird was moving around, presumably feeding, in a grove of black locust trees.
Call: not singing; also don't recall hearing call
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

distinguished from warbling vireo by yellow throat, white belly, yellow undertail coverts, and short tail

Resources Used

Sibley Guide to Birds (2000/2011) after the sighting, discussion with Steve Mlodinow after the sighting

Previous Experience

I had not seen a Philadelphia vireo before, but I was familiar with several other vireos from birding during the previous 4 months.
