Description of the Bird

Small short tailed Vireo , short stubby dark bill with the vireo hook ,dark eye line extending through lores , gray cap changing to gray green on nape and upper back , white eyebrow , yellow throat and vent areas , whitish belly . dark greenish olive wings , no wing bars .
Behaviors: Actively feeding in Russian Olives .
Call: None heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

WAVI doesn't have a yellow throat or a dark line through eye and lores. REVI doesn't have a yellow throat and has a gray crown with a white supercilium bounded by two dark stripes.
