Description of the Bird

The song got my attention and I was expecting to see a Red-eye Vireo but instead it was a chunky Warbler Vireo looking bird but with a yellow throat and yellowish wash around the vent area. Uniform gray brown back and wings , no wing bars , darker gray cap , whitish pale supercilium with a black line through the eye to the base of the bill.In comparison to an adjacent Warbling Vireo looked chunky and shorter tailed.
Behaviors: Feeding in willow and cottonwoods about 20 feet up , and singing the Red-eyed Vireo type song.
Call: Very much like Red-eyed Vireo , perhaps a little quieter and longer intervals between phrases.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

With the song only other possibility was Red-eyed and that does not have a yellow throat or a gray back and is a larger bird with longer tail.
