Description of the Bird

Medium sized warbler with rusty cap, brownish streaking on chest, yellow vent coming up onto lower belly. Pale supercilium and dark transocular line. Wagged tail most of time.
Behaviors: see above; fed on ground
Call: not heard
Plumage: alternate

Similar Species Discussion

No other warbler with rusty cap, yellow vent, face pattern as described. Thin warbler like bill eliminates sparrows as well
