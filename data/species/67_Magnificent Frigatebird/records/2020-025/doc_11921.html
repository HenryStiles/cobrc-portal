Description of the Bird

Large black and white  bird soaring over CC res. M shaped wing silhouette pattern and long, deeply forked tail. Long and big bill, hooked on the end. All black head, white breast and 1/2 belly with white spilling into the 'armpits', pale feet. Rest of the underside was black. Top of bird was black except for a pale patch from the wrist extending toward the primaries.

Similar Species Discussion

Greater Frigatebird - the white extends into the throat and not into the 'armpits'.
