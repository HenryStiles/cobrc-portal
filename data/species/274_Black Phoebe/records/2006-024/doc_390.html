Description of the Bird

Grabbed our books and determined that this little black bird matched the Black Phoebe description. Black body with white belly - sharp black bill. Exibiting flycatching activity.

We were a little puzzled by his head - looks somewhat like he had a crest - it appeared from a distance like it was partially missing - we thought perhaps he was "clenching" it due to the cold. We didn't want to traumatize him any longer, so we proceeded on our way.
Behaviors: Originally noticed the black Pheobe by it's flycatching behavior from a branch about 2 ft above stream. It proceeded to fly futher up the stream and landed on another branch and once again demonstrated the flycathing movement on and off the branch.
Call: wasn't singing
Plumage: black with white belly
