Description of the Bird

Black head, back wings, breast, and tail. White belly and undertail coverts. Black eye with head crest visible at times. Black beak and black legs. Size = 6 inches.
Behaviors: The phoebe was making frequent short darting flights to catch insects and then returning to the branches it has just left. It emitted high pitched squeaks at frequent intervals.
Call: High pitched "squeaks" at frequent intervals
Plumage: Black head, back, wings, white belly
