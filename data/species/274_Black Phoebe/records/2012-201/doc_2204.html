Description of the Bird

Black head and chest, white underbelly, brown shoulders with golden highlights in wings. About 6-7 inches long.
Behaviors: Bird was perched on willow branch overhanging Rio Grande River in Rio Grande County. Bird frequently flew out over the river to catch insects, then returned to same perch.
Call: None heard
Plumage: Juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

The bird exhibited flycatcher behavior by flying up into the air to catch insects, then returning to its perch. However, the field marks were unlike any other flycatchers we have identified in the San Luis Valley. After studying the flycatcher category in our field guide, we determined it was a Black Phoebe.
