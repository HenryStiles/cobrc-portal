Habitat

Description of the Bird

A sayornis flycatcher nearly all black, with a white belly and undertail coverts
Behaviors: flycatching underneath a bridge, picking insects from old Cliff Swallow nests
Call: tseew call given several times
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Says and Eastern Phoebe, neither are completely black with a white belly and undertail coverts
