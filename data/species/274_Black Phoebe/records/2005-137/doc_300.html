Description of the Bird

The flycatcher with black head, chest, and back, with white belly and under tail coverts. The bird pumped the tail often.
Behaviors: Bill McGurn and I found the bird first on 11 December 2005 along the Arkansas River, below Pueblo Reservoir dam, near Valco Ponds. The bird mostly stayed near the river picking insects from the water.
Call: A loud pip loud, similar to Swamp Sparrow.
Plumage: adult
