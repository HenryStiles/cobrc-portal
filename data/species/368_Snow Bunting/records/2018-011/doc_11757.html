Description of the Bird

Bird was feeding along road.   White wing patch.  No streaking on sides (as in Lapland Longspur). Rusty patch around ear. Dark back. Orange bill.

Similar Species Discussion

Lapland Longspur has streaking along sides and no white wing patch.

Resources Used

Sibley field guide, Cornell's All About Birds web site and I posted pictures on What's This Bird Facebook page to confirm.  James Muller and Cole Wild confirmed ID as Snow Bunting.

Previous Experience

None with this species. Have seen Lapland Longspurs near Pueblo Reservoir.
