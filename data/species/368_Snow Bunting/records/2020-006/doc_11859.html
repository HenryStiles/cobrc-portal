Description of the Bird

Seen about 200 feet west of I-70 on the Clear Creek Trail (39°46'23.6"N 105°08'43.8"W) on rocks in shallow river with no ice, hopping rapidly from rock to rock near or at shoreline.

- white neck, chest and body

- mixed white, rust, brown on wings

- light head with darker area at and behind eye

- black legs

- size of a large sparrow/bunting
