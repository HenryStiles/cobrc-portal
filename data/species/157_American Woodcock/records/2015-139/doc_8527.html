Description of the Bird

Squat, round bird with somewhat triangular head (from side); large eye on side towards back of head, long pink bill fading to a black-ish tip.  Buffy, slightly scalloped breast; alternating grey and black/brown "stripes" on back extending up to crown.  Pink legs.


Was walking along creek in the peculiar bobbing style particular to this species.  Also rested in sunlight with bill tucked. 

Similar Species Discussion

The only species I can think of that comes close to a Woodcock would be a Snipe species, with the Wilson's Snipe being the most likely in this area.  The Snipe has a similar but different body shape (not as blocky as the Woodcock); the striping on the snipe's back has thin white stripes, not wide grey stripes; and the snipe does not have the buffy belly.  The snipe also does not exhibit that funky, full-body bob that the Wookcock uses - the snipe has more of a head-bob.
