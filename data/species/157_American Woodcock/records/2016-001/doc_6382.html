Description of the Bird

Woodcock was first seen on the edge of the stream in grassy brush. Fairly small, with beak tucked into feathers to rest. Buffy orange overall, with some gray and dark brown patterns. Pink beak and brown block shaped battern from crown to nape. Dark brown eyeline and dark brown stripe below eye. Gray surrounding nape. 

Similar Species Discussion

Wilson's Snipe was ruled out quickly because of no buffy orange coloring and no dark brown stripes on crown and nape. Ground birds like doves were ruled out quickly because of this bird's thick, pink beak and elaborate patterns.
