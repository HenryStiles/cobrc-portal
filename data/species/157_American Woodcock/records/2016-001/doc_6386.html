Description of the Bird


Seen by MOB 100 yards up River from ranger house. The pink legs, bands on head and buffy underparts say Woodcock since the snipes have green legs, lengthwise head stripes and white underparts. I have a nice iPhone video of the bird bobbing and rocking as it feeds along the stream bed
