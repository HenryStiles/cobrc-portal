Description of the Bird

**Stakeout. One individual found on 1 January, which was presumed to be the same individual that was here last winter. Then a couple day ago another was found. We only found one individual, but with all the other people around we didn't spend more than a minute looking for the other bird.

The bird fed in shallow stream the entire time we were there, often bouncing slowly back in forth in the distinctive, odd, woodcock manner. I'm not sure how to age birds this late in the season. Did not see any obviously retained juvenile wing coverts (juv coverts and tertials would have buff tips, with dark subterminal bars).

Photos show bird well.
