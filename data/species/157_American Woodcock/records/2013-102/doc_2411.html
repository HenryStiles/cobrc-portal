Description of the Bird

I was walking east along a dirt road, west of the Valco Ponds parking lot, when a bird flushed out off the ground and heading north thru the trees and out of sight. I probably only saw the bird for 10 seconds, as it flew. The bird had a long bill, buffy orange color underneath, it was plump looking. The back seemed more gray in color, not brown like snipes, the wings were brown, without any stripes. I wasn't expecting this bird at all, though the sounds the bird was making, plus the odd plump shape, and colors, elimnate all other possiblities. I remember looking at the ground where the bird flushed up, and there were dead leaves, below the cottonwoods.
Behaviors: Flushed up from the ground, flying north, probably went across the river into Rock Canyon. I never could find the bird again.
Call: In flight, making loud twittering sounds with its wings.
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Snipes would be the only other bird, that is this shape. This bird was more plump looking than snipe, also lack of white stripes in the wings, and the sound was wrong for snipe.
