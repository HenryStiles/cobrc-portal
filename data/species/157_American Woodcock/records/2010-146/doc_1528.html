Description of the Bird

Extremely large bill with large bulging eyes placed high on head. Leaflike camouflage pattern, broadly barred crown. Black nape crossed by pale lines. Short tail.
Behaviors: Wandered around in the yard looking for food in the grass with his long beak. When flushed, rose staight up and then took off quickly.
Call: unknown
Plumage: Brown leaflike camouflage pattern

Similar Species Discussion

Asked a person who we know is a birdwatcher and sent her pictures. She reviewed the pictures and said it looked like an American Woodcock. I went to website pictures and they look exactly like the bird we photographed.
