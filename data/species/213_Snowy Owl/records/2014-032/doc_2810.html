Description of the Bird

Large predominantly white owl sitting atop a power pole. 

Face all white with two forward-facing glowing yellow eyes. 

Top of broad head feathered more in brown. 

Back of bird white with tips of feathers edged in brown.

Photos obtained by Joey Kellner - assuming he will also submit a report.

Similar Species Discussion

The only pale owl would be a BAOW - this bird had an all white face and lacked the heart-shaped face pattern.  Back was too white for BAOW.

Other non-owl raptors which should be considered would all lack the forward facing eyes.

Resources Used

Prior experience.  SNOW is a pretty unmistakable bird.
