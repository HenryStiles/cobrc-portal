Description of the Bird

A mostly white fairly large owl sitting on the ground.

The round flat face was all bright white; eyes yellow; the chest and back were bright white and barred or patterned heavily with brown; the top of the head reminded this viewer of a bad combover of brown fuzz.
Behaviors: It sat on the ground in a field of corn or similar plant stubble. It looked around swiveling the head almost 360 degrees total. It otherwise did not move.
Call: N/A
Plumage: immature

Similar Species Discussion

This was obviously an owl. All but Barn Owl can be eliminated by the bright white face and/or size. Barn Owl is eliminated due to the brown patterning and the non-heartlike face.
