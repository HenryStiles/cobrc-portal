Description of the Bird

Large, squat white bird with horizontal black barring on back and front, white face and black cap, yellow irises and black bill. Very “owl-like” round face, but without any ear-tufts.
Behaviors: This bird sat on the ground on a low ridge over a hollow drainage in a barren winter wheat field. It sat pretty much motionless except to rotate its head mainly to look at the three of us observing on a road a couple hundred yards away.
Call: N.A.
Plumage: 1st year
