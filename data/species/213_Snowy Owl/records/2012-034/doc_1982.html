Description of the Bird

Large, nearly all-white yellow-eyed owl with heavily feathered white feet and very light black tipping on crown & back feathers.
Behaviors: Roosting on a post bordering a horse pasture.
Call: not heard
Plumage: Possible Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Albinistic or leucistic large owls would lack black tipping on feathers & have structural differences such as long ear tufts in Great Horned Owl. Head turning and blinking separates this from a white inanimate object.
