Species, Date, Time and Location Information

Species:  Snowy Owl
First Date/Time:  2/4/2012
Last Date/Time: 
Duration (total time in view):  30 minutes
County:  Bent
Specific Location:  1/2 mile south of Hwy 50 on County Road 26.5 on the west side of the road
Number:  1
Age:  Unknown
Sex:  Female
Plumage:  Other/Unknown

Habitat

-rural agriculture crop land
-reservoir near
-windbreak

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  10x42 binoculars
Distance:  about 600-700 feet (about 2 city blocks)
Light:  good light

Description of the Bird

-large white owl
-smallish round head
-some darker markings on top of head
-face white
-brownish horizontal barring across breast
Behaviors: -sat still for the entire time we observed
-seemed to be sleepy
Call: was silent
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

-not any species very similar to a large white owl
-a very light colored Great Horned Owl has a much larger head and eyes, and also has ear tufts

I observed another Snowy Owl in Prowers County just a week prior at a much closer range so was familiar with the species
