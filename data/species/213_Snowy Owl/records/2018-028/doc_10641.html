Description of the Bird

Big whitish owl with many black bands on individual feathers. Perched both days and looked in all directions including up (but not down into water). Flattened slightly when BAEA flew overhead. 

Similar Species Discussion

Barn Owl - has rufous feathers and heart-shaped head.
