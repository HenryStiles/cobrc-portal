Description of the Bird

The bird was a large whitish owl, with faint darker markings on the underparts and crown, and extensive blackish barring on the wings. It had clear yellow eyes and a small blackish bill.
Behaviors: perched on haybale
Call: none heard
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

nothing else is at all similar; it was too large and too heavily marked to be a Barn Owl and too large and whitish to be anything else
