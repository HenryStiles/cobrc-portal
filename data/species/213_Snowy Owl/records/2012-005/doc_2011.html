Description of the Bird

The owl had a rounded set in face and did not have any ear tufts.Its body was quite stocky and plump. Its tail was medium length. Its entire body was almost pure white. It was about the size of a raven.
Behaviors: At first it was sitting on the ground beside a green marker post. This was in the middle of a very large field with short stubble and grass. Later it flew to the top of the post and just scanned all its surroundings. Overall it was not an active bird.
Call: The bird was too far away for us to hear any noise it might have made.
Plumage: I think this means age. Adult.
