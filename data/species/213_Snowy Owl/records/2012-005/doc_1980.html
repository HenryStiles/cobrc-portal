Description of the Bird

Large, squat, mostly white owl with faint black streaking on breast, belly, wings, and crown. The face and upper chest were pure white. The white on the upper chest formed a "V" bordered by the black streaking on the breast and belly. The owl had large, yellow eyes, and a rounded head with no ear tufts.
Behaviors: Perched on the ground in a crop field the entire time.
Call: None heard.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Barn Owl ruled out by pure white body with black streaking and lack of a facial disk. Albino Great Horned Owl would show ear tufts.
