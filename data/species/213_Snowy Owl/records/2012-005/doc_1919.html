Description of the Bird

Large white owl on ground in field. Back, median (and lesser?) wing coverts moderately checkered in black. Greater coverts and tertials lightly marked with black. Primaries white. nape, hindneck, crown appeared mostly white, but upper and outer parts of facial disks lightly marked, setting off white facial disks. Sides white. Remainder of underparts not seen. Eyes yellow.
Behaviors: Sitting quietly on ground, awake, occasionally swiveling head
Call: not heard
Plumage: probably adult

Similar Species Discussion

None. Bird was not stuffed/decoy, as it moved. Not an albino, due to pattern of dark marking, c/w known Snowy Owl appearance
