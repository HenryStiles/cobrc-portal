Description of the Bird

Found by Lorraine Lanning earlier on 2/20/2022. Refound by Will Anderson just before I arrived. It was perched on a wooden fencepost about 1/4 mile away when I arrived, but flew farther away just as I was getting my scope out of the car. It spent the rest of the time sitting on another wooden fencepost about 1/2 mile away. Occasionally preening. Stretched its neck out a couple of times. Also stared intently at possible movement to its right a couple of times.

Large white owl with dense black bars all over except a completely white face and black dots on the crown.

Similar Species Discussion

The only other species I considered was a very pale Ferruginous Hawk, but the extensive barring all over and the completely white rounder face eliminated that. Also this was a much bulkier bird.
