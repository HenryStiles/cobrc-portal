Description of the Bird

Large owl that was basally white with extensive black streaking all over the body except for its face, which was pure white. Head was rounded and smooth, lacking ear tufts. I first saw the bird flying low over the ground. It had a butterfly-like wingbeat which was deep and slow.
Behaviors: First observed in flight as described above, then perched on a bit of ridge between crop fields.
Call: None heard.
Plumage: Juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Size, coloration, and behavior of being active during day rule out all other possible species.
