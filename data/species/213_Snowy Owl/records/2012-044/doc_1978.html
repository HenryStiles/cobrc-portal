Description of the Bird

The bird was a large, whitish owl with extensive blackish barring everywhere except for the face. The eyes were bright yellow and there were no visible ear tufts.
Behaviors: perched on what I believe was a fence to protect a natural gas structure
Call: None heard
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

No other species is particularly similar at all; it was much too large and too-heavily marked to be a Barn Owl and too large and too whitish to be anything else
