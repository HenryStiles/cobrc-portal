Light:  We were looking north most of the time, the sun was towards the east.

Description of the Bird

A very large and round bird sitting on the ground with a very round head and a fairly short tail. It was mostly all white. It had quite a few small brown barring on the back and even a little on the back. It had feathers going all the way down the legs onto the feet. It had a small black bill and yellow eyes. In flight it had very rounded wings.
Behaviors: It was sitting on the ground for a while, it then flew about 200 yards and then landed on the ground again. It then flew again and landed again on the ground.
Call: Never heard it
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

The large size and mostly white body eliminates everything. The closest things to it is a pale Great Horned Owl and a white phase Gyrfalcon. The short tail and yellow eyes and also the weight of the bird eliminate Gyrfalcon. The white with a little bit of brown barring eliminates Great Horned Owl, also the habitat would eliminate Great Horned Owl.
