Habitat

prairie grassland, farmland

Description of the Bird

about 2 feet tall and slender looking, nearly all white with dark patterns on wings and back, bright yellow eyes and big furry feet
Behaviors: Sitting on a post off the highway looking around, sat for a few minutes then flew off towards the north
Call: none
Plumage: nearly all white

Similar Species Discussion

The color of the owl was a dead give away and being that close gave you great views for identifying, and I had seen 2 other Snowy Owls earlier in the year
