Description of the Bird

Large all white owl , with black barring on lower chest , light black spotting on wing coverts and a few black spots arranged in a horizontal line on top of head.In flight underwings all white.
Behaviors: Resting on ground , often with eyes closed and made one brief flight of about 50 yards before settling on ground.
Call: None
Plumage: adult female or imm male

Similar Species Discussion

Unique , no other owl has this appearance. An albino Great Horned Owl would not show the black barring or spotting . From the amount of barring and spotting I think this was an immature male.
