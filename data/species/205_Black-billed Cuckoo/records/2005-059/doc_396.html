Description of the Bird

Slender cuckoo with long dark bill. Upper mandible black, lower mandible slightly more gray in color. Upperparts grayish brown, slightly olive in good light. Wings plain grayish brown above. Dark eye with red orbital ring. Underparts white. Long tail, plain grayish brown above, more gray below. Rectrices tipped white creating a gray and white pattern on underside of tail.
Behaviors: The bird would stay near the ground in cover and then fly up to a low perch where it could, at times, be easily seen. On occasion it flew to other nearby, low perches.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow-billed Cuckoo has yellow lower mandible, yellowish orbital ring, rusty patch on primaries and more strikingly patterned undertail.
