Description of the Bird

Three key field marks very visible -- all black bill, red orbital around eye, small white tips on black tail feathers
Behaviors: Moved every minute or so in bushes and trees, usually staying 5 to 8 feet off the ground. Dropped to the ground once to pick up an insect.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow-billed Cuckoo -- This was not a Yellow-billed Cuckoo as it had an all-black bill, a red rather than yellow orbital, and the tail pattern of a black-billed bird.
