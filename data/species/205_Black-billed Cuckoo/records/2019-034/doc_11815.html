Description of the Bird

Clearly seen cuckoo with long tail, white below, brown above, red eye and completely black bill.
Feeding in willows along creek.
Bird seen at creek crossing right at bottom of hill on trail down from parking lot.
lt called once, not singing.

Similar Species Discussion

I have seen many cuckoo species around the world. The long tail, brown back, white belly could not be confused with other families.
Yellow-billed cuckoo would not have all-black bill.
