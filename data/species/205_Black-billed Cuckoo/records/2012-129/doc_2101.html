Description of the Bird

Bill Maynard yelled "cuckoo" as this bird flew into a cottonwood tree. The bill was mostly dark. I took a couple of photos, one perched and one flying by. See photos.
Behaviors: Flew in from the weeds into the cottonwood tree, then flew off, not to be seen again.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile
