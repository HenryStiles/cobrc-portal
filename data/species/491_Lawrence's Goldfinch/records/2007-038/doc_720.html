Description of the Bird

Small goldfinch with black mask on face and forehead with black extending onto throat. Small conical shaped pink bill. Dark eye. Rest of head and nape gray. Gray back with some yellow tinting. Yellow breast patch, yellow on rump. Wings extensively yellow with some black. Black tail.
Behaviors: Bird came into a thistle feeder a couple of times.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult alternate
