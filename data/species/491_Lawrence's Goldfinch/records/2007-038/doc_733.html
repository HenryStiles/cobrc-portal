Description of the Bird

Got good pictures. Really unmistakable. Male with the black face, yellow belly patch, yellow on wings.
Behaviors: Eating on the thistle feeder made for great photo opps. LEGO and AMGO were also on the feeders for easy comparison.
Call: not heard by me.
Plumage: breeding
