Description of the Bird

Same bird observed all summer (CBRC #2007-38), many observers and photos, no obvious plumage changes from adult male breeding plumage noted two months earlier - black face, yellow wing bars and breast, otherwise various shades of unstreaked gray quite unlike LEGO, AMGO, or PISI, in otherwords no obvious molting during the period.  Flight calls similar to LEGO, and the bird was still actively pair-bonding with a female LEGO (billing/mandibulation behavior, following flights).  Foraged both at thistle (niger) feeders and on Berlandiera lyrata (we had planted) that the LEGO foraged on, a few feet below our patio.

Similar Species Discussion

LEGO, AMGO, and PISI adult male plumages were all quite different than this bird, see above
