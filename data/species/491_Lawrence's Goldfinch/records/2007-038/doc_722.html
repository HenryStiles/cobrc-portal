Description of the Bird

adult male plumaged LAGO with black face (chin important), pale gray back and head, flanks, yellow chest and wing bars, not a color/marking combo exhibited by any of the other carduelis finches.
Behaviors: goldfinch flight pattern, feeding preference for niger thistle and Berlandiera lyrata similar to LEGO, interacts with LEGO. AMGO has not been present at same time as LAGO to myknowledge so i haven't seen this particular interaction.
Call: flight calls somewhat like LEGO, but separable from LEGO
Plumage: adult male breeding plumage

Similar Species Discussion

many people will have photos!
LEGO, AMGO - both have different arrangements of color markings, no black face or pale gray back and flanks.
Behavior note - the last few observations are interesting: LAGO has arrived with female LEGO, chased off male LEGOs, and departed with female LEGO. Noted yesterday by the Prathers and this morning by me.
