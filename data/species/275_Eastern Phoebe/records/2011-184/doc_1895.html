Description of the Bird

Flycatcher with dark brown rounded head , dark tail , pale underparts with light gray wash on upper breast , no wing bars , no eye ring , wagged its tail occasionally .
Behaviors: Spent a lot of time investigating the eves of a derilict ranch building or perched on a fencepost and sallying out to catch insects.
Call: Hard chip. Did not hear the classic "phoebe call "
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

My expectation in this habitat would have been Say's Phoebe but bird did not have the buffy rufous belly and completely gray upper breast for this species and call heard was characteristic of Eastern or Black Phoebe. Not Black Phoebe because not black .Habitat and behaviour not typical for Western Wood Pewee which has narrow wing bars , grayish vest and different call.
