Description of the Bird

A large, dark, stocky sparrow; obviously larger and darker than Song Sparrows seen in the same area. Appeared to have all chocolate brown upperparts in shade with heavy triangular spots coalesing into lines and a spot accross breast. Heavy yellow bill, red legs and darker red feet.  In direct light of setting sun the darkest areas were dark red-brown with grey supercillium and shoulder. The photos taken in this light of the setting sun show enhanced red on the bird and other things in photo. No vocalations were heard.

Similar Species Discussion

Larger and darker than other sparrows that occur in area.

Resources Used

The Sibley Guide to Birds; The Sparrows of the United States and Canada, Rising and Beadle; Fox Sparrow Facebook Group 

Previous Experience

No experience with Sooty Fox Sparrows. I've seen numerous Red and Slate-colored Fox Sparrows. I am very familiar with Song Sparrows and other regular species in this area.
