Description of the Bird

Crisp white throat with very well defined borders, yellow lores, streaked mantle, gray washed breast and auriculars, white supercilium, bright rufous wings with white wing "bars".
Behaviors: Eating seeds on ground underneath a pair of tube feeders. Was around for 15 minutes. Joined in with a flock of Juncos that had been at the feeders for over an hour before it arrived.
Call: None observed
Plumage: adult
