Description of the Bird

Calling woodpecker with gray upper forehead and crown. Sides of head and neck grayish-white. Black medium length pointed bill. Back of neck red. Back barred black and white. Uppertail-coverts white. Slight orange color to chin and just above bill. Low center of belly with slight reddish patch. Rest of underparts grayish white. Legs grayish.
Behaviors: This bird was actively foraging and flying between mature trees in the campground. Calling frequently.
Call: Heard. As described in National Geo.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Golden-fronted Woodpecker has a different call and the nape is golden orange rather than red.
