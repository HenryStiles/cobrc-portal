Description of the Bird

I was birding with Kara Carragher and others, and Kara spotted a thrush, and asked me to look at. So, I did, and the bird had gray cheeks (which eliminated Swainson's Thrush), brownish back (which eliminated Veery, and brownish tail (not reddish which eliminated Hermit Thrush. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying low.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Hermit Thrush - have reddish tails, not gray/brown
Swainson's Thrush - buffy eye ring, not gray/brown
Veery - more reddish back, also brighter white underparts
