Description of the Bird

cheek gray, partial eyering, auricular streaked whitish, throat white with blackish streaks, upperparts grayish-olive, flanks grayish with slight olive tinge, breast white with blackish triangular spots, belly white, bill blackish with base of mandible bright yellow

wing formula: P9>P6, P8-P1 >30mm, P6 slightly emarginate
 

Similar Species Discussion

Swainson's Thrush has buffy face and distinct buffy spectacles.  P6 is not emarginate.
Hermit Thrush has brown back contrasting distinctly with rufous tail.  Wing formula: P9<P6.

Resources Used

Previous Experience

I banded one Gray-cheeked Thrush in May 2012.  I have banded many Swainson's and Hermit Thrushes over the past twelve years.
