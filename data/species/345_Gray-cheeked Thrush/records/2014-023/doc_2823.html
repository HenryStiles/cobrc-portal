Description of the Bird

cheek gray with thin white streaks, partial eyering, throat white with dark streaks, upperparts grayish-olive, breast white with blackish triangular spots, belly white 
 
wing formula: P9>P6, P6 slightly emarginate, P8-P1>30mm
 

Similar Species Discussion

Swainson's Thrush has obvious buffy face and distinct buffy spectacles.
Hermit Thrush has brown back contrasting with rufous tail.
