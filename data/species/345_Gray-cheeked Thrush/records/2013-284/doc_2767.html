Description of the Bird

very gray cheek - no eyering or rufous on tail - the other observers also got great looks
also had lighter gray oblong patch around eye

-the bird was seen on May 1, May 2, and May 11 - I reported as one bird

Similar Species Discussion

-Hermit Thrush was eliminated as there was no rufous tail and the face was very gray
-flanks were olive grayish
