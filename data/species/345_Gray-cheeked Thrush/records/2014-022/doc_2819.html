Description of the Bird

cheek gray with thin white streaks, no eyering, throat white with dark streaks, bill blackish with base of mandible bright yellow, upperparts grayish olive, breast white with dark triangular spots, flanks brownish gray, belly white

wing formula: P9>P6, P8-P1>30mm, P6 slightly emarginate

Similar Species Discussion

SWTH has obvious buffy face and distinct buffy spectacles.

HETH has brown back contrasting with rufous tail; P9<P6.
