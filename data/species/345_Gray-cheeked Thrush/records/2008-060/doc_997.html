Description of the Bird

A decent-sized Catharus Thrush. Brownish-olive on the back. This color continued all the way down the tail. The face was very plain gray with just a hint of a gray eyering. The breast was grayish-white with heavy blackish spotting.
Behaviors: none noted, bird was handheld
Call: none noted
Plumage: Definitive adult

Similar Species Discussion

Hermti Thrush and Veery both have grayish faces. This bird did not have a brighter rufous tail eliminating Hermit Thrush. This bird was too olive on the back for Veery, eliminating that possiblity
