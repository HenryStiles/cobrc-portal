Description of the Bird

I was walking with my scope and noticed a large white wading bird in flight, with black wing tips, a curved reddish bill, and reddish legs going out beyond the tail. I quickly thought it was an adult White Ibis. The bird landed in a tree, and quickly photographed the bird.
Behaviors: The bird was standing in trees, and seen if flight.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
