Description of the Bird

Medium sized wading bird that was mostly white.  Bright red, long downcurving bill, and bright red, quite long legs and feet.  Small black p[atches at the end of the wings. Light colored eye, but with a dark pupil.  See attached photograph taken by Mark Chavez a little later.

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing like this species when it's in adult plumage.

Resources Used

Quick look at the Sibley Field Guide, mainly to see where the species usually resides.

Previous Experience
