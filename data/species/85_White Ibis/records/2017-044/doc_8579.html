Description of the Bird

Standing and walking on small bare, gravel mound surrounded by shallow water and algae.  Several killdeer on mound as well.

Similar Species Discussion

Snowy egrets were also abundant at the site.  This bird stood out due to it large decurved bill, birght red skin patch on face and white plumage (slightily darker than the egrets).  I could not really see the leg color..

Resources Used

Sibley, eBird sightings using BirdsEye. The bird is pretty unique and easily identified by appearance.

Previous Experience

I've seen White Ibis in south Texas and White-faced Ibis through out the west.  I'm familiar with egrets and small herons.
