Description of the Bird

Nice Healthy White Ibis, pink/reddish legs, curved bill and pink/reddish face, all white except for black wingtips.  White Ibis was wading in shallow water feeding and often moved to small sandy island on east side of the middle pond.  Avocets harassed the bird and it flew over to the south west corner of the pond closer to the parking lot area.  Another Avocet scared the bird off and it flew into the NW corner of the pond closer to the paved sidewalk.  Bird feed in the shallows for about 20 minutes until a Snowy Egret harassed the bird again and it flew of to another pond area NE.  Was not seen again and did no return before 645pm.

Similar Species Discussion

Several Snowy Egrets abounded.  I have seen several White Ibis in Florida.
