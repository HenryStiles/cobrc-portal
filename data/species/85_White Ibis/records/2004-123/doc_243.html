Description of the Bird

The bird was a large white bird, with small black corners to the tips of the wings, with bright red curved downward bill, and long red legs extending back from the tail.
Behaviors: Ruth Hulsey and I were talking, when I noticed a bird flying by east of me. The bird was an adult White Ibis. It flew from north to south, then I ran west along the bike path to see it more, when I saw the bird again for a few seconds, it was heading east toward Valco Ponds. I never saw the bird again.
