Habitat

It was in a shallow pond with a lot of grasses growing up out of the water.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Swarovski spotting scope
Distance:  300 feet
Light:  The sun was behind and to the west of the bird, so it made for pretty poor lighting.

Description of the Bird

It was an all white bird with red legs, face, and bill. The bill was half red, closer to the face, and half black, farther from the face.
Behaviors: It was in a shallow pond. It was probing in the pond trying to find food.
Call: Didn't hear it
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Wood Stork - It is a bigger bird, without feathers on the head, dark legs, and more black in the wings.
Egrets - They don't have a long or curved bill and they also don't have red legs or bill.
White-faced and Glossy Ibis - The are Dark brown birds with greenish wings. They also don't have bright red legs or bill.
Scarlet Ibis - It is an entirely bright red bird.
