Description of the Bird

Adult. Foraging and standing around at waters edge, moving out of view or flying away after we looked at it for over 30 min, but we did not notice when it left view or where it went. Similar standing size to SNEG, but heftier bulk, clearly smaller than the adjacent GREG. All white body and wings (could not discern black wing tips generally. Red or reddish legs, bright red face and bill, darkening on the distal portion. Bill decurved in ibis fashion, maybe even more pronounced curve than WFIB. Foraging with bill in water searching for things in manner like WFIB, but mostly it was just standing there as we watched.
