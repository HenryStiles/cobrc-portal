Habitat

We have close to 100 Turkey vultures roosting here in our windbreak. In over 50 years, this is the first time I have seen a Black Vulture.

Description of the Bird

I had been watching the Turkey Vultures leaving the roosts and soaring on the wind. The Black Vulture circled over me several times before it sailed off. Compared with the Turkey Vultures, it had a short, square tail. It was overall more black with only the primary feathers on it's wing a light color. It's wings appeared broader and it flew with them more level something like an eagle. I saw it very clearly as it circled not far above me.

Previous Experience

This was my first sighting of a Black Vulture but Turkey Vultures have roosted close to our house for many years. I watch them come in in the evening and hang out in the morning before they soar off for the day.
