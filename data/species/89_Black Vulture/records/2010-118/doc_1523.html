Description of the Bird

See attached txt and rtf document of the Colorado Breeding Bird Atlas II - Verification Form. I saw this bird on my BBA block SE of Limon. All black plumage with no brownish and black head, grey legs and bill shape and color all say BLVU and not TUVL. Also attached are 2 pictures.
Behaviors: The quick wing beats and walking on the tree branch also supported BLVU.
Call: None
Plumage: Not sure

Similar Species Discussion

Please see my attached report to the BBA on my sighting. After orginally thinking it was a juv TUVU after reviewing the photos I knew it was a BLVU bacause of the short tail, color of legs (gray), color of body (black not brown), shape of bill like a BLVU.
