Description of the Bird

A resident (Sydney Moon) in Pueblo West called the Pueblo Nature and Raptor Center on November 9th, because they had a injured vulture in their north Pueblo West neiborhood.  Margie Joy (Pueblo West birder, who volunteers to help with injured birds, and gets them to the Raptor Center), was called to go help some other Raptor Center people, to get the bird and get it to the raptor center.  Once they found the bird, Margie realized it was a Black Vulture!  Diana Miller at the Pueblo Nature and Raptor Center took this photo, at the Nature Center once they got it there.  The bird was present for four days, November 6-9, 2023.  Margie informed me, the bird wasn't in good health, and died on November 19th at the Pueblo Raptor Center.
