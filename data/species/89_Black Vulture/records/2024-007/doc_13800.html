Similar Species Discussion

Turkey Vulture was considered but ruled that out in favor of a Black Vulture for the following reasons:
- body was all black and lacked the brownish coloration of Turkey Vultures
- tail was short and ended relatively close to body compared to the longer tail seen on Turkey Vultures
- dark grey head instead of a red head found on Turkey Vultures
- bill was long and slender and grey/brown. Size and coloration did not match Turkey Vulture.
- ruled out juvenile Turkey Vulture because it is too early in the year for juvenile Turkey Vultures to exist
