Description of the Bird

Bird was seen with Kathy Kay from very close distance. It was very un-fazed by our presence and didn't even move when cars and trucks drove close by. Large black raptor, black bill and briefly from a deer carcass at the side of the road up to a gate and then about 10 feet to a pile of wooden planks.bare black skin around the head and bill. Short tail, but the tail feathers looked very worn. Only seen in flight
