Description of the Bird

Bird first found by Joelle Marier. Then confirmed by Doug Clark. Then as I heard about it, I went up to document and photograph it. Bird was the size of a red-tailed hawk. It was black plumaged over most of its body. It had a bare black head with pronounced raptor like decurved bill. The primaries of the bird are white shafted and lighter than nearby secondaries. Tail, short, squarish. 

 

First day bird was perched on a fence. Second day, it was feeding on a dead deer and then flew up to the fencepost. When it flew could clearly see the white shafts of the primaries. 

 

The breast of this bird was devoid of feathers. I wondered if this bird was sick.  

Similar Species Discussion

Turkey vulture is similar but a bit larger. It will be mostly black, with reddish head. Wings will be gray on the trailing edges of primaries and secondaries. 

 

Previous Experience

+Just returned from Brazil in September and saw this species everywhere in Brazil and Argentina. Also saw it in Arizona June 2023.
