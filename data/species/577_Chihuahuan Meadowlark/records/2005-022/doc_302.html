Habitat

Seen in short grass prairie with yuccas and cactus. At Lesser Prairie-Chicken lek.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  20 to 60 zoom bausch and lomb spotting scope.
Distance:  40 meters.
Light:  sunny, excellent.

Description of the Bird

Meadowlark, singing and calling. Whiter face than Western Meadowlark.
Behaviors: Singing and calling on top of yuccas.
Call: singing and calling. Clear whistle song and "bzert" call note, typical of the species.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Western Meadowlark: song and call different.

Resources Used

none

Previous Experience

Seen Eastern Meadowlark at the same location in spring 2004. Seen and heard lots of Eastern Meadowlarks in the East and in New Mexico and Texas. Seen and heard Western Meadowlarks many times in Colorado and other western states.
