Description of the Bird

No calls.  Warbler with yellow crown lined by black, with black coming down from eye area towards throat. Black and white back and wings, chestnut sides, white below. Methodically working through the trees at top and mid level.  Continually moving but not so fast that you couldn't get good looks at it. Found by Kat Dunlavey.

Similar Species Discussion

THe breeding male plumage ruled out other warblers.
