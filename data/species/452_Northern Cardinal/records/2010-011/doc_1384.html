Description of the Bird

Bright red bird, with red crest, black throat, black above the bill, black going to the eyes. Black eyes. Red bill. Red belly, under parts, grayish red back and wings. Grayish black legs. See photos.
Behaviors: Seen in thick bushes.
Call: chink call, and some singing
Plumage: adult
