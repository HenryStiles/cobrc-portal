Description of the Bird

Thick red bill, black mask, red crest, reddish tail and wings, slightly smaller than robin, more slender.
Behaviors: Observed on the ground and in nearby trees. Often turned head to peer at something on the ground. Did not approach the sunflower seed feeder, though was observed eating on the ground.
Call: Did not hear vocalization
Plumage: Brown with red crest and dark mask

Similar Species Discussion

Cardinals are distinctive and there are no similar species. I grew up in Florida and they were frequent there.
