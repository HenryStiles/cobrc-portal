Description of the Bird

Typical male Cardinal...red, crest, black chin, etc. Attached photo should suffice, which is also available at this link:
http://www.flickr.com/photos/kirkh1/3540972210/in/set-72157631370267734
Behaviors: Calling, sitting up in top of cottonwood tree.
Call: Chips, as well as usual 2-tone song, as well as multiple notes. Not the trilling call.
Plumage: Fresh, bright red
