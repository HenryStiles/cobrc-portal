Description of the Bird

A female sapsucker on a pine tree. It had brown head, brown and white barring on the back, white rump, dark tail hanging down on the bark of the tree. The bird had dark eyes and bill. Brown chin, black breast and yellow on the belly and brown and white barring below the belly through the undertail coverts.
Behaviors: On Pine Tree in Lakeside Cemetery in Canon City.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion
