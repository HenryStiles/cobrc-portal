Description of the Bird

See attached photos by Yappert and Maynard.  Small, dark stub-tailed wren.  Dark rufous-brown head with paler reddish-buff supercilium.  Throat and breast cinnamon-brown.  Plain dark rufous-brown back.  Sides reddish-brown with blackish barring.  Exposed culmen of 9.8 mm indicates Pacific and eliminates Winter Wren (Pyle pg. 367).

Similar Species Discussion

Winter Wren eliminated, as it is overall paler, with more contrasting buffy throat and breast; browner, less rufous back and sides, and more patterned on back and flanks.  Also longer culmen.
