Description of the Bird

A dark, chocolate brown stub-tailed wren with uniform dark nape and very few white spots on wing.  The bird was calling with a dry tickin sound first heard by Dan.  We found the bird and the field marks substantiated PAWR as ID.  See photos, Hear audio recording.

Similar Species Discussion

Winter Wren is paler and sounds more like a Song Sparrow with it's calls.  Winter Wren typically has a whitish throat and a spotted nape.
