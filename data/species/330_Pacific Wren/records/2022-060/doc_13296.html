Description of the Bird

We were coming down the drainage and heard a bird calling and at first thought short-tailed wren....the chip chip...chip chip.  I had just heard Winter Wren calls at another location recetnly and thought they sounded similar but higher pitched and less "harsh".  I pulled up Merlin when we first heard it calling and it said Pacific.  Linday quickly spotted the bird calling from on a fallen log over the creek in some thick willows and I was able to get photos.  The bird was noticably rufous below than typical Winter Wren.
