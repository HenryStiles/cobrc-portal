Description of the Bird

One bird, I assume it was the male. took a fairly high point and was singing. The other two appeared to be foraging
All 3 were very dark brown and each had 2 or 3 white spots on wings. Very short tails, often held almost vertically

Similar Species Discussion

Considered House Wren, but these were darker and shorter tails
