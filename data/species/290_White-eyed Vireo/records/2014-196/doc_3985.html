Description of the Bird

Small yellowish-gray bird. Stark yellow spectacles and white iris, 2 white wing bars. Complicated song of nasaly whistles and chers. Gleaning activity in mid-story choke cherry.

Similar Species Discussion

The yellow-colored spectacles of the white-eyed vireo were distinctive for me. Plumbeous vireos are all gray with white spectacles. Cassin's vireos are not nearly as yellow-tummied as the white-eyed vireo and have white spectacles.

Resources Used

Previous Experience

I am quite familiar with plumbeous, warbling and Cassin's vireos--sight and song. I observed a white-eyed vireo at short distance with Bill Maynard at Chico Basin Ranch in early May.
