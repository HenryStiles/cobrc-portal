Description of the Bird

A rather husky warbler with proportionately short tail and big bill. Crown striped black and buff. "Lowest" stripe was a black transocular stripe. Back and wings olive colored. Underparts buffy, brightest on throat, chest, and vent
Behaviors: Skulking in dense leaves
Call: don't remember. Christian Nunes picked out vocalization as that of WE Warbler
Plumage: unknown
