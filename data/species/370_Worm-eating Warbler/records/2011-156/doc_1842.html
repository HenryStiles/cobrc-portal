Description of the Bird

I only got a brief look at the bird. The creamy orange/yellow underparts, with four black stripes on the head, one going through each eye. The bird had a olive green colored back and tail. The tail was short. The eyes were dark. That was all I saw.
Behaviors: In tangles low to the ground, near some other birds.
Call: Bird giving a high "zeet zeet" call that I've heard before. I think it was giving this when it was flying around.
