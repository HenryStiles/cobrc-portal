Description of the Bird

This was a bird collected from an avid bird feeder who observed the bird after it hit a window. It expired shortly after discovered. I was called and came to identify. No prior observation was noted.

Similar Species Discussion

No other west slope warbler has the striping on the crown. A remotely simular water thrush has a lighter breast and is streaked.
