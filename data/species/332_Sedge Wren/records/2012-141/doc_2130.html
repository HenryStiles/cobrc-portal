Description of the Bird

A small wren flushed in the sedges. Streaked crown, eye line not distinctive, like Marsh Wren, buffy overall looking wren, eliminate Marsh Wren. The bill was short and pale. Seen photos.
Behaviors: Bill Maynard and I were walking in the wet sedge habitat, hoping to kick up a Le Conte's Sparrow, instead we kicked up this wren.
Call: a soft call when it flew
Plumage: fall

Similar Species Discussion

Marsh Wren is reddish, with white eyeline, lacking a streak crown.
