Description of the Bird

A small secretive wren with bold black streaks on the back a buffy supercillium and whitish throat.
Behaviors: Fast moving and tending to stay hidden.
Call: The bird gave a distinct metallic chip note. Very different from any call note I know of.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Chip note rules out similar Marsh Wren. Excellent photos by others. Much paler than Marsh Wren and had a yellow bill. Barring on wings also distinctive.
