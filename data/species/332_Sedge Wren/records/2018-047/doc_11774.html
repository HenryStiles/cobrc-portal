Description of the Bird

Active bird, moving around within a small area, often calling and singing, and sometimes perching in visible locations.  Wren color and shape but with a relatively short bill for a wren.  Bright white streaks on back.  Pale streaks on crown.  Barred wings.  Distinctive call and song (recordings attached).

Similar Species Discussion

Tail longer than that of stub-tailed wrens (Winter and Pacific).
Back and crown more streaked than in Marsh and House Wrens.
Wings more barred than in Marsh Wren.
Distinctive call and song, eliminating other wren species.
