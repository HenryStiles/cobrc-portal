Description of the Bird

Ovenbird was heard (usual tea-CHER) and then found about 200 feet west of the little, low corral on play pen path, Dawson Butte Ranch. It was among other sightings of Plumbeous Vireos, Black-headed Grosbeaks, Western Bluebirds, Lesser Goldfinches, and a lone Western Tanager.
