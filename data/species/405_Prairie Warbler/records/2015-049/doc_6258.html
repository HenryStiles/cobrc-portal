Description of the Bird

Setaphaga warbler , basically yellow warbler with dark semicircle below eye and dark neck spot , yellow eyebrow , black streaking on sides of breast , rufous streaks on greenish olive back , white in outer tail feathers in flight . Low chip call heard . Feeding in Russian Olives close to the banding station .

Similar Species Discussion

No other warbler has this combination of features .
