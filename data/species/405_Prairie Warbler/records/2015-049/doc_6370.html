Description of the Bird

head with olive-green cap and nape, yellow supercilium and auricular, black eyeline, and black subauricular

upperparts olive-green, the back with chestnut streaks

throat, breast and belly bright yellow; flanks streaked black

Similar Species Discussion

No other species considered, but similar Magnolia and Townsend’s Warblers have black masks and lack chestnut streaks on their backs.
