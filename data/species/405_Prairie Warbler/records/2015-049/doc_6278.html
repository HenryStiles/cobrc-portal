Description of the Bird

 This was a 2nd year male Prairie Warbler caught and banded at the RMBO banding station at Chico Basin Ranch, El Paso County. Warbler bill, yellowish overal with reddish vertical stripes on olive/yellow back, distinctive face pattern with the black loop below the eye and black line through the eye. Yellow belly with black flank streaks. Dark, longish bill. Call distinctive given on occasion. 

Similar Species Discussion

Townsend's closest but back without reddish streaks and face with black behind the eye in addition to the black loop below the eye, plus males with black throat.  Black-throated Green somewhat similar but with less pattern on face and no reddish streaks on back and males with black throat.
