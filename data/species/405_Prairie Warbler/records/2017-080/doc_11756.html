Description of the Bird

I was birding alone, when I found this bird.  I saw the bird pumping its tail, which made me this Prairie or Palm, certainly wasn't a Palm.  Took many photo, during its long stay.  See photos.  Saw it on the Pueblo Reservoir CBC on Dec 16th, the 2nd time for this CBC.

Similar Species Discussion

Tail pumping elimnates most species.

Previous Experience

I've seen only a few Prairie Warblers in the fall and winter in Colorado.  I've seen Prairie Warblers back east as well.
