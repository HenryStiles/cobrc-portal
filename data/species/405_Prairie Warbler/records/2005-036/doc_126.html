Description of the Bird

A small Dendroica warbler olive above and yellow below. On the mantle the bird had rusty streaking. On the sides the bird had black streaking. Intricate black and yellow face pattern.
Behaviors: Typically foraged high in Cottonwoods and elms
Call: Call a sharp chip "tuk", song a rising buzzy thin Zee zee zee zee ze
Plumage: adult
