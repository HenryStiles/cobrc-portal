Description of the Bird

While birding at Thompson Ranch, was surprised to find this warbler.  Gray head, full eye ring, long undertail coverts, yellow underneath.  I had seen this species in the fall before.  I sent Chris Wood the photos to make sure it was this species.

 See photos.

Similar Species Discussion

Clearly not a Nashville Warbler, which would be more likely, due to the long undertail coverts.

Full thick bold eyering elimnates fall plumage Mourning Warblers, than have thin eyerings.

Previous Experience

I've seen a couple of Connecticut Warblers in Colorado during late May, and also seen them on their breeding grounds in UP of Michigan.

I've seen many Nashville Warblers, some Mourning Warblers.
