Description of the Bird

A warbler with gray hood and complete white eye ring. Light yellow under parts, and long yellow under tail coverts. See photos.
Behaviors: A hard to see warbler, that moved slowly or not at all, in dense olives, low to the ground.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
