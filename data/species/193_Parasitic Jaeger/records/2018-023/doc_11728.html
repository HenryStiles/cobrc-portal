Description of the Bird

Dark gray, gull like bird with pale patches noted under wings.   Aggressive behavior to Ring-billed gulls and a small flock of Common and Forster's terns on the sand spit.   The jaeger was seen diving/harassing the terns causing them to scatter.   It then followed three gulls in flight, nipping at the tail of one causing it to drop something (fish?) which it then dived after.   Saw it twice land on the water and the second time was able to get a photo.

Similar Species Discussion

Other jaegers were considered, but the photo appears to eliminate both the Long-tailed Jaeger and Pomarine Jaeger.
