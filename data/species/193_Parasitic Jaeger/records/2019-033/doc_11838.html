Description of the Bird

Probably a sub-adult vs immature. See photos

Similar Species Discussion

Pomarine Jaeger ruled out by lack of double white flash in underwing, less bulky body and two pointed central tail rectrices. Smaller size than Pomarine Jaeger and very acrobatic flight. Long-tailed ruled out by lack of long tail streamers and the warm brown color of this bird. I also think the photos confrim ID as Parasitic Jaeger.
