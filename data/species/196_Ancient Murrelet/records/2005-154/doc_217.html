Description of the Bird

Small alcid, 8 inches in length (measured when dead). Black cap and auricular extending to upper throat, wings, tail. Gray back, nape. Some gray on upper sides and lower flanks. White below. Short, pale bill. Upper mandible curved downward. Gray legs.
Behaviors: Bird was observed waddling on residential street. Picked up and placed in bathtub overnight where it died.
Call: not heard
Plumage: juvenal

Similar Species Discussion

Thick, pale bill ruled out other murrelets; plumage pattern rules out other murreletes and auklets.
