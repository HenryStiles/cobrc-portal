Description of the Bird

Black "hood" extending below eye and onto nape of neck. Yellowish bill. Dark eye. Partly white throat extending up behind eye, white chest and flanks. Slight black streaking on flanks. Black "collar" at nape of neck extending slightly onto flank. Gray back, black tail. White under-tail coverts. Please see photo.
Behaviors: Bird was actively swimming and diving in this small lake.
Call: Did not hear
Plumage: Not sure

Similar Species Discussion

The pale bill, contrast between the black nape and gray back and pale chin are distinctive field marks for this bird. Please see photo.
