Description of the Bird

When seen with American Wigeons, similar in size. Gray body. Pinkish breast. Rufous head with tan cap. Blue bill with dark tip. Axillaries grayish, not white.
Behaviors: 12/16 Seen in small pond with other ducks.

12/23 Seen in wet field with other ducks, probably feeding.

12/31 Seen grazing on park lawn with dozens of American Wigeons.
Call: None
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Rufous head, pinkish breast and gray body distinguish it from American Wigeon.
Redhead has black breast and does not have the tan crown.
