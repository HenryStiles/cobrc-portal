Description of the Bird

Same size and shape of adjacent American Wigeon.Reddish brown head and neck , cream crown , gray back and flanks , black tail make this ID straightfoward.
Behaviors: Feeding on short grass in park in accompany with 75 American Wigeon. Did not join the AMWI attracted to the pond being fed by people but joined a group of 6 AMWI which to flight and departed east along the Arkansas River.Appeared wild and wary , no sign of leg bands or wing damage.
Call: None heard
Plumage: adult
