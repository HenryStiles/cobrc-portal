Description of the Bird

male wigeon with a reddish brown head and a buffy crown otherwise similar in appearance to AMWIs present for comparison. Has a tad of green behind eye which by itself not an indication of EUWI x AMWI.

Similar Species Discussion

Redhead male has black breast. Male AMWI does not have a red head nor a buffy crown and has irredescent green stripe behind eye to nape.
