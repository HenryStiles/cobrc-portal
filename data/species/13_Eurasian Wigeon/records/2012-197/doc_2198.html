Description of the Bird

Deep rusty head with yellowish forecrown. Pale gray back and sides. Black undertail coverts. Short pale blue bill. Feeding from the surface near shore with American Wigeons. No trace of green in the head. Rufous color appeared uniform. No brownish coloration in back or sides.
Behaviors: Feeding from the surface of the river near shore with American Wigeons.
Call: None
Plumage: alternate
