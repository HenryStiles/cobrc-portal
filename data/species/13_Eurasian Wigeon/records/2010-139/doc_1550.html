Description of the Bird

Rufous head with a yellow forehead. It had gray spots on a rufous side. It had a mostly gray back with a dark tail. It also had rufous to pink breast. It has a small blue bill with a black tip. White on the lower flanks.
Behaviors: Swimming with American Wigeon and Northern Shoveler
Call: Didn’t hear it
Plumage: Adult Molting from Basic to Alternate

Similar Species Discussion

The red colored head eliminates everything except Redhead. The yellow forehead eliminates Redhead. Also the rufous sides with a rufous to pink breast eliminate everything. Nothing on the bird suggests it being a hybrid.
