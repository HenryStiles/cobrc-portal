Description of the Bird

Gray bill with a black tip. Red head with a yellowish buffy forehead. Pinkish breast. Gray sides and back. With a white spot in the wing and on the side towards the back of the bird. The back of the bird and the tail was black.
Behaviors: Swam around the whole time I was watching it. It was following a female American Wigeon most of the time. It called occasionally.
Call: It gave a wigeon type call, but it was different than any American Wigeon that I had heard. It sound higher and it was a single note.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Redhead - It doesn't have a yellowish-buffy forehead and it has a black breast.
Hybrid wigeon - No green was seen in the head and no brown on the body of the bird.
