Description of the Bird

Dabbling duck on ice adjacent to American Widgeon . Same size as AMWI but with a reddish brown head , buffy forehead , gray mantle and scapulars , gray side , and black undertail coverts .

Similar Species Discussion

Hybrid male AMWI / EUWI would show some brown on sides and scapulars and or incomplete reddish brown head or a noticeable green patch behind eye . One photo shows small green area behind eye which Sibley shows in one of his illustrations for male EUWI . Walked about on ice shelf with a female AMWI .
