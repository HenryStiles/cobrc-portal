Description of the Bird

Feeding with other ducks on north east side of pond. Was with American Wigeon, American Coot, Gadwall, Bufflehead, Lesser Scaup. Bird had a more rufous head with cream colored "face-plate" a white spot next to black feathers on rump, bill has black tip.

Similar Species Discussion

American Wigeon was also present and much more numerous however the differences were very plain to see. Bald plate patch different color, head more red, white patch on wings bigger.
