Description of the Bird

On the Pueblo Reservoir Christmas Bird Count on the morning of December 18th, I got a text from Chris Knight who was counting birds at Valco Pond #4, that he was seeing a male Eurasian Wigeon.  I was able to get over there right away, and see it from 7:30am to 8:00am, until Kara Carragher arrived.  We looked at the bird, and then went to our area of the CBC Circle, to start counting in our area.  The duck had red head, and golden crown, and gray sides.
