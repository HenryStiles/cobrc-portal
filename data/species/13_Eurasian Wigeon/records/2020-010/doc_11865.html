Description of the Bird

Bird had a rusty red head, gray flanks, and salmon breast. Black rump patch and white hip were readily visible. Creamy forehead strip. Blue bill with black nail. No black visible at base, no sign of AMWI influence.

Similar Species Discussion

Hybrid with AMWI should always be considered. This bird did not display any characteristics that I could see. The bird DID NOT exhibit any of the following:
- AMWI eyestripe
- AMWI blush in flanks
- Black line at base of bill
