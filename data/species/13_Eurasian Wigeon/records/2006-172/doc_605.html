Description of the Bird

This is a duck with an all rufous head, buffy forehead, gray bill with dark tip, gray back and gray flanks. Very contrasty white vent with black undertail coverts, light rufous breast.
Behaviors: Loafing on reservoir with American Widgeons, Redheads, Common Mergansers and others.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult male

Similar Species Discussion

Redhead and Canvasback are the only other North American ducks with red heads. Both Canvasback and Redhead have black breasts unlike this bird. Both Redhead and Canvasback lack the contrasty white vent.
