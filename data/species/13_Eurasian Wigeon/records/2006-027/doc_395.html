Description of the Bird

I was scanning (with binoculars) a body of water with ducks on it, when I saw what I thought looked like a male Eurasian Wigeon. I quickly got the scope out, and it indeed was a male Eurasian Wigeon. The red head, with cream yellow color crown, and gray sides were all seen. Photos included. Nothing about the bird, looked like a hybrid to me.
Behaviors: Swimming with Gadwalls and American Coots.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing. The Red head, cream yellow crown and gray sides, elimnate all other species.
