Description of the Bird

Smaller gull, with yellow bill, black dot behind the eyes, black legs, black on the wings. See photos.
Behaviors: Sitting on rocks with larger gulls and a Caspian Tern.
Call: none
Plumage: molting juvenile to adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other gulls have yellow bill and black legs.
