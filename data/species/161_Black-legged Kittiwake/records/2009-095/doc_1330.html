Description of the Bird

Gull by mostly gray plumage. shape, and webbed feet.

Bill - all-yellow without dark tip; short, slightly decurved.


Gray head with darker charcoal gray crescent behind eye. Slightly darker gray hind collar. white breast, dark tibia and feet. Dark wingtips without white mirrors.

small darker gray carpal bar reminants
Behaviors: Dead in the cattails
Call: none heard
Plumage: 2nd winter

Similar Species Discussion

Red-legged Kittiwake - this other look-alike species has an even shorter and more curved all-yellow bill, darker gray mantle and red, not black legs and feet.
