Description of the Bird

Approximately the same size as Ring-bill Gulls which were close by. Dark collar, large dark spot on face. Black, slightly thick down-curved bill.
Behaviors: Bird was resting on the water
Call: None heard
Plumage: Juvenile/1st winter

Similar Species Discussion

Compared in field guides with juvenile Bonaparte's Gull, which we had seen at the same lake earlier in the week.
Black collar and bill shape eliminated Bonparte's Gull

Resources Used

Sibley's Guide to Birds, Perterson Field Guide and comparing internet photos
