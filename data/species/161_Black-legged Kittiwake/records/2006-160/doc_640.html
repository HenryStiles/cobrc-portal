Description of the Bird

A small gull, with a black nape on the white head, all black bill, black stripe in the wings. In flight a bold wing pattern, black and white W wing pattern, and black on the end of the tail. See the photo of the bird on the water.
Behaviors: Swimming and flying around.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Bonaparte's Gull - is smaller, doesn't have the same wing pattern.
