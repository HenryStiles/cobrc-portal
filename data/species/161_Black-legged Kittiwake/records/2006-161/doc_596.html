Description of the Bird

Gull-shaped bird, gray wings, dark "stripe" of feathers through gray wing from carpal across secondary coverts. Body and head white, dark spot behind eye more crescent-like than spot. Dark eyes, dark bill. Dark collar in nape continues around side of neck. Dark tip to tail.
Behaviors: Bird spent entire time of observation floating on surface of the lake. Never saw it fly or get up at all.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

First impression was Bonaparte's or Ross's gull, but the dark collar was clear and well-defined. "Ear spot" seemed much more like a thin, long crescent, which confused the issue for a while.
