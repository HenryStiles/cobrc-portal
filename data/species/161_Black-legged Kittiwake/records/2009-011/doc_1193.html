Description of the Bird

Seen with Ring-billed Gulls, bird was smaller and shorter winged, with noticeably forked tail. Black terminal tail band, black bands across wings, black neck collar, sharply demarcated black ear spot, large black triangle on primaries. Bill all black, longer than Red-legged Kittiwake bill would be.
Behaviors: Seen in flight and on the water, picking at the surface.
Call: Did not vocalize.
Plumage: 1cy

Similar Species Discussion

Other small gulls with similar wing pattern lack neck collar or have less distinct ear spot or lack the terminal band on the tail. Red-legged Kittiwake has darker mantle and shorter bill. (I didn't see leg color at the time, but Cole's photos show black legs and feet.)

Resources Used

Previous Experience

I've seen thousands of kittiwakes on both the Pacific and the Atlantic, most recently in Newfoundland. I've seen both kittiwake species together in Alaska. I've seen kittiwakes on shore in California. This is my first for Colorado.
