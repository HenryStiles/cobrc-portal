Description of the Bird

A gull that was a little smaller than nearby Ring-billed Gulls. All yellow bill, black smudge on the collar, gray mantle which was a little darker than nearby Ring-billed Gulls. In flight black corners on the wing tips, with out any black, also paler primaries. The bird seems to flap more than the Ring-billed Gulls. See photo.
Behaviors: Seen on the water and flying.
Call: none
Plumage: adult in basic plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Red-legged Kittiwake -- Shorter bill, mantle color even darker.

Previous Experience

Seen lots of Black-legged Kittiwakes each day for three months while on St. Paul Island, and also saw Red-legged Kittiwakes every day too. I've seen other Black-legged Kittiwakes in CO, AK, and CA.
