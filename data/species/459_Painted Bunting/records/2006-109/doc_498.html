Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Leica 8x32BN Binoculars and a Swarovski Spotting Scope
Distance:  It flew up to about 20 feet from us
Light:  No clouds in the ski. The sun was reflecting off the bird so it made the bird brighter

Description of the Bird

Blue head. Red eye ring, under parts, throat, rump, and upper tail coverts. Greenish yellow back and wings. Unmistakable.
Behaviors: It was singing towards the top of trees and bushes.
Call: Song is a lot like the other Colorado Buntings, a sweet warble. I was not able to ID by song.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Varied Bunting has a blue rump, face, and under tail coverts, a red nape, and blueish purple back, wings, breast, and belly.
