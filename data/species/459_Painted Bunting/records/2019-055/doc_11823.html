Description of the Bird

Passerine.  Landed on bench to feed on scattered seed (white millet).  Very distinctive plumage.  Blue-purple head, yellow-green back, red breast, red rump.  Observed feeding at a distance of 12-15 feet with eye and binoculars.  I did not take photo's though others did. Size was comparable to a Lazuli Bunting - more common in this area.  

Similar Species Discussion

The closest species in the area is the Lazuli Bunting - coloration was clearly different.
