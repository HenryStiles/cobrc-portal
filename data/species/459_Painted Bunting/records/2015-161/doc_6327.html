Description of the Bird

Bird flew into mist nets at Ridgway Banding Station, Confluence Park, Ridgway State Park, Ridgway, CO. Bird was unknown sex, hatch-year individual. Feathers beginning to have blue cast over green rectrices. Missing most feathers on top of head.
