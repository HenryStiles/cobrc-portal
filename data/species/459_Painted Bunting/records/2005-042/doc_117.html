Description of the Bird

Boldly patterned passerina bunting. The head was entirely blue, except for the red chin/throat and red orbital ring; the underparts were solidly bright red as was the rump; the remaining upperparts were a bright green; both the wings and tail looked to be a very worn brown with only traces of red along the lengths of the rectrices and remiges (hence the consideration of third year plumage); the bill was stout and short, coming to a blunt point, and entirely dark.
Behaviors: Observed feeding at a seed feeder in a suburban backyard.
Call: None heard.
Plumage: adult possibly third year
