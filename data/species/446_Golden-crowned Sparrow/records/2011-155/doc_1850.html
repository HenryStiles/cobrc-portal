Description of the Bird

Dull yellow crown flanked by wide, black, lateral, crown stripes that extend back on to the nape. Brown back with darker brown streaks. Pale throat and belly. Flanks and breast muddy brown. Slightly bi-colored bill with maxilla just a bit more dusky-colored than paler lower mandible. Thin, whitish wingbars.
Behaviors: The bird was at a platform feeder, but it was feeding exclusively on the ground beneath the feeder. It had an apparent preference for the ground beneath the surrounding shrubs, to which it would quickly retreat having ventured forth briefly to take advantage of the seeds on the ground.
Call: none
Plumage: nonbreeding adult

Similar Species Discussion
