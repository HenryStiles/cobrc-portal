Description of the Bird

See photos: Large sparrow (larger than Gambel's WCSP present at same time) with thick blackish lateral crown stripes and yellow central crown from bill to about eye, then white behind. Back streaked medium and dark brown. Smudgy pale grayish beneath. Darkish bill. Bright wingbars.
Behaviors: Feeding with other sparrows/juncos at feeder
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult basic

Similar Species Discussion

No evidence for hybridization with WCSP. No other species similar
