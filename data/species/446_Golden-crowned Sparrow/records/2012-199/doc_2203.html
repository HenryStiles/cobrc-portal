Description of the Bird

GPS location 39.634254x -105.019886. The bird was in the company of 8 White-crowned. Large Sparrow, Zonotrichia. Black lateral crown stripes, YELLOW CROWN, light gray throat and breast, lighter belly, slightly buff flanks, bi-color bill (dark up/light down), black-brown streaks on wings, two distinct wing bars, gray nape, black-brown streaks on back and scapulars, unstreaked gray-brown rump, long squared tail. Excellent views in full sunlight. I had the opportunity to study Golden-crowned on 11 separate occasions at Red Rocks between 12/21/11 and 4/15/2012, 8 of those observations are recorded on eBird. Most of these observations were early morning with Mike Henwood and included much discussion about ID characteristics. I am familiar with the bird and 100% confident in this ID.
Behaviors: The bird was in a small flock of Zonotrichia.
