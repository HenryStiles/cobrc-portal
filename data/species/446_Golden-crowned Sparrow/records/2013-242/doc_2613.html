Description of the Bird

Bird did not call.  It stayed under the feeders or in a honeysuckle bush will other sparrows.

Similar Species Discussion

Present were White-crowned Sparrows.  Other Zonotrichia sparrows are possible in Nucla.  We have had Harris's and White-throated several times in our yard but this was the first Golden-crowned.  Two field marks set this bird apart immediately.  The heavy black eyebrow and the yellow forecrown made the ID easy.  Lack of white throat and yellow lores eliminated White-throated Sparrow.  The two-toned bill eliminated the other two Zonotrichia sparrows as both Harris's and White-crowned have yellow-pink bills.  There are many more subtle field marks that differentiate look alike species but Brenda was able to get several good photos.  I will let the photos do that part.

Resources Used

We did look at several field guides but mostly relied on our experience with sparrows.

Previous Experience

Brenda and I are world birders and have had a lot of experience with birds in general.  We have been able to study all ABA sparrow species except Henslow's Sparrow.
