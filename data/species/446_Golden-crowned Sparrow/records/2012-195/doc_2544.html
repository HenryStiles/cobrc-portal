Description of the Bird

Largish sparrow comparable to White-crowned Sparrow is size and shape.  Underparts, neck, and face below the eyes were uniformly medium gray and unmarked.  The most obvious plumage feature was the golden crown patch offset by a modestly thick black border.  The border seemed to be extending down to the eye, so I think the bird must have been transitioning into breeding plumage at the time.
