Description of the Bird

A large Zonotrichia sparrow. Mottled brownish on the back, plain gray underparts. Face mostly gray with a balck cap. Down the center of the black cap was a bright yellow strip.
Behaviors: On the ground and in Russian Olive trees, loosely associating with Chipping and White-crowned Sparrows
Call: none noted
Plumage: Adult Alternate

Similar Species Discussion

White-crowned Sparrow has more black and white stripes on the head.
