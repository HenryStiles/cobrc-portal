Description of the Bird

Long-tailed Spizella sparrow with distinctive golden crown extending back about half way where replaced with white and contrasting with blackish lateral crown stripes. Wings rufous with small white tips on two sets of coverts giving it a narrow wing bar look.
Behaviors: skulker coming out of dense bushes to feed on ground near bird feeders.
Call: none heard
Plumage: winter adult

Similar Species Discussion

IM White-crowned Sparrow has a different color crown that extends all the way to the rear.

White-crowned Sparrow has yellow lore on some individuals and not a yellow crown.
