Description of the Bird

A medium large sparrow, brown wings with slight white wingbars, black crown and white stripe on head with noticeable yellow-golden crown, pale bill, long tail, dull grey breast. Seen in bright light for 5 minutes moving from a tree stump in a shallow pond to the shoreline weeds and bushes.
Behaviors: Moving from submerged tree stump in pond to bushes alongside pond adjacent to La Plata River at mile marker 16 at Colorado Highway 140 south of Ft. Lewis, La Plata County on April 27, 2013 at 3:30 p.m.
Call: none
Plumage: Adult breeding plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Not White-throated or White-crowned Sparrow because yellow-fore crown clearly seen.
