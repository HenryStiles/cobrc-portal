Description of the Bird

Large sparrow feeding on deck with other house finches and juncos.  Had yellow crown bordered by wide black cap.  The wings were brown with some rufous streaks and white wing bars.  Only stayed a short time to feed on sunflower seeds on the deck.

Similar Species Discussion

White crown sparrow.  Eliminated because of the yellow crown and the black cap and the rufous coloring on wings.
