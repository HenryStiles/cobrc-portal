Description of the Bird

Zonotrichia sparrow in flock with WCSP and Harris's. Larger than WCSP, smaller than Harris's. Head only part seen well. Thick black lateral crown stripes meeting over bill (on forehead). Central crown, behind where black crosses over, bright yellow in front fading to gray posteriorly. Face plain gray. Bill mostly dusky/silvery
Behaviors: Hiding in bushes with 20+ WC Sparrows and 2 Harris's Sparrows
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other bird has this head pattern. No evidence of WCSP genes
