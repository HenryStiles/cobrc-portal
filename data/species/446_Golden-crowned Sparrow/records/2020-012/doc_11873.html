Description of the Bird

Flitting about the reeds on the north side of Sand Creek, among White-crowned Sparrow and Song Sparrow, and of a comparable size. Golden forecrown, with dark but patchy (not uniformly black) head to mid-eye. Faint partial white eye ring, two-toned bill, unstreaked breast. Observed for about 10 minutes and photographed.

Similar Species Discussion

Eliminated from adjacent WCSP by golden forecrown and lack of white stripes on head, darker bill. These features and the position of the gold plumage eliminate WTSP.
