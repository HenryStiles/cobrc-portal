Optics:  10 X 42 binocs, camera
Distance:  5' to 35'
Light:  excellent

Description of the Bird

-ground fed with other sparrows and finches from seed thrown out onto gravel drive
-large sparrow yellow forecrown with white on back of crown
-distinctive black stripe from crown down to eye from bill to back of head
-bicolored bill with gray face
-dull gray belly and rufous tinged flanks
-two white wing bars with rufous on wings
-brownish gray back with tail a little darker

Similar Species Discussion

other sparrow species were eliminated becasue of bright yellow forecrown and black stripe on side of head - very distinctive
