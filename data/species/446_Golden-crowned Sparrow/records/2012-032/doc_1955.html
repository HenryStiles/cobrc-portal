Description of the Bird

See photos. Zonotrichia sparrow, larger than nearby Gambel's WCSP, with yellow center of crown from mid crown to bill surrounded by moderately broad darkish brown lateral crown stripes. No white below lateral crown stripe or eyeline to suggest hybrid status.
Behaviors: Feeding on ground with one immature Gambel's WCSP
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult molting basic to alternate

Similar Species Discussion

Head pattern unique, except similar head pattern seen in some hybrids with WCSP. Lack of whitish supercilium and eyeline eliminate hybrid
