Description of the Bird

Pale male redpoll: back whitish to pale sandy colored background. Somewhat limited pale pink wash on chest. Small red poll. One tiny mark on undertail coverts. Pale pink, moderate size, unmarked rump patch. Very bright and prominent white greater wingcovert bar and tertial edges. Medium sized bill
Behaviors: At feeders
Call: heard same odd call note as with Schmoker's bird
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Unmarked pale pink rump patch, nearly unmarked undertail coverts, pale pink chest, bright white edges to wing coverts/tertials, etc eliminate CORE; 13.5-14.5 on Sibley scale, with >13 = HORE for males.
