Description of the Bird

Male redpoll with fairly pale and fairly limited pink on breast, sandy gray background color on back, moderate diffuse streaking on flanks, one very thin streak on undertail coverts, large unmarked rump patch. Also, outertail feather extensively white below, suggested as likely mark for HORE by Thede Tobish. Bill small and stubby. Red "poll" somewhat limited. Bold thick greater wingcovert bar, moderate white edges to tertials
Behaviors: at feeders
Call: not heard for certain
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

See previous discussions: scores 15 on Sibley scale, with 14-18 = Hoary by that measure. Also, cheek rather white, fairly broad white edges to wing coverts/tertials, much white on undertail
