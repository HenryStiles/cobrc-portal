Description of the Bird

Very pale female redpoll with one thick streak on longest undertail covert feather and moderately sized very lightly marked rump patch. Very white face, quite pronounced white edges to greater wingcoverts, tertials, and even secondaries. Light side marking for female. Bill moderate sized
Behaviors: In trees near feeder
Call: not heard for sure
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

13 to 13.5 on Sibley scale, where 11+ = HORE for female/immature. Among the palest redpolls I saw all winter
