Description of the Bird

Seen mostly from below. Modestly limited, diffuse pale side streaking. Somewhat limited, rather pale (except just below throat) pink wash beneath. Small stubby bill, smallish red poll, one undertail covert streak. Rump seen, but not photographed, had moderate unmarked pale pink patch.
Behaviors: In trees, awaiting visiting feeders
Call: not heard for certain
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Unmarked rump patch, one streak on undertail coverts, pale pink below, small bill all bespeak HORE. 14.5 to 15.5 on Sibley's scale, which = HORE
