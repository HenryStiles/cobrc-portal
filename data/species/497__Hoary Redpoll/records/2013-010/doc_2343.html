Description of the Bird

Pale female redpoll, with pallid grayish-buff background color to upperparts, modest to moderate side streaking, large unstreaked white rump patch, and single moderate streak on longest undertail covert feather. Prominent white greater covert wingbar and prominent broad white edges to tertials. Small red cap. Prominent bristles around base of bill with bill appearing smallish.
Behaviors: At feeders
Call: not heard specifically
Plumage: unknown

Similar Species Discussion

Scored 13.5 on Sibley index, which for female/immature = HORE (11+ is HORE per that scale). In addition to those marks used by Sibley's scoring index, the very prominent lower wingbar and tertial edging seems to be somewhat indicative of HORE
