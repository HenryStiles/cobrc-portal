Description of the Bird

This bird was seen at about 9:15, at 15-20 feet, for about 30 seconds. The lighting was bright shade and view unobstructed. Throat and auriculars gray separated by wide white stripe. Narrow white supercilium. Dull yellow fore and mid-crown. Wings gray with two dull yellow wingbars somewhat converging to form a panel in between. Back grayish. Remainder of underparts whitish/grayish. Overall, a rather stout warbler but warbler-sized. Bill black.
