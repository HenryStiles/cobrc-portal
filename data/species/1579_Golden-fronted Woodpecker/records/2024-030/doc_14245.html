Description of the Bird

A middle-sized woodpecker with a black and white striped back. The head was a tan color with an obvious yellow patch on the back of the head. The bill was quite long and black. The chest was also a tan color. I didn't see any yellow on the lower belly: either I was never in a position to see it, or it might indicate that it was a young bird born this summer. When it flew, it had an obvious white rump.

Similar Species Discussion

The other Melanerpes woodpecker seen on the eastern plains is Red-bellied: the female of that species is red on back of the head.

No other Colorado woodpecker has this combination of a striped back and yellow on the back of the head. 

 

Previous Experience

I have seen this species several times in Texas: I saw some in April 2024 in the Texas hill country.
