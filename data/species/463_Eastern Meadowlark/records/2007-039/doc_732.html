Description of the Bird

I do have a picture. It shows the white malar and the very dark black eyeline. Of course the Eastern Meadowlark song is very distinctive and quite different from the Western Meadowlark song. High contrast head pattern on the eastern compared with the much duller pattern on the western was very noticable.
Behaviors: Calling, chasing and singing, evidently in breeding behavior.
Call: Eastern song and calls.
Plumage: Breeding

Similar Species Discussion

See above. I was able to compare the eastern and western at this same spot visually and by calls and songs.
