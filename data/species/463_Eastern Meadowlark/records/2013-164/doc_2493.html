Description of the Bird

I observed a western meadowlark chasing a second meadowlark that showed much more white in its tail. That was the only visual I had of the bird.
Behavior: singing from a perch
Call: Singing "spring of the year"-type song 4 to 6 times in one short period.
Plumage: adult
