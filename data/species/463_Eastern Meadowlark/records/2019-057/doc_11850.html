Description of the Bird

This bird gave a song much higher pitched and thinner than Western Meadowlark which is how we identified it.  The bird had a white malar and more white in the tail than WEME. 

Similar Species Discussion

Western Meadowlark was eliminated by the high picthed nature of the song and the extra white in the tail and white malar.
