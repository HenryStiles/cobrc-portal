Description of the Bird

Only determined visually to be a meadowlark.
Behaviors: Sitting in top of tree calling repeatedly. There were WEME all over the place, some on ground, some on short veg, and a few in trees
Call: A repeated Bzzzrrrt. Loud.
Plumage: not determined

Similar Species Discussion

The call was utterly typical for Eastern Meadowlark. We were even able to play iBirdPRO while listening to the bird, and the recorded call notes were an excellent match with the meadowlark's calls. I've never heard a Western Meadowlark utter a similar noise.

Resources Used

I consulted Chris Wood on this issue, who stated that the call was, at this time of year in particular, the most definitive ID mark for Eastern (vs Western) Meadowlark -- and given the duration during which it was heard, and the direct comparison to recordings, that the ID was solid. So, Chris was my resource consulted (as well as iBirdPRO in the field
