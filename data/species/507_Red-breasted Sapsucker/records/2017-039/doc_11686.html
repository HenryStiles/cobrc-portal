Description of the Bird

Sapsucker by shape, size, coloration, and behavior - head entirely red except some black areas on back of head; red breast which was clearly demarcated and yellow belly below. Back black with bold white wing patches and some whitish gold colored streaks and spots. Short and narrow white streak just behind bill (ruber) becoming black area just before the eye. In flight (seen once) it had a narrow white streak extending from the mid back to lowest part of back.

Similar Species Discussion

All red head, throat and breast eliminates the other three sapsuckers. The small white streak behind the bill base eliminates the southern subspecies, daggetti as does the red on the breast more or less abruptly ending at the yellow belly
