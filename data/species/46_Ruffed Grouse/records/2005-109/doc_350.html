Description of the Bird

A smallish brown grouse, with a crest and black tail band. See photo.
Behaviors: Sitting on log and walking on grouse.
Call: heard drumming once.

Similar Species Discussion

Previous Experience

Have seen Ruffed Grouse briefly in NH in 2004 and 2005. Heard them drumming in NH both in 2004 and 2005. Seen lots of Blue Grouse, including many that same day.
