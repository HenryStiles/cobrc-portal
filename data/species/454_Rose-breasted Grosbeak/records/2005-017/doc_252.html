Habitat

Dr. Funk's backyard is next to Minnesota Creek which has very nice mid-elevation riparian habitat (thick shrubs, mostly large Gambel's Oak).

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Leica 8x32 binoculars
Distance:  ~120 feet
Light:  Good lighting - see photo

Description of the Bird

Head: all black with a large pale bill. back: all black except for a white rump patch. Tail: black. Rose bib on a white belly. Black wings with white wing bars.
Behaviors: This bird was seen at a feeder at the east edge of Paonia, CO. Other seed-eating species were feeding with it.
Call: not heard
Plumage: alternate
