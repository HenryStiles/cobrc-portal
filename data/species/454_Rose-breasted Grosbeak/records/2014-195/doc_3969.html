Description of the Bird

Large headed and big conicle-billed,silvery bill, black hood and back, crimson bib, white tummy, white spots on primaries. Singing much like a black headed grosbeak, but less fluted.

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing else in Colorado looks like an adult male rose-breasted grosbeak.

Resources Used
