Description of the Bird

Jane Stulp and I were talking in her front yard, when a humingbird making no noise, came zooming into some flowers, the bird faced us, and had bright ruby red (not pink) throat, with a black chin, and a forked tail, and then it flew over the house.  Later, the bird was seen at the hummingbird feeder in the back yard.  See photos.
