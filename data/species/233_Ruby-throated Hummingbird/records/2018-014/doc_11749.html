Description of the Bird

This Ruby-throated Hummingbird was a slightly small hummingbird with shorter wings and a barely decurved bill. The face was darker gray green including in front of eyes. This immature male had a few red feathers on the gorget. The head and mantle were bright emerald green. The chest was dirty light gray with green spots heavier on flanks and gorget. 

Similar Species Discussion

The Black-chinned Hummingbird was eliminated due to the lack of light green on cap and back. Also the lighter patch in front of eye was not evident. The Broad-tailed Hummingbird was eliminated due to the lack of thick throat and cheek spots. Also the medium patch in front of eye was not evident.

Previous Experience
