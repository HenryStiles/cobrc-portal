Description of the Bird

About the same size and quite similar to Black-chinned Hummingbird. This was an immature male with some dusky streaks on throat. Quite metallic green back and head. Black mask. Some cinnamon wash to flanks indicating juvenile. Emarginated primaries with P10 having tapered tips. Forked tail with white-tipped rectrices.  

Similar Species Discussion

Black-chinned Hummingbird would have a blunt tip to P10.
