Description of the Bird

I observed both of the Colorado Plegadis Ibis in a flooded field. Here (an see photo) were the differences in the two species that I observed.

Glossy Ibis

Head with dark brown eye. Bill light brownish.

LIGHT BLUISH LINES run from bill base up to sides of eye by do not encirle the eye.

Legs grayish with red "knees"
Behaviors: Foraging in a flooded field with about 14 White-face Ibis
Call: no vocalization heard
Plumage: alternate

Similar Species Discussion

White-faced Ibis is similar and was present for comparison (see photo)

White-faced Ibis had all red legs without contrasting "knees"

WFIB had lighter grayish bill not a darker brownish bill.

WFIB had white border around a reeish face and a dark red eye comparfed to the bluishish lines that did NOT encircle the dark brown, not red, eye.
