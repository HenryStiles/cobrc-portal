Description of the Bird

A dark ibis with a thin, bluish-white border to the bill that extended to the eyes but not beyond them, dark bluish-gray facial skin between the eye and bill, brownish bill, dark eye, and dark legs except for the faint reddish ankles ("knees").
Behaviors: Mostly foraging on the edge of the pond.
Call: none heard.
Plumage: adult alternate

Similar Species Discussion

White-faced is very similar, but the salient characteristics mentioned in the description rule out that species. I also paid close attention to the possibility that it was a hybrid. In particular, I cautiously looked at whether there was any white feathering along the blue-white skin border - none, whether any blue/white extended beyond the front of the eye - none, and whether there was any pink/purple infusion to the facial skin between the eye and bill - none.
