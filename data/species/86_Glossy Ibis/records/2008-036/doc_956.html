Description of the Bird

Dark Plegadis Ibis.

Legs, gray with red joints
Iris color - brown
Lore - dark
Facial skin by eye - blue
No blue wrap around eye
Behaviors: Feeding at edge of marsh with 7 White-faced Ibis
Call: heard but not useful for I.D. purposes
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

White-faced Ibis

WFIB has reddish legs, has red eye, and has white wrap-around color around eye, and reddish facial skin

Hybrid - WFIB x GLIB has combination of characters usually with red eye, and reddish facial skin, unlike this bird
