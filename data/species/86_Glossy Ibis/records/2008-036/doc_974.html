Description of the Bird

A Plegadis ibis in Breeding Plumage. The main difference was in the face and legs. The legs were grayish. The facial skin was grayish-blue bordered by pale blue. The eye was very dark brown. See photo for exact distribution.
Behaviors: Feeding and preening with other 60 Pleagadis ibis
Call: None noted
Plumage: Adult Alternate

Similar Species Discussion

The Dark eye, gray facial skin with thin blue borders and gray legs cinch the ID
