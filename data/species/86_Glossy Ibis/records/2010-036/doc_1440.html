Light:  Overcast , sun behind high clouds , bird somewhat backlight.

Description of the Bird

Plegadis species with a dark eye and bluish lores with a small white border around face. Legs uniformly grayish ,no hint of red coloration on legs. Bill coloration difficult to judge as overcast and to some extent bird was backlight .
Behaviors: Bird was an individual feeding at edge of marsh , flew off maybe 50 yards and proceeded to preen and rest before commencing feeding again but lower part of body now no longer visible because in long grass. No other ibis were present for comparison.
Call: None
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

White -faced Ibis would have a reddish eye and lores and legs would show some reddish coloration especially around the knee joint. This bird seems to have a wider white border than I would expect for a 100 % Glossy in one of the photos so a hybrid is another possibility with the Glossy genes dominating. I have seen birds with this amount of white on the face pronounced a Glossy Ibis before e.g. Chico Basin Ranch , May 2008.
