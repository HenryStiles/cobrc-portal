Description of the Bird

Medium sized dove with large white wing patches. Long slighty droopy black bill. Tear-drop shaped blue orbital ring. Gray-brown head, neck, back, belly, and upperwings, with darker brown primaries. Black mark on face below eye. Tail slightly rounded and tipped with broad white.
Behaviors: Bird was calling and displaying.
Call: low pitched cooing
Plumage: adult
