Description of the Bird

Large, square-tailed pale brown dove with red eye, blue orbital, broad white stripe on folded wing, black and white undertail, and rich brown uppertail, and red legs.
Behaviors: Sitting in bare branches of large tree, and feeding on the ground
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other North American columbiform has a white stripe on the folded wing

None
