Description of the Bird

A large square-tailed dove, pale brown overall, with a white stripe on the wing visible in flight and to a lesser extent on the sitting bird; undertail blackish anteriorly and pale whitish posteriorly. Iris red, blue eye-ring and blue skin at base of bill, which was gray.
Behaviors: Sitting high in a deciduous tree. Flew short distances within treetop.
Call: Called several times, a seried of coos in a pattern reminiscent of the "who-cooks-for-you" call of the Barred Owl
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Mourning Dove has pointed tail; lacks white in wing.

Eurasian Collared Dove lacks white in wing; less extensive black at base of undertail; different call; lacks blue orbital ring.
