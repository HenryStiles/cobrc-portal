Description of the Bird

A medium-sized dove, pale brownish on folded wings, with darker primaries; gray wash on rump area (lower back), vent area cream colored; underside of tail about two thirds black, one third white (at tip); head pale brown with blue skin between dark-gray bill and orange-red eye, including orbital; a black comma on lower auricular.

Tail shape difficult to assess with the angle of view (see photos).

A narrow strip of white could be observed at the tips of some secondary feathers on the resting wing (see photos).
Behaviors: Sitting on branch resting
Call: did not vocalize
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other North American dove has exposed blue skin on face
