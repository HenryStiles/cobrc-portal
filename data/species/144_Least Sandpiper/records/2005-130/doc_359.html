Description of the Bird

A small shorebird with yellow legs, and short bill, about an inch or two smaller than the nearby Baird's Sandpiper.
Behaviors: Walking around in the rocks along the Arkansas River with a Baird's Sandpiper and Killdeer.
Call: none
Plumage: winter
