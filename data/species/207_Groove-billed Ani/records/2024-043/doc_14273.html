Description of the Bird

Lorraine Lanning was the finder as I understand it.

This bird was unbelievably accommodating.  Came within 5' of the MOB.

Adult bird.  I will upload my photos.  Plumage differential is neglible in this species.  Long tailed, huge bill with obvious grooves.

Similar Species Discussion

No other species considered due to the nature of this species.  Blackbirds would be the only other remotely close species but the bill itself and length of tail readily eliminated all other possibilities.

Previous Experience

I've seen several of this species in south Texas as well as the bird found in Colorado in 2019.
