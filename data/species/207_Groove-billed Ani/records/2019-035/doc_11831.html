Description of the Bird

Came to the location after the first report came up. Waited along the south side of the creek with many others. After about half an hour, we heard the two-note cuckoo sounding call, before it flew out of the brush a short distance into a tree where it remained until I left. 

Similar Species Discussion

Crows, ravens, and magpies were considered, but the large bill with distincitve grooves along the length as well as the featherless facial areas eliminated those birds.
