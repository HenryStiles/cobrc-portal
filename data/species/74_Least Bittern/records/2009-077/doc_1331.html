Description of the Bird

Small wader sized bird. Crown, back, and tail greenish black. Neck, sides, and underparts brown and white. Wings chestnut with contrasting, pale patches. Bill thin and yellow.
Behaviors: The bird climbed into some willows responding to my tape and began calling. It then climbed higher into a tree.
Call: tut tut call
Plumage: adult
