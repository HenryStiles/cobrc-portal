Description of the Bird

small (for heron-type) size; golden brown plumage with dark back and cap; bright yellow iris; stout golden-orange bill, duskier at the tip; long legs and long thick neck that when extended was almost as long as the body.
Behavior: mainly in freeze posture while standing in the open(!), but occasionally foraging for a fish
Call: NA
Plumage: adult, probably female

Similar Species Discussion

right size for a Green Heron, but strong golden brown color and dark back rule that out.
