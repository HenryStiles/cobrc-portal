Description of the Bird

Small size but larger than a robin- Tan side with white shoulder stripe - Dark on top of head and top of back - Light belly and throat with stripe on side - Long yellowish bill - Dark eye - Light colored legs - Heron type shape - Very small tail

A not very good photo will hopefully accompany this report.
Behaviors: Sat on a bush in the middle of the pond in full sun for several minutes and then walk around the bush onto the other side and disappeared in the bush and reed area.
Call: none
Plumage: Breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Possible Green Heron but Tan side wasn't right. Looked in Sibley's Guide to Birds (large book) which I had carried with me. Everything exactly matched the picture in the book of the Least Bittern. Photo to confirm.
