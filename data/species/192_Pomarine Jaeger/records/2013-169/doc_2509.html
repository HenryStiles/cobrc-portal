Description of the Bird

classic adult Pomerine Jaeger with dark, long, broad tail twisted at a 90 degree angle from the horizontal. Dark head with whitish throat and neck, here with yellow-buff blush in certain light. Dark mottled breast band and whitish belly. Dark legs with dark webbed feet.
Behavior: While I watched it, the jaeger was mostly stationary and mostly sitting on the wrack line of the beach below the campground at Antero Res.
It did stand at one point, grabbed a dragonfly from the grasses and sat again.
Call: non heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The other jaegers and skuas as adults have noticeably different tails, Long-tailed longer and tapering to a point, and Parasitic shorter and tapering to a sharp point, not curled or twisted as in Pom. Broad beast band, none in Long-tailed and much thinner in adult Parasitic.
