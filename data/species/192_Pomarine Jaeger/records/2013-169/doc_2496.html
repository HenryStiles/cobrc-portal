Description of the Bird

Typical Jaeger profile , heavy body , heavy bill not fine , dark black cap , pale whitish nape and neck ,dark brown back and upper wings , short tail with twisted central feather , pale under belly with dark breast band. Did not observe bird in flight.
Behavior: Sitting or standing along shoreline .
Call: None heard
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other jaeger or skua has this combination of features . Both Parasitic and Long-tailed adults have smaller less extensive dark caps and tail proportion and shape very different
