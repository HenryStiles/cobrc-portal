Description of the Bird

Dark jaeger sitting on the water. When it flew it showed the double white patches in the wings. Since this bird was seen by many others. I'm just including my photo of the bird.
Behaviors: Sitting on the water and then seen flying.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile dark morph

Similar Species Discussion

Parasitic and Long-tailed Jaegers are smaller and have different over all colors. The double white flash in the wings in flight should elimnate both of these species.
