Description of the Bird

Gull like, mostly brown overall with white showing on primaries. Much faster than gulls in flight. Seen harassing gulls that were sitting along rubber tires protecting the front of the docks in the marina. Came in fast and low getting all the gulls up that were in the area. It would then pursuit one or other of the gulls and in turn getting chased by larger California Gulls. It then flew past observers on the point that were watching moving to the west.

Similar Species Discussion

The gulls that were in the area including California Gulls, Herring Gulls, Ring-billed Gulls.
