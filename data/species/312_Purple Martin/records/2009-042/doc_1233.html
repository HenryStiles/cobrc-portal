Description of the Bird

A large long-bodied swallow with a long slightly forked tail. Very dark purplish above and pale below. Forehead was grayish and collar was grayish, otherwise all dark above. Throat was grayish continuing down the sides all the way to the flanks. Otherwise underside was mostly off white.
Behaviors: Flew in and perched on a wire and preened briefly.
Call: not detected
Plumage: female type plumage

Similar Species Discussion

No other swallow is as large as the purple martin or has the features noted in my description
