Description of the Bird

Warbler with chestnut colored ear patch, yellow above the eyes, dark cap, yellow throat and breast with strong streaking. All dark black bill, whitish underparts, olive colored back, with white wing bar, dark legs, and tail. See two photos.
Behaviors: Singing in an elm tree, seen nearby a Blue-winged Warbler for a few minutes.
Call: high song "seet seet seet seet" My guess was it either a Cape May or Bay-breasted, until I saw it.
Plumage: adult
