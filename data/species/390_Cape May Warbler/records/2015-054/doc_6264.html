Description of the Bird

warbler with upper breast and throat yellow , breast heavily streaked black , yellowish eyebrow , malar and nape yellowish with gray auriculars and crown , white vent and undertail  , dark line between eye and base of bill . Actively feeding insects from surface of leaves , occaisonally walking along tree limbs like BAWW .

Similar Species Discussion

PRAW breast streaking on sides not completely across , doesn't have gray auriculars , PIWA doesn't have breast streaking , MAWA has completely gray head ( f) or black auriculars ( m) . TOWA/BTGW complex have black throats/breasts with bright yellow eyebrows .
