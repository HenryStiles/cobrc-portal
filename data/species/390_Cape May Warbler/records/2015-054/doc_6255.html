Description of the Bird

I was birding with Gene Rutherford at Hasty Campground, when I noticed a small warbler in the upper parts of the Cottonwood trees.  I quickly identifed the bird as a Cape May Warbler, do to the bold streaking on the breast, yellow color in the face.  See my photos.

Similar Species Discussion

Doesn't look like too much to me, in this plumage.
