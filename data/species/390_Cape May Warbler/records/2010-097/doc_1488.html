Description of the Bird

Seeing this bird in he hand, it's yellow rump contrasting with an olive back and dark tail eliminate all but Magnolia and Yellow-rumped Warblers. It has extensive yellow on the face and olive auricular and olive cap. The legs were dark gray. The breast was streaked with verticle dark bars and had a yellow wash.
Behaviors: Moved up into a tall tree after it gave a loud and thin chip note. It was caught while foragging in Kochia near the ground.
Call: very high chip when it was released from banding
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Magnolia Warbler - immature has all gray face and white eyering, with much more yellow on the breast and with dark streaks in lower belly.

Yellow-rumped Warbler - Face in immatures is warm brown without any yellow, back is more brown and less olive green.
