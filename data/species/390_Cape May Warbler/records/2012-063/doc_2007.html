Documentation of Cape May Warbler
2012-063

Observer Information

Reporter:  Jane Stulp  24247 Hwy 287,   LAMAR, CO  81052-9707
E-mail:  jane@stulpfarms.com
Other Observers:  Dave Leatherman and Dotti Russell observed the Cape May briefly on the 26th; original finder and identifier

Species, Date, Time and Location Information

Species:  Cape May Warbler
First Date/Time:  4/26/2012
Last Date/Time: 
Duration (total time in view):  on and off for about 3 hours
County:  Prowers
Specific Location:  -5 miles south of the south edge of Lamar on Hwy 287 and about half mile to the west
-in the back yard of the farm house
Number:  1
Age:  Unknown
Sex:  Male
Plumage:  Other/Unknown

Habitat

near the water feature with pinion pines, flowering honeysuckle, locust trees, and lots of miller moths in the grass

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  binocs
Distance:  10' to 35'
Light:  on the 26th the bird was in the shadows of the trees - the other two days were in the sunlight and very clear

Description of the Bird

-colorful warbler
-dark olive crown and back with darker streaking on back
-yellow neck, throat and face with rusty or rufous colored auriculars and above eye
-light lower belly
-black streaks converge at throat
-white greater coverts
Behaviors: -fed warbler like in the pinion pines
-secretive
-disappeared several times
Call: n/a
Plumage: adult male

Similar Species Discussion

-considered Blackburnian but the face pattern was different and the bird was more olive colored

Resources Used

The Sibley Guide for Birds
and other observers confirmed

Previous Experience

previously observed a Cape May in the yard on the exact same day (April 26 and 27) in 2010

Notes

Notes made DURING observation

Additional Information

Comments: this bird's rufous patch on the auriculars did not seem as bright or quite as well defined as the previous Cape May
Time: first observation around 6:45
Elevation: 3800 feet
Other Dates: -first two photos submitted was taken on April 26 -observed also on April 28th around 8:00 am, and did get better photos
Nearest Town: Lamar
Independent Observers: original finder and identifier
