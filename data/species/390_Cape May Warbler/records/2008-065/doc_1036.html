Description of the Bird

Gray crown, and cheek patch. Yellow supercillium. Yellow through - extending up on side of neck and down onto lower side.

Streaked gray lines down the underside from neck down onto belly.

I did not get a good look at the upper side. There was a small wing bar in upper wing.
Behaviors: In a mixed flock - several yellow-rumps around - lots of migrants were in the general area (Thrushes, empids, sparrows, etc.).

Actively feeding
Call: silent
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Very distinct plumage, really did not struggle with id'ing the bird.
