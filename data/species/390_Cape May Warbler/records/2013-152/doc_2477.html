Description of the Bird

I was at Rose Pond at Chico Basin Ranch, after leaving the Headquarters (where there was a male Cape May Warbler), and quickly saw a male Cape May Warbler. Then later east along the dam, there was a male Cape May Warbler there as well. I'm not sure how many of these birds were around. I assume the bird from the Headquarters wasn't the bird at Rose Pond. See photos of the bird at Rose Pond, which stayed for three days.
Behavior: In trees, moving around the leaves eating food.
Call: none
Plumage: adult breeding plumage
