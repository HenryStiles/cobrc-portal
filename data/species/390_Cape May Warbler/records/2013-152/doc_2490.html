Description of the Bird

Setophaga warbler , yellow neck , reddish brown auriculars , upper breast yellow heavily streaked in black , white greater coverts made this an easy ID .
Behavior: Activeley feeding in willows along dam wall at Rose Pond , Chico Basin Ranch.
Call: thin short high pitched call , song not heard
Plumage: adult
