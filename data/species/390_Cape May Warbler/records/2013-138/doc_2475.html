Description of the Bird

The bird was a yellow type Dendroica type warbler, with white wing patches and black streaked back. Eye and bill were dark. The rufous auriculars were surrounded below by bright yellow and on top a darker crown. The yellow extended onto the back of the neck and into the lores. The yellow breast and flanks had black streaks. The vent area was clear and yellowish.
Behavior: The bird was observed fly catching over a small pond. Flying back and forth from a willow tree hanging over the pond. Occasionally, the bird would glean insects from the tree
Call: none heard
Plumage: breeding
