Description of the Bird

A phalarope, mostly red, though molting out of breeding plumage.  Probably a first for Huerfano County and probably a first for July for the state of Colorado.  Polly Neldner took many great photos, my photos aren't great.

Similar Species Discussion

Red and Wilson's Phalarope -- don't have red colors all over.

Previous Experience

I saw lots of Red Phalaropes in June of 2014 in Barrow, AK.  I've seen a few in Colorado, none with this much red color.
