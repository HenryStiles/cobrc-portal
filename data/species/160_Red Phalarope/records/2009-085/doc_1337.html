Description of the Bird

Swimming shorebird with pale gray upperparts. Neck and underparts white. White crown with small amount of black on the rear of the crown. Black eye-patch. Short, stout black bill.
Behaviors: Swimming with 12 Red-necked Phalaropes.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult/winter

Similar Species Discussion

I had a direct comparison to the Red-necked Phalaropes that it was swimming with. All of the bird were in basic. The Red Phalarope was slightly larger and had an obviously shorter and much stouter bill than the Red-necked Phalaropes. The Red-necked Phalaropes also had a darker head, less black on nape and crown, and their upperparts had a more streaked appearance.
