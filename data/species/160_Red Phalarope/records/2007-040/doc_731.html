Description of the Bird

I have pictures of this bird with an American Avocet. It is about half the size of the Avocet. I could plainly see the white cheeks and red neck and red body. Clearly not a Red-necked Phalarope since the whole neck and body were red. I could also see the lighter breeding plumage bill.
Behaviors: Feeding around the edge of the little pond in the water. The Avocet flushed it once and I got a picture. The pictures are distant but ID'able.
Call: Not heard.
Plumage: Breeding plumage
