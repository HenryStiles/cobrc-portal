Description of the Bird

Small shorebird spending most of its time swimming in deep water; white underparts, pale gray upperparts, gray hindcrown, blackish patch over the eye and auricular; narrow bill held in a downward angle.
Behaviors: Foraging while swimming in open water
Call: none
Plumage: basic

Similar Species Discussion
