Description of the Bird

The following characteristics were very clear to each of us:
Large very strong bill
Yellow eye-line
Yellow on the breast
Black bib on the yellow breast - it was obviously a male in breeding plumage
White chin
Chestnut wing patch over brown wings

The bird was perched in a small sapling for about 15 minutes

Similar Species Discussion

No other species was considered.  We knew the bird but it is not common on the Western Slope and there are very few other times it was reported.
We were doing the annual Spring Bird Count and this species has never been reported on this count in all the past years starting in 1993.  I was the compiler for several years for Grand Valley Audubon and have the records for all the past years.
