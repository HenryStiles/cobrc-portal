Description of the Bird

Male adult in breeding plumage. Black V on chest, yellow breast, and supercillium, rufous shoulders, grey bill and nape. Female like pale version of male, lacking black V
Behaviors: Female landed on barbed wire fence. Male landed next to her. Female lifted her tail and male hopped on top. After copulation they sat for a few moments together before taking off.
Call: None heard
Plumage: Adult
