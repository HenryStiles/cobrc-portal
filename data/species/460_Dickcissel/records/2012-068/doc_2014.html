Description of the Bird

Male had grey nape, black V on chest, yellow breast, rufous shoulders, greyish beak. Female lacked black V and had paler of all other field marks. Around 6 or so inches.
Behaviors: Birds flew onto a fence, female displayed briefly, male hopped on top. They mated...then sat quietly for a few seconds before taking off.
Call: None heard at the time
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other Colorado bird looks like this. Am familiar with the breed, have seen it elsewhere.

Comments: We had stopped to check on the Bobolinks on County Road 360. We saw the female light and I wondered what it was...when the male lit next to her we recognized him immediately. We would be interested to know if there are any other breeding records for this bird in Huerfano County.
