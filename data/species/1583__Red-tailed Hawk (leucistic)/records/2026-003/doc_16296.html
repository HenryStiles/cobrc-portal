Description of the Bird

The bird was sitting near the top of a juniper tree for a few minutes. I took an unclear photo through the window. He flew away and I captured a better photo, again through the window. About 40 minutes later I saw it fly overhead, from the back of my house and over to the front, then he disappeared. 

My neighbor says he submitted my picture earlier today, but that he wasn’t sure if it worked. He called me while I was watching it in the tree, but he didn’t see it. 

Similar Species Discussion

Merlin bird came up with nothing so I searched the picture with Google images and it brought up pictures of other leucistic red tailed hawks, at least one from this area. 

Previous Experience

I have seen a white hawk looking creature a number of times in the area, but never close enough to get a good photograph. I saw one two or three years ago that was smaller than this, but all white, in the juniper on the other side of my house and then diving. I see what I believe are red tailed hawks all the time around my house.
