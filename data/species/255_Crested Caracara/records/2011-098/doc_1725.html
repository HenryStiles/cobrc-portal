Description of the Bird

Long-legged bird with huge bill and raptor-like appearance. Black cap that extended down to the nape. Bill huge and yellow orange with noticeable hooked tip. White cheeks extending down to upper breast where fine dark vertical streaks extended to all dark belly. Very long legs were yellow orange. When preeing could see some white towards the tip of the underwings.
Behaviors: Perched about 3 feet off of the ground. Preening for over 20 minutes.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

In South American the South American Checklist Committee splits South Caracara from "Northern Caracara" but the AOU Checklist Committee does not so there are no other species that look like this bird in the americas north of Mexico.
