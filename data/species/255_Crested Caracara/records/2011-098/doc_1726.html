Description of the Bird

I spotted the bird about a mile and half away, near the dead part of a tree on a hill east of Road 26.5. I could see the dark crest, white area in the face area, and orangish and blue tipped bill, on a raptor. The only other part of the bird I could see was the dark brown color on the wings. Luckily the species is easy to identify, as I didn't get to see as much of the bird as I would have liked. I identified by the dark crest, and white area in the face, no other big birds would look like this.
Behaviors: In a tree on a hill. Mostly could see the top of the bird, the head, face, and below that. Couldn't see legs, feet, area at all.
