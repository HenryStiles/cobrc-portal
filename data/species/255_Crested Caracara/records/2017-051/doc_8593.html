Description of the Bird

The bird was sitting in the top of a dead cottonwood along the Highline Canal.  It was a Crested Caracara.  
It perched for about 25 minutes and then took off in a north by northeast direction towards Chatfield State Park.

Similar Species Discussion

Before putting my binoculars on the bird I thought it might be an osprey but the moment I looked through my binoculars I knew what the bird was, hard to believe as it may be.
