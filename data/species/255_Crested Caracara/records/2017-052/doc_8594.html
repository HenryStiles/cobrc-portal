Description of the Bird

The bird was mostly calm and content to perch and preen.  It only moved when attacked by a Red-tailed Hawk, which happened a couple of times. 
It had an orange beak with a light blue tip, dark capped and crested head, white on the neck and also the lower portion of the front of the body.  It was dark in the middle and it had horizontal barring at the bottom portion of the white neck.  When it flew it had the white outer wing tips and white tail with the dark band.
It didn't vocalize that I could tell.
