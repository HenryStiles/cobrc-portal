Description of the Bird

Paul Morey and later 21 more birders came to view the bird.. Photos to be submited.

The bird was roughly the size of a raven, dark back and belly. White neck and throat, dark cap. The face was red. In flight white tail with dark band , wings with white tips. 

Similar Species Discussion

Very unique large bird at close range both perched and in flight made ID as easy as it gets for a rare bird. Brenda and I have seen most of the carcara species of the world and would not mistake this caracara for any of the other species of cracaras.

Previous Experience

We have seen Crested Caracara many times in the south, Mexico, Central and South America. We have seen nearly all other species of Caracaras as well and falcons in our area.
