Description of the Bird

Large bright, white swans, black feet, and sloping black bills with yellow spot at upper base of the bill. Found by R. Laroche and T. Britton earlier. its my understanding this would be a Teller county first record, thus the submission. 

Similar Species Discussion

Small yellow spot at base of bill separates from all other swan species.
