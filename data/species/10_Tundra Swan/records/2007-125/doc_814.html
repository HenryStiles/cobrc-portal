Description of the Bird

Three Large white swans with black bills, all three swans had some yellow on the lores. Posterior dorsal edge of the bill on each bird was convex and rounded. Each bird has a reddish "grin patch" or tomia. All three birds had entirely white plumage. One bird had reddish-brown staining around its head. All three birds had black legs and feet.
Behaviors: All three birds remained close together and swan the width of the reservoir and half way back during my observation. Two of the individuals remained very close while the third bird, the one with the stained head, kept near continually bobbing its head up and down. After around 45 minutes of observation, the birds tucked their bills onto their backs and went to sleep.
Call: None
