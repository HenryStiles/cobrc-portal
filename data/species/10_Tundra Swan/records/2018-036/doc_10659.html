Description of the Bird

Immature swan. Black legs, eye set apart from bill. In Tundra Swan the black line started out small, smaller than the eyeball, and stayed narrow for a ways forward.  Swimming comfortably with Snow/Ross's Geese.  Foraged on bank at one point.

Similar Species Discussion

Black legs, eye set apart from bill. On Trumpeter Swan the black area in front of the eye starts wide and continues to widen toward the bill. Leg color would be different in immature swan, Trumpeter would be larger.
