Description of the Bird

A purplish colored plump shorebird, feeding in the inlet creek.  Orange legs, down curved bill with orange color at base see my photos.  The bird was found on 16 December 2016, and seen my many on 17, 18, 19 December, 2016.

Similar Species Discussion

Rock Sandpiper - yellow legs, lack of orange on base of bill
Dunlin - has all black bill
Surfbird - Shorter bill, not down curved
