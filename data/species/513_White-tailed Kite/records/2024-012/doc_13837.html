Description of the Bird

Mike Thompson found the kite and reported it to us. Kite did not call. Kite spent most of the observed time perched. On one occasion the Kite caught some sort of prey item and perched on a post to eat it.

Small kite, mostly white, silvery upper parts, white below including tail, and dark patch through eye. Most noticeable was a black wing partch when perched and in flight on the upper wing. In fight from underneath the light feathers were gray and a dark patch could be seen in mid-wing. 

Similar Species Discussion

Mississippi Kite does occur in western Colorado rarely but is all gray without the black shoulder patch. Swallow-tailed Kites are larger and have a large swallow like tail.

Previous Experience
