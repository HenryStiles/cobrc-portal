Description of the Bird

A very conspicuous bird prone to being mobbed. Many photos. At the time of the list, the bird was located on private land but can be easily seen from NPS land at Yucca House NM by standing at these coordinates 37.2468457, -108.6848792 and scoping cottonwood snags to the south.
