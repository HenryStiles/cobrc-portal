Description of the Bird

I spotted this bird, along the canal. Bright yellow head, big black eyes, dark bill, blue/gray wings, bright yellow underparts, with white undertail coverts. See photos.
Behaviors: Stayed mostly in some old weeds along the canal.
Call: "sweet sweet sweet" heard once, actually before we saw the bird.
Plumage: adult
