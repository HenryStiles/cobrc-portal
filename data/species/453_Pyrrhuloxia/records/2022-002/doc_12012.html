Description of the Bird

Bird was flitting among trees around houses, but did not venture far.  Occasionally landed on clothesline and on hanging strands of lights.  Visited feeder on side porch of house.  Bird was heard doing the "chipping" call.

Similar Species Discussion

Northern Cardinal.  Clearly the wrong plumage to be a cardinal.
