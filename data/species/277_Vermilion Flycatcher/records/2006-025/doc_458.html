Description of the Bird

This was a small flycatcher, all red on face and undersides with a black mask. The hindneck, nape, back, tail and wings were blackish and there was two faint whitish wingbars.

As the Sibley guide says, unmistakable.
Behaviors: This bird spent a lot of time on power lines and fence posts but did some flycatching for minute insects.
Call: none
Plumage: alternate
