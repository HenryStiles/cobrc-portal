Description of the Bird

A flycatcher, with bright red, head, chin, throat, breast, belly, undertail coverts. Brownish eye line. Browish back and tail. All black bill and black legs, and dark eyes.
Behaviors: I was looking in the russian olives and noticed a male Vermilion Flycatcher. The bird flew around the russian olives and nearby power lines and fence posts.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
