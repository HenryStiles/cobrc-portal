Description of the Bird

Ric Olson and I were below the dam at Two Buttes Reservoir, when we spotted a flycatcher with pink under the tail. We quickly figured out it was a Vermilion Flycatcher. I tried to get a photo of the bird, though it flew, so the photo isn't great. The bird had a brown cap, with brown wings and tail. Also, a brown cheek patch and small dark bill. The chin and throat were white. The under parts below the throat were white with light streaks going up and down. The belly was pink, and under tail coverts were lighter pink. The legs were dark and the eyes were dark. We saw the bird well, though wasn't able to get any other photos later.
Behaviors: The bird was flycatching in trees below Two Buttes Reservoir dam.
