Description of the Bird

A small flycatcher, slightly smaller than SAPH. Brownish gray above and paler below with a distinct pinnkish wash on the flanks and lower belly. some faint streaking on the sides and breast.
Behaviors: Sitting on a fence and occasionally a tree. It made occasional sallies out to catch bugs.
Call: none noted
Plumage: adult female

Similar Species Discussion

SAPH is the only similar species. SAPH is eliminated by the streaking and the pinkish wash on the flanks
