Description of the Bird

A small active flycatcher that was mostly red. There were a few patches in the red that were brownish that led to my conclusion that the bird was a second year individual. The browish eyeline expanded through the eye to the back of the head. The wings, back and tail were also this brownish color. The tail was wagged around regularly as the bird sat in small trees between forgaing sallies. The red parts of the bird were the crown, throat, breast and belly. Bill black and eye dark.
