Description of the Bird

Small vibrant red flycatcher with black wings, nape, back, tail, and black mask. Two very faint white wing bars. Thin black bill.
Behaviors: Hunting from mullein stalks and small cottonwoods, grabbing small moths on or near the ground.
Call: not heard
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Very few other birds this bright red. Male Northern Cardidal larger, different bill shape, lacks black wings, etc. Male Scarlet Tanager bigger, different bill shape, lacks black mask, nape, and back, etc.
