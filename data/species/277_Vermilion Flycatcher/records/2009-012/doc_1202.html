Description of the Bird

Bright red bird. The red was on head, breast, belly, flanks, and undertail coverts. It had a black mask going through the eye. Black back and tail. Black legs and a small black bill.
Behaviors: It was usually sitting fairly low to half way up the trees. It would occasionally sit on top of the mullens. It would then fly from its perch down close to the ground to catch moths.
Call: didn't call or sing
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Unmistakable, but others similar:
Scarlet Tanager - Has red back, no black mask, and the bill is to large and thick.
