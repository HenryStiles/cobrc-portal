Description of the Bird

Male Vermillion Flycatcher while we were spending an afternoon at the city park in Campo during Atlas block work. Black back and wings with bright red in the belly, breast and head.

I have seen the species before at Higbee, in Arizona, Texas, Mexico, and even in the Galapagos.

There really isn't a similar bird with the red color and flycatcher behavior.
Behaviors: Behavior was typical for flycatcher with frequent sallies out for insects.
