Description of the Bird

Smallish bird that was gray/brown above with dark tail. White throat, with dusky streaking on white breast. Lower belly and undertail coverts were peachy/red. Black bill. Dark almost blackish eye mask with slight white supercilium. Dark legs.
Behaviors: Flycatching behavior, foraging from high perches in various trees.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
