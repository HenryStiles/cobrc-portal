Description of the Bird

A flycatcher with brownish back and wing, very light wing bars, dark ear patch, white throat and belly, with lightly streaking, and yellowish undertail. A dark tail. Small black bill, dark eyes. See photo.
Behaviors: Bird found along the olives south of the banding station at Chico Basin Ranch. The bird continued to go north landing in tall cottonwood trees. The bird was on the move, not sticking around much.
Call: none
Plumage: first spring
