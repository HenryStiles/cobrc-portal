Description of the Bird

An orange oriole, with big bill, black under the bill, brighter orange below the eyes, near the black under the bill. Duller orange head. Grayish/green back with streaking. Grayish wings with white wing bars. Yellowish tail. Yellowish underparts.
Behaviors: Seen in pine tree and at feeder from inside Connie Kogler's kitchen window.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Bullock's Oriole - not as bright orange on the face. Streaking on back elimnates other species.
