Description of the Bird

Large Grebe, dark grey/black back and top of head. Head double crested. White face, red neck.
Behavior: Observed on several occasions over a four day period. Floating on the surface, moving around the lake, foraging, diving to feed on occasion. Stayed alone, did not mingle with numerous Western Grebes courting and feeding on the lake.
Call: N/A
Plumage: Summer breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Western Grebes do not have striking red necks.
