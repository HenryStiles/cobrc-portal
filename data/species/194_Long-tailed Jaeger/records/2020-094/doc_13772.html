Description of the Bird

I Amy Hudechek found this bird. I was alone when I saw this bird. I photographed it for 5 minutes. I have mult pictures I will add.

I was new to birding at the time. I enjoyed photographing bird. I was photographing an Avocet when I heard a bird calling and appeared to be coming at the avocet. I thought it was a juvenile gull but seemed weird to me so I kept photographing it. He was basically gray all over with barring. He had black on the wings. His tail was white with gray strips on the top with black tips, His legs were bluish in color.  His beak was mostly gray with a black and a sharp downward tip. 

He flew around the pond several times, landed on the side and then took off. 

Similar Species Discussion

I thought it was some type of gull.  Merlin helped, told me it was a Jaeger. I didn't believe Merlin and submitted it to a facebook group that helps ID birds and a few friends who knew more then I did about birds.  They all agreed it was a Long-tailed Jaeger.
