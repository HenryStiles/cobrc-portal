Description of the Bird

A jaeger with long wings , broad tail same width as wing depth , very long central tail streamers , small black cap and white underparts. Upperwing dark gray with white flashes on two outermost primaries ( also showing on underwing ). Underwing also showing black edging on trailing edge of secondaries.
Behaviors: Either sitting on surface of water feeding on insects . Every 10 minutes or so would chase a CAGU or RBGU and force them to disgorge a small fish or other food item. On chase extremely agile flight behaviour with sudden twists and turns.
Call: No call heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other jaeger or gull has this combination of very long tail streamers , small black cap and wing pattern.
