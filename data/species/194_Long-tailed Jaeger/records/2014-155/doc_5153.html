Description of the Bird

adult; seen at close range harassing gulls on shore at swim beach; also seen floating on water opposite the jet ski launch and also in the south end of the lake. About the size of Parasitic Jaeger and similar in plumage but lacking white patch under the wings, and with white marks on the upper wing restricted to two shafts of outermost primaries; black cap more extensive that that of Parasitic Jaeger. Central tail feathers long and pointed, extending about 6 inches beyond length of tail. Otherwise dark brown upperparts, and gray underparts becoming white on breast, neck and head (except for black cap).

Similar Species Discussion

No other jaeger has such long tail streamers.
