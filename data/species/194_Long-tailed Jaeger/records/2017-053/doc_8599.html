Description of the Bird

The LT Jaeger was almost the first bird that I saw after pulling up to the boat launch on the nw side of Antero. The bird was a couple hundred meters off, but due to color (dull and grayish) I was thinking stongly of Long-tailed Jaeger. I drove to a spot considerably closer (still 100 meters, very roughly). The gray plumage with crisp pale edging was quite evident. The bird was too dull for Parasitic, too small for Pom. Then it flew, headed right at me, going directly over my head. See photos

Similar Species Discussion

Pomarine and Parasitic eliminated by only two white shafts on primaries and dark secondary bar contrasting with paler gray secondary wing coverts (above)
