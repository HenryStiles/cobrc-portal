Description of the Bird

The bird accompanied a flock of about 20 Western Tanagers and returned to my yard three times during the day.  I have photos from each visit at 8:47am, 3:14 pm, 
and 5:44 pm.  I have a video from the 3:14 pm visit.  The appearance of the bird is classic Hepatic Tanager, red, with strong gray bill, dark lores, and dusky auriculars.

(I have several excellent photos and a video, but have been unable to attach them to this document.   I can text them to anyone with a cell phone.  My number is (719)200-6019.)

Similar Species Discussion

Summer Tanager doesn't have the dusky auriculares and dark bill of this bird.
