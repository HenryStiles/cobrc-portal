Description of the Bird

Large about AMGO size , tanager shape and jizz , basically red-orange all over with dusky gray auriculars and grayish upper back , no wing bars , stout dark gray bill ,dark lores.
Behaviors: Moving about every 5 minutes or so singing from higher Pondorosa Pines above PJ . Came in immeadiately when I played back the song.
Call: Song was sweeter tha other tanagers with little hoarseness and quite similar to Black-headed Grosbeak . Call note however was a single note chup nothing like the call notes of the other likely tanagers or BHGR .
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Summer Tanager prefers riparian habitat , does not have dusky auriculars , has more hoarseness in song and has a very different call and red plumage in male is a deeper red.
