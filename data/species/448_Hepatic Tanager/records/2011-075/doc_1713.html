Description of the Bird

Adult male was AMGO size , tanager shape , reddish orange all over with dusky gray auriculars and grayish upper back , no wing bars , stout gray bill, dark lores. Adult female had a dull yellow throat , upper breast , forehead and tail , grayish auriculars , flanks and upper back , stout blackish bill , no wing bars , same size and jizz of male.
Behaviors: both birds came into to a very short playback with the male perched on top of juniper and pinyon pines counter singing and acting territorialy .
Call: Song sweeter than other tanagers with no hoarseness , similar to BHGR .
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

SUTA prefers riparian habitat , does not have dusky auriculars , more horseness in song , different call note , adult female SUTA is completely pale yellow with a longer bill. Just wrong size and shape for NOCA male.
