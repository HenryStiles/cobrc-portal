Description of the Bird

Bill Maynard and I were looking at tanagers in a tree, when I spotted this tanager, with dark bill, dark ear patches, and dark flanks.  See Bill Maynard's photos.  Mark Yaeger found the bird yesterday, the 20 of May, so we were looking for it, and hoping to find it and photographed it, since it is a review species for Pueblo County.  Only my 3rd in the county.

Similar Species Discussion

Summer Tanager - has light colored bill, not dark cheeks and flanks.
Western Tanager - has wing bars
Scarlet Tanager - has black wings and tail
