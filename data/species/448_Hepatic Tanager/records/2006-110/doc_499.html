Description of the Bird

It was red with dark auriclars, back, flanks, and upper tail. Flat headed compared to Summer Tanager. It also had a dark bill. The red on the breast and belly was not as bright of red as on the throat and head.
Behaviors: A pair was flying back and forth to the nest and they were feeding the nestlings.
Call: I can't remember if I heard one or not.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Summer Tanager - male is all red with a lighter bill. They have a slight crest. The red is brighter than most of the red on the Hepatic. Female is all yellow. The yellow is not as dark of yellow as that of the Hepatic. It also has a light bill.
Scarlet Tanager - Bright red everywhere except the wings and tail. It has a small gray bill. Female is yellow with dark wings and tail with a small yellowish gray bill.
Northern Cardinal - bright red bird with a large crest and a black face. Large red bill. Female is a grayish brown color with a red tail, crest, wings, and bill. Also has a blackish face.
