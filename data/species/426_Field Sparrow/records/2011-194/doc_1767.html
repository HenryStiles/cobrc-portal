Description of the Bird

I was standing at corner in road near Black Hollow Reservoir on 5 May 2011, when I heard a Field Sparrow sing. I looked down the fence line, and about 150 feet or so away, I could see a Spizella sparrow singing (the above mentioned song). The bird had a rusty cap and bright white eyering on generally plain face. Bill pink
Behaviors: see description
Call: Bouncing ball song typical of this species
Plumage: adult
