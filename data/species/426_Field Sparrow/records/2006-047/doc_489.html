Description of the Bird

A small sparrow of the Spizella genus. Long tail plain gray breast and heavily patterned grays browns and tans on the dorsal surface. light wing bar. Plain gray face with small pink bill. The crown was patterened with rufous and gray stripes the exact arrangement of which I don't recall. The eye hada complete thin eye-ring around it. larger and brighter than most BRSP would show.
Behaviors: Flew past me and landed on a small shrub
Call: none noted
Plumage: definitive

Similar Species Discussion

The only other similar species are Spizellas and Rufous-crowned Sparrow. The pink bill eliminates all these as well as the complete thin whitish eye-ring. Plain gray face.
