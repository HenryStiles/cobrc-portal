Description of the Bird

Crown and back rusty-brown. The sides of the head were quite gray. Distinct white eye-ring with rusty-brown streak behind eye. Two wing bands. Brown tail. Underparts whitish and unstreaked. Pink bill and pink legs.
Behaviors: In with a large flock of Brewer's and Clay-colored Sparrows. Flying out into an open ag field to feed and then coming back to some large deciduous trees where it would sing quite distinctly from the Brewer's
Call: as described in Nat. Geo., "clear, plaintive whistles accelerating into a trill."
Plumage: adult
