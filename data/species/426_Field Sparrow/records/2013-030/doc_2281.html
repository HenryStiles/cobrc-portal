Description of the Bird

Small spizella type sparrow with unmarked pale grayish breast ,gray face , pale eye ring , pink bill and reddish brown crown stripe.
Behaviors: Actively feeding along a drainage ditch in small willows and grasses with other sparrow species with White-crowned the predominant . Small size in comparison to White-crowned noticeable.
Call: None given.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

American Tree Sparrow has a bicolored bill , and a rufous eye line , and a central breast spot.
