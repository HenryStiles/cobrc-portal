Description of the Bird

Two birds, at Chico Basin Ranch.  One at the Headquarters Pond and one at Rose Pond.  

Both were plain faced small sparrows with with bold eye ring and pink bill. See photo from the one at Rose Pond at Chico Basin Ranch.

Similar Species Discussion

None really have plain face and bold eye ring, with pink bill.

Previous Experience

I've seen lots of Field Sparrows in Eastern Colorado, only a couple of times before in Pueblo County.  Field Sparrows are rare along the Front Range counties.
