Description of the Bird

This bird was seen on a day when 180 Plegadis ibis were present. I thought it was a hybrid, as it seemed to have skin lines that were too weak for a Glossy and pale feathering far too limited for a WFIB. The eye seemed dark in the field. The knee joints fairly bright (not vis in photos), but legs otherwise fairly dull.
Behaviors: feeding in wet grassy marsh/field by probiing
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult Alternate

Similar Species Discussion

See description. Essentially, the bird looks more GLIB like than WFIB like, but the facial lines are weak, for what they are, and have a pinkish hue... plus there is some pinkish-white feathering around the eye.
