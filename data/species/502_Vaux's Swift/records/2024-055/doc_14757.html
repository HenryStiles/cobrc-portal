Description of the Bird

Picked up on the bird booking it going south, immediate impressions were of an erratic flight style and that it was very small. Tiny swift with short (but fairly broad at the base) wings and a very short tail. Our whole group noted the obvious pale rump while in flight, as well as a very pale throat/chest.
