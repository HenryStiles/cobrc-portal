Description of the Bird

Photos clearly show uniform lighter-toned body and more broad, swallow-like wing shape vs CHSW which can have a lighter-toned throat and wings are more narrow and swift-like. Post-list eBird research shows Vaux’s have been documented near Mexican Hat (60 miles SW) at 2 locations on Sept 23, 2016.
