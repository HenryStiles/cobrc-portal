Description of the Bird

Gull considerably smaller than adjacent California Gull , mid gray mantle , white secondaries , dark primaries extending beyond tail , small head with long dark bill drooped at tip . Some dark streaking around back of head and appeared to have some gray wash on sides of breast but this was difficult to confirm.
Behavior: Walking slowing along shoreline feeding on flies.
Call: None heard .
Plumage: adult none breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Franklin's gull has small bill and in any plumage would have a dark half hood .Mew Gull has smaller bill , yellow tipped black.
