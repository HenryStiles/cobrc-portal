Description of the Bird

Medium sized gull with long wings, long black bill, medium gray above and white below. White head with gray markings behind the eye and crown. Mostly dark wing-tips.

Similar Species Discussion

Franklin's gull was eliminated because it has more white on the wing-tips and shorter and thinner bill.
