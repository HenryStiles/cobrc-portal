Description of the Bird

Medium-sized gull. White head with grayish nape. blackish marking from nape and ear-coverts to just in front of eye. White eye-crescents. Dark gray mantle. Outer primaries entirely black with no white spots. White tail. Bill black, long and with a slight droop.
Behaviors: Swimming with Franklin's Gulls
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult in basic

Similar Species Discussion

Swimming with Franklin's Gulls so I had a direct comparison. Larger and stockier than Franklin's with a longer, thinner bill that drooped. Larger white eye crescents. Longer more pointed wings than Franklin's. No white in primaries.
