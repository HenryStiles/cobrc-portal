Description of the Bird

       A black- headed gull seen flying over the water and perched on the mud flats. The head had a solid black hood with white eye-arcs above and below. The breast was pure white with no pinkish hue. The tail was plain white. In flight, the upper wing was grey with solid black tips on the primaries. No white spots on the black tips. The under sides of the wings were white with solid black primaries. The bill was large and thick (as compared to a Franklin Gull) and showed a slight downturn.*


*Copied from my ebird entry on 5/22/14

Similar Species Discussion

Breeding Bonaparte Gull:   Laughing Gull larger than BOGU.  Larger, redish bill.  Primaries dark above and on undersides.  

Breeding Franklin Gull:  Laughing Gull did not have pinkish on breast.  Heavier bill with drooped tip.  Black primaries dark above with no white band or spots.  Extensive black on underside of primaries.
