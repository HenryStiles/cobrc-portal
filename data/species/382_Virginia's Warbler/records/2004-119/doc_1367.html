Description of the Bird

Plumage was identical to an adult female Virginia's warbler. The bird was overall a dull gray in appearance, with her back being slightly darker than her underparts. Distinctive, complete eye ring. Yellow undertail and uppertail coverts. Small yellow wash on breast.
Behaviors: The bird was bathing.
Call: None heard
Plumage: Adult female
