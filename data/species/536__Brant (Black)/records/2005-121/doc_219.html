Description of the Bird

Small short-necked goose similar in size to accompanying "Richardson's" Cackling Geese (Branta hutchinsii hutchinsii); Black head, neck breast, with a white mark on the side of the neck. Stubby black bill, similar in shape to bill of Cackling Goose. Dark brownish-gray sides with several whitish vertical streaks. Bright white lower flanks, vent and undertail coverts. Dark gray brown back and wings.
Behaviors: Swimming on a lake alongside Cackling Geese.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other goose has all dark head and breast with black bill and white mark on neck.

Dark brownish-gray color on sides indicated that this is a Black Brant, Branta bernicla nigricans, rather than a "pale-belliled" Brant, B.b. bernicla.
