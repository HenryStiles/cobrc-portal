Description of the Bird

In flock of Canada Geese, initially noted due to small size; zoomed in with scope to see a bird with entirely black head, neck, and chest except white "necklace" on throat that looked complete (though can not exclude small break). Back nearly equally dark as head/neck/chest. Black on chest extending onto belly, narrowing and fading posteriorly towards legs. Broad side patches. Bill black and relatively small for goose. Vent white. Legs dark, probably black
Behaviors: walking among flock of (mainly) Lesser Canada Geese
Call: not heard
Plumage: not determined

Similar Species Discussion

None, really. Black Brant. Too dark beneath for Atlantic or "Gray-bellied". Side patches too extensive for Dark-bellied Brent Goose
