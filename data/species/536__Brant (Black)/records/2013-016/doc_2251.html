Description of the Bird

Dark mostly black goose with white necklace and bright white undertail. Significantly smaller than nearby snow geese. Belly more pale than neck, but darker underneath.
Behaviors: Associating loosely with Snow Geese.
Call: None
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Too much black for any other Colorado goose. The dark belly identifies this bird as a Black Brant.
