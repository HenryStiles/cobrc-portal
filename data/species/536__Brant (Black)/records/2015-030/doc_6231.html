Description of the Bird

Bird was noticably smaller, stubbier billed and darker than Canada Geese it was with. All dark head and beak with white necklace, black chest and belly, large white patch on side. Long white tail coverts seen well in flight. No terminal band.

Similar Species Discussion

No white patch on face eliminated Cackling. Size and no white patch eliminated much larger Canada. Eliminated from dark juvenile snow goose because of white necklace and bright white patch on side. Wing pattern completely different from snow goose. This bird had dark wings with thin white stripes on outer wings, snow goose would have shown broad white patches in wing while in flight.
