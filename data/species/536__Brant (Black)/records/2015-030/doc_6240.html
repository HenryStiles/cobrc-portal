Description of the Bird

Lisa Edwards and I went to Monte Vista N.W.R., and went south of there, to a crane viewing pull out, on the east side of the highway.  We quickly noticed some geese in the grass, fairly close, and one of them was the Brant, that has been present for a few weeks.  Photos taken.  See photos.
