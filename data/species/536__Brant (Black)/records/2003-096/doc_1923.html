Description of the Bird

A goose like bird which was overall a dark gray with the exception of the underparts from behind the legs to the tail. The white neck collar was very faint suggesting to me that the bird was in juvenile plumage. Soft parts were black or dark gray also. The upper wing coverts were tip in a thin line of white. Overall size of bird was about 2/3 the size of the Canada Geese that were with it.
Behaviors: I don't remember the behavior other than it was feeding on the grass with the Canada Geese.
