Description of the Bird

Small, dark goose. Head entirely black, iris dark, rich brown. Bill medium long for a goose and black. Thin white collar that appears to completely circle the neck, but collar is patchy and broken. Neck and breast black. Back of bird dark brown (darker than CAGO.) Flanks brown, with thin white bordering arcs above brown flanks. White tail coverts. Black primaries. 4 thin white pale bars in tertials (juvenile trait.)
Behaviors: Bird was feeding with Mallards and Canada Geese.
Call: n/a
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

I don't believe that any other duck or goose has these plumage traits. Additionally I see nothing to suggest hybrid characteristics.
