Description of the Bird

The bird was a smaller goose, with a black head and neck with a white ring around the neck just below the head.  Most of th rest of the body was a dark gray, and the vent was white.


Similar Species Discussion

Cackling Goose has a white cheek patch, and not a white ring around the neck.
