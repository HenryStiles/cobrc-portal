Description of the Bird

Two small, dark geese with small heads and bills. Black head, bill, breast, primaries, and tail. White undertail coverts. White, incomplete necklace around middle of black neck. Never got a great look at the belly but from what I could see it appeared dark, consistent with "Black" Brant. Age determined by mantle feathers with whitish distal margins, side of body blotchy. Upperwing-coverts with whitish ends. Secondaries and inner primaries tipped with white.
Behaviors: Swimming with Canada Geese
Call: not heard
Plumage: juvenile
