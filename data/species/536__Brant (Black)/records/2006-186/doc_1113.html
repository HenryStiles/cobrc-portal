Description of the Bird

Ellie discovered this bird among about 200 plus Canada Geese. Very dark, smaller duck. Black head, neck, front, belly. Very white tail area contrasting with very black (or dark) feathers above the bright white (primaries?). White band around upper neck below head. Its sides or flanks were white streaked by fingers of gray (vertical stripes). Very dark small goose, very dark on front to belly, this was my limited reaction to seeing the Brant and I yelled out "Brant!"
