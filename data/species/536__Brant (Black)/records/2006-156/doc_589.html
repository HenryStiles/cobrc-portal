Description of the Bird

A small dark goose, with thin white stripe around the neck. It had a small bill, that was black, all dark head, dark back, dark neck and belly. White under tail coverts.
Behaviors: Swimming on the water and feeding the grass, with other Canada Geese and a immature Greater White-fronted Goose.
Call: none
Plumage: adult
