Habitat

This is a city park with lots of yard type grass, some large trees and a large lake and small pond.

Viewing Conditions

Distance:  30 to 50 feet
Light:  no issues

Description of the Bird

Complete black head and neck, could not see white neck bank, white wing bars, high contrast flanks and undertail when compared with front of bird, smaller in size than other geese in area (see pictures)
Behaviors: Observed bird very close by, many other white cheeked geese in area, bird was grazing on the grass within the park
Call: none noted
Plumage: Immature. No white neck band

Similar Species Discussion

1. Direct comparison with other geese in same area namely Canada Geese.
