Viewing Conditions

Optics:  binoculars, camera

Light:  early morning sun on southeast horizon, bird viewed toward south

Description of the Bird

goose, smaller than nearby Canada geese, all black head, black breast and belly, white necklace, vertical white side stripes
Behaviors: The bird was initially swimming among Canada geese. Then, I was pleasantly surprised to see it swim and then walk toward me. After being out of the water for a few minutes, it flew northwestward with many of the Canada geese.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The all black head and white necklace are quite distinctive.
