Description of the Bird

Shorebird about same size as adjacent Baird's, longer yellow legs , buffy breast and face , black eye and fairly short dark straight bill , scaly back . Walking amonst short grass vegetation pecking at surface . In flight showed dark rump and pale underwing with dark trailing edges .

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing similar has this combination of sandy buffy breast and face , dark eye and short bill.
