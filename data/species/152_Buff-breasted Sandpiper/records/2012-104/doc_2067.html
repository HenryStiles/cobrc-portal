Description of the Bird

Larger than BASA, upright posture, bright ochre-buff on chest, upper belly fading posteriorly. Plain face, yellow legs. scaly back, dark crown, spotting on sides of chest. See photo. Two birds present on mudflats, about 100 yards apart. Birds looked pretty much identical as far as I could tell
Behaviors: One was standing on sandy portion of flats near Killdeer, standing upright, walking, bending over to pick here and there at sandflat surface. The other was high up on mudflat with a number of various peeps. Feeding style same.
Call: note heard
Plumage: juvenile
