Description of the Bird

Unique shape and color , makes this an obvious ID. Medium size shorebird , dark black eye on buffy face , underparts also buffy with black spotting on breast , yellow legs , dark scaly back with white fringing indicates that these birds were juveniles .
Behaviors: In a small flock of Baird's would run a few paces and then peck at the mud surface for insects with their relatively short black straight bill. Did not probe below surface,
Call: None Heard
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

No other shorebird has this combination of features.If you having a really bad day , a juvenile Ruff comes closest but this has a longer neck , longer body , longer bill etc.
