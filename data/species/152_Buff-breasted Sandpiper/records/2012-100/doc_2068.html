Description of the Bird

Medium small shorebirds (larger than Baird's, smaller than Stilt Sand), bright buff beneath to mid belly, fading to white posteriorly. Short straight bill. Plain face. Juv pattern on back. Legs yellow. Some spotting at sides of breast. Crown spotted, otherwise head unmarked.
Behaviors: Walked among W Phalaropes, yellowegs, peeps on mudflats picking at potential food items
Call: not heard
Plumage: juvenile
