Description of the Bird

juv; buffy below through upper belly fading to white towards vent. Scaly upperparts. Plain face, streaked cap. Yellowish legs, upright posture. Some spotting at sides of chest. Larger than Baird's, smaller than Killdeer. Straight relatively short bill for non-plover shorebird
Behaviors: Wandering around on sandier area of flats, picking at potential food items. At times near Killdeer, thought typically on own
Call: not heard
Plumage: juvenile
