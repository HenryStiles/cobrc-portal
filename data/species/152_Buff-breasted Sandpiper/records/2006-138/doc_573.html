Description of the Bird

A shorebird with buffy underparts and yellow legs. Taller than Baird's Sandpiper, and smaller than Killdeer.
Behaviors: Walking along the shore with other shorebirds.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Ruff - is taller.
Baird's Sandpiper - is smaller with black legs.
