Description of the Bird

Approximately 7" inches. Distinctly streaked rufous and gray back. Had well defined rufous spots. Crown and auriculars patterned rufous on gray. Black streaks on flanks
Behaviors: Bird scratched itself a spot and sat down for a moment. Scratched the ground like a towhee
Call: Non heard
Plumage: Red Taiga
