Description of the Bird

Crown and auriculars patterned rufous on gray. Distinctly streaked rufous and gray on back. Whitish wing bars
Behaviors: The bird arrived at my feeder and promptly dug itself a little hole and sat there for a moment before getting up and scratching at the area beneath the feeder. It foraged around on the ground.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: Adult Red Taiga

Similar Species Discussion

Arrived with a Song Sparrow. At first I assumed they were the same...until I took a closer look...photographs confirmed Fox Sparrow

Resources Used

Previous Experience

We have one or two Song Sparrows in the yard every winter. I have seen Fox Sparrows only in Alaska
