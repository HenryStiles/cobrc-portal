Similar Species Discussion

Rusty well marked face, white wing bar, gray and rust streaked back, moderately streaked underparts without area of confluence on flanks/sides eliminates other subspecies of Fox Sparrow. Relatively large size and lack of central breast spot, in tandem with above, eliminates other species of sparrows
