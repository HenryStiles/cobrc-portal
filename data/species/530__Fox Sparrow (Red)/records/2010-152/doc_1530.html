Description of the Bird

similar in size to a Hermit Thrush - at first glance that's what I thought it was.
-overall appearance reddish /rufous color with gray rump and red/rufous stripes across back;
-reddish rufous triangular shaped face patch with top edge connected behind eye with very narrow black line;
- three white patches on throat - one in center just under bill and a white "mustache"
-bicolored bill - bill gray on top with lower yellow
-gray head with hint of rufous crown;
-red/rufous wings with narrow whitish wing bars;
-reddish/rufous tail;
-dark streaks on white flanks
-dark rufous streaking and spots on white breast;
-white lower under belly
-pink legs
Behaviors: -similar feeding action to a towhee (scratching through fallen leaves)
-was uncovering and eating sunflower seeds on ground that had fallen from feeder
-stayed more under brush than in the open
Call: not heard
Plumage: Red Taiga

Similar Species Discussion

-color similar to Hermit thrush at first glance, but markings definitely different (striped back, gray rump, face patch, breast streaking that thrush does not have
-similar to Song Sparrow but a little larger and more red in appearance;
-Song Sparrow has more streaks on breast as opposed to more spotted on the Fox Sparrow
-face patterns different
