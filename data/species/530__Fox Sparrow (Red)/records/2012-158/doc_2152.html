Description of the Bird

Two large reddish sparrows. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying low in the bushes/trees with other sparrows.
Call: Giving a loud call, that reminded me of Kentucky Warbler. I heard the call before seeing the birds, and I thought it was a Fox Sparrow.
Plumage: Winter

Similar Species Discussion

The bird was a large sparrow, too large for Song Sparrow. The Rocky Mountain race of Fox Sparrow has an all gray head, which these birds didn't.
