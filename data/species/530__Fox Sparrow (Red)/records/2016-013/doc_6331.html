Description of the Bird

Bird appeared from behind the platform feeder at Red Rocks Trading Post. It foraged for sunflower seed and mixed with other sparrows.

Similar Species Discussion

At first, I thought it was a Song Sparroe, but as I looked closer, the gray & red tones, as well as lack of spot in center of chest elimminated Song Sparow.

Resources Used

I used my Golden Birds of North America, as well as other experienced birders present.

Previous Experience

This was the first time I had seen the Fox Sparrow. At the time (and still,) I have not got a lot of experience with Sparrows.
