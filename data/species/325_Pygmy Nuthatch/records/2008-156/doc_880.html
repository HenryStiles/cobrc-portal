Description of the Bird

Small bird with straight needle bill with white belly and gray top with black eyeline. No question of ID. Got some pictures which are attached.
Behaviors: Running in a small pack with quick jerky movements. Climbing all over the pine tree with there feet. Able to hand upside down with their good grip.
Call: The high clear short and rapid pip of the flock was heard.
Plumage: N/A

Similar Species Discussion

Much smaller than a WBNU and no dark top.
