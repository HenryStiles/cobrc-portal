Description of the Bird

The birds were walking on the ice and in the grass area looking for food.  They were at the second bridge at the last parking lot after the Plum Creek Delta picnic area. They were making their call sound on Sibley Birds App.

Resources Used

Sibley Birds v2 on my smartphone and my Sibley Birds West book.

Previous Experience

I have seen this species on the east coast, where I used to live.  I had one in my yard under my feeders.
