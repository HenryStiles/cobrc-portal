Description of the Bird

A small sparrow, flushed up from the marsh. The cream colored central crown stripe, purplish streaked nape, eliminate Nelson's Sparrow and all other sparrows. See photos.
Behaviors: Secretive small sparrow in the marsh.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile molting

Similar Species Discussion

Nelson's Sparrow doesn't have the creamy colored central crown stripe or the streaked purple colored nape, among other things.

Previous Experience

I've mostly seen Le Conte's Sparrows in the fall and winter in previous years in eastern Colorado. I've seen them in North Dakota in the summer as well.
