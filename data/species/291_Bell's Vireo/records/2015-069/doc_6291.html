Description of the Bird

 Small Vireo with vireo bill singing scratchy song . gray head and back , one pale wing bar , whitish throat and upper breast , yellowish wash to underbelly and flanks .bobed tail side to side when singing so likely eastern race . Sang characteristic BEVI song from tops of russian olive bushes moving changing position every few minutes . First found by Brandon Percival earlier in week .

Similar Species Discussion

 No other vireo has this song .
