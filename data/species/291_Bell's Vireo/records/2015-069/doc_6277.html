Description of the Bird

 I saw/heard this bird several times between 24 May and 31 May 2015.  Stan Oswald reports that it is still present on 2 June 2005.


I was walking around the 3rd Valco Pond, when I was hearing what sounded like a Bell's Vireo in the distance to the south.  When I got closer, I was able hear it better, and was sure it was a Bell's Vireo.  Their song is distinctive.  I got off the trail and walking in the Russian Olives, so I could get photos of the bird, since it is a rarity in Pueblo County.  I was able to get photos of it on 24 May 2015.  


Several other birders came to see and or hear it from 25 May to at least 2 June 2015.  These birders were David Chartier, Leon Bright, Lisa Edwards, Robb Hinds, Bill Maynard, Mark Peterson, and Stan Oswald.  There may have been more people.
