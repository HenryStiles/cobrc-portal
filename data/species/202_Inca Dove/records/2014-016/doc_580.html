Habitat

The bird sat near water feature made of natural rock in open weedy area (not incorporated into yard yet but just outside of lawn). The farm headquarters is the only green spot for several miles in all directions.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  10x50 field binoculars
Distance:  approximately 30-40'
Light:  Good

Description of the Bird

-Typical dove shape, but smaller
-Pale face
-Reddish cast to eye
-Dark edged feathers across back and wings appear scale like
-Possibly immature because of pale gray color and breast feathers not as striated as adult
Behaviors: -only observed sitting near the water
Call: Not noted
Plumage: Immature or female

Similar Species Discussion

Other doves seen at this location (Mourning Dove, White-winged Dove and Eurasian Collared-dove) were much larger than this bird. The "scaly" appearance is unique.
