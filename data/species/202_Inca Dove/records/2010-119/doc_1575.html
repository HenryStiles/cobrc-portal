Description of the Bird

It was a small dove. It had a small dark bill and a red eye. The color of the bird was a tan color. It had a long tail. It had scaly edges on the back, face, and belly. It had a pale pink feet and legs. In flight I was able to see rufous underwings and white on the outer tail feathers.
Behaviors: I was sitting in a thick row of junipers. When I finally was able to see it the bird flew north to a farm yard, I am guessing I accidentally flushed it off. I wasn’t able to obtain photos.
Call: It was giving a two part song that sounded like NO HOPE which was being repeated over and over.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

First off the song eliminates all other dove and birds.The rufous in the wings eliminated all doves other than Common Ground-Dove, Ruddy Ground-Dove, and Key West Quail-Dove. The long tail eliminates both Ground-Doves. With the scaly patterned feathers it helps eliminate all other dove other than Common Ground-Doves, but the thin dark bill eliminates the Common Ground-Dove.
