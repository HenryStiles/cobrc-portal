Description of the Bird

Small dove with scaly pattern covering the body with a long tail. When the dove flew the wings were rufous and the outer tail feathers were white.
Behaviors: The dove was checking out our feeders. When the dove would fly it it wings gave off a rattling sound.
Call: Repeated two note call several times on several occasions at different locations while in our yard.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The most common dove present is the Mourning Dove. Mourning Dove has different call and does not have rufous wings. Mourning Dove is not scaly and in flight wings squeak but do not rattle. We have had White-winged Doves and Band-tailed Pigeons but both species are much larger. Ground Doves have a very short tail and is not likely.
