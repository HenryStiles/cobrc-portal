Description of the Bird

This single bird was working along the shoreline at Barr Lake.
Very pale grayish color, yellow legs. Short black bill.
Feeding was typical of a plover, not a sandpiper.

Similar Species Discussion

Basic body form and manner of walking eliminated sandpipers.
The tiny bill and colors matched Piping.
Too small and quiet for Killdeer.
Gray, not brown, color eliminated Semi-palmated.
Yellow legs eliminated Snowy Plover.
