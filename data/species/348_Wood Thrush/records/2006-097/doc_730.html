Description of the Bird

Bright reddish-brown upperparts including head and nape. Slightly more brown on the back and wings. Tail more olive. Large white eye-ring. Sides of face streaked. Underparts white with large, round dark spots on the breast and sides of the throat that extended along the flanks. Dark eyes. Upper mandible very dark, lower mandible paler. Pink legs.
Behaviors: Skulking in low vegetation and would occasionally fly.
Call: Heard very briefly
Plumage: adult
