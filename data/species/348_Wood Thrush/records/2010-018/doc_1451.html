Description of the Bird

A thrush with reddish/brown colored back, and black round spots on white underparts. See photos.
Behaviors: staying on the ground most of the time.
Call: heard giving "pep pep" call.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Brown Thrasher has longer tail, same color back however.

Veery lacks the black spots on the underparts, other wise has a similar back color.
