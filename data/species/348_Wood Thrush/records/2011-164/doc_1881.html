Description of the Bird

Nick Moore yelled "Wood Thrush", while he, Lisa Edwards, John Drummond, and I were at Haxton City Park. We had just seen a Northern Parula and Golden-crowned Kinglet. The thrush was mostly staying on the ground in someone's yard just south of the park. The bird had a very reddish brown colored back, short tail, white underparts with bold black spots. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying on the ground mostly, also jumped up to the fence.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Veery -- same color back, no black spots on front
Brown Thrasher -- much longer tail, spots are more like streaks, than spots
