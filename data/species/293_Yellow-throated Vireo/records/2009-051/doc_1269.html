Description of the Bird

A vireo with yellow throat, spectalces, white underparts, thick bill. See photos.
Behaviors: Singing from the top of my front yard locust tree.
Call: a loud vireo type song
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other vireos have bright yellow throat and white underparts. The bill was thick, and not thin, so it wasn't a warbler.
