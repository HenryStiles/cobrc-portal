Description of the Bird

large shorebird, about 20% smaller than an Avocet; gray head with white supercillium line; long, bi-colored (orange at the base) upturned bill; dark red breast; dark back black tail with heavy barring under the tail.
Behaviors: feeding on shoreline
Call: n.a.
Plumage: adult, alternate
