Description of the Bird

Large shorebird, brick red on underparts, gray upperparts and head, moderately long dark legs, long thin bicolored, slightly upturned bill, dark with pink base; in flight, white wingstripe, black underwing linings.
Behaviors: standing, sleeping, foraging in shallow water; flying short distances
Call: none
Plumage: alternate

Similar Species Discussion

Bill shape and color and body size indicates a godwit; body color rules out Marbled Godwit; wing morphology in flight rules out Bar-tailed and Black-tailed Godwits.
