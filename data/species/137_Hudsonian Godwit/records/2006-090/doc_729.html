Description of the Bird

Large shorebird. Long, slightly upcurved bill that was bicolored. Orange at base, darker at tip. Dark chestnut below with fine barring. Gray head with white supercilium. Dark upperparts with white spotting.
Behaviors: Feeding at the edge of a small reservoir.
