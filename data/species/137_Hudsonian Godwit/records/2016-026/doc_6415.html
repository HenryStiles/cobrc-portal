Description of the Bird


Stephany McNew and I drove to Lake Holbrook, hoping to see the godwits, both Marbled and Hudsonian, that Stan Oswald reported the day before.  We scoped the NW side of Lake Holbrook at 7:25am, and didn't see any godwits.  We drove to the NE corner of the lake, and saw 15 Marbled Godwits.  We returned to the NW corner of Lake Holbrook at 9:20am, where the Hudsonian Godwit was present.  It was smaller than the Marbled Godwits, that we just saw.  The brick red underparts and white eye line were keys to why this bird was a Hudsonian Godwit.  See photos that I took on this cloudy morning.
