Description of the Bird

Smaller godwit, seen with Marbled, when it flew, had black and white in the tail, unlike Marbled Godwit.

Similar Species Discussion

The other godwits are bigger.

Resources Used

None

Previous Experience

I've seen Hudsonian Godwits in Colorado a few times, also many in Central Kansas in the Spring, and many in Anchorage area of Alaska in June.  I've seen many Marbled Godwits, including at the time with this Hudsonian Godwit.  I've seen Bar-tailed Godwit in Alaska.  I haven't seen Black-tailed Godwit.
