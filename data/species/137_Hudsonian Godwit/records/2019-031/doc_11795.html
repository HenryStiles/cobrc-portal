Description of the Bird

Found by Steve Mlodinow after I pointed out two MAGOs landing amid trees
A small godwit -- noticeably smaller than nearby MAGOs -- with noticeable primary projection; long, upturned, bicolored bill; gray upperparts; grayish underparts with splotches of undetermined darker color; black tail; white wing stripe; black wing linings

Similar Species Discussion

Obviously smaller and very differently colored than MAGO; black wing linings rule out Black-tailed Godwit
