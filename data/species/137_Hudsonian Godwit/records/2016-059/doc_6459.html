Description of the Bird

Large shorebird, with modestly upturned and distinctly bi-colored bill (pink basally, black distally). Dark rust beneath, grayer on face. Compared with nearby marbled godwit, shorter bill and shorter legs. Darker overall color. In-flight, blackish primaries with prominent white wing stripe. White tail-base with black distal half to tail. Black underwing coverts. All observers agreed with ID
