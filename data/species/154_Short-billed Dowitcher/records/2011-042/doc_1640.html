Description of the Bird

This bird had the "clean orange" front neck and breast that are an identifying characteristic of the short billed dowitcher. The billed was shorter than a long billed dowitcher and it appears to have a slight kink in it. Vertical (while bird is standing) bars on flank/belly transition to to spots on sides of neck. Feathers on back were dark with white tips. White ring around eye broken by dark line extending from bill to eye and then behind eye. Whitish eyebrow that is almost exactly the length of the eye. Very dark bill and eyes. Top of head is basically dark with some light speckling,
Behaviors: The bird's beak was under water most of the time that I was watching it. Steadily wandered between shore and shallow water where it could feed.
Call: not heard
Plumage: breeding
