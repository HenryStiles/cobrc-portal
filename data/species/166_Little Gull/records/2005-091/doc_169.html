Description of the Bird

I was at Pueblo Reservoir at Sailboard launching area, when I spotted three small gulls together. Two of them were juvenile Sabine's Gull, and one was smaller, and I identfied it as a juvenile Little Gull. The Little Gull was smaller than the Sabine's Gulls, with a black cap, and black and white overall, and not brown and white overall as the Sabine's Gulls. In flight the bird had a bold black and white W/M wing pattern.
Behaviors: The bird was seen on the water and in flight near two Sabine's Gulls.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion
