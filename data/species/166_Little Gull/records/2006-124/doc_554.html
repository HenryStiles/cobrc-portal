Description of the Bird

A small Larus gull with light grey mantle and upper wing surface, dark gray to black underwing. White body, tail and head with small black spot behind eye and faint black smudge on crown. Bill was small, thin and black. Ends of all flight feathers were white creating a narrow white boarder along trailing edge of wing to primary tips.
Behaviors: Flying with Franklin's Gulls low over moist fields on north end of large reservoir, in close proximity to cattle feedlots.
Call: none
Plumage: adult basic
