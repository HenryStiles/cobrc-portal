Description of the Bird

I was scanning Upper Queens from the south side when I came across two Little Gulls sitting on the water. Both appeared two be dark above with narrow whitish barring. Nape and crown dark. Dark behind eyes. Folded primaries dark. White below with long dusky vertical shoulder mark down from hindneck. Returned in afternoon and found 6 birds at greater distance. In flight, some appeared to be gray backed, others black backed. All had blackish slash on upper secondary coverts and blackish outer primaries (on upperwing) forming an M when combined with black on lower back. Underwings mostly white, with a small trailing blackish edge to primaries (outer primaries, anyway). Tail white with narrow black terminal band. Tail rounded, not wedge-shaped.
