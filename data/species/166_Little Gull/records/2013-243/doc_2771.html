Description of the Bird

Smaller than Bonaparte's Gull, white underparts, pale gray upperparts with heavy black "M", black tail band restricted to inner rectrices, tiny black bill, dark gray ear spot, dark patch on hindcrown or nape. White underwings.

Similar Species Discussion

Bonaparte's has thinner "M" pattern with black bar on secondaries; Black-legged Kittiwake has darker gray mantle, blacker nape patch, larger bill and is quite a bit larger.
