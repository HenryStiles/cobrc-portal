Viewing Conditions

Light:  Bright and sunny but the yard was heavily shaded by numerous trees, especially where the feeders were hanging.

Description of the Bird

Small hummingbird, metallic green above. Longish, fairly straight bill. Black throat with a purple/violet band at lower border. Black face, chin and throat. White spot behind eye. Greenish crown. Flanks dusky gray/green. Tail dark and forked with no rufous present. White tips to outer tail feathers.
Behaviors: Bird was feeding at hummingbird feeder.
Call: Calling soft "tchew" as described in National Geo
Plumage: adult
