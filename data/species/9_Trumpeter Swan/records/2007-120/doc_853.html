Habitat

Frozen lake with open waterhole, at western edge of Great Plains, foot of the Rocky Mtns.

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Kowa TSN-4 scope
Distance:  150
Light:  Good

Description of the Bird

Large white swan with black bill, lacking yellow. Frontal feathers above bill in the shape of a "V".
Behaviors: Sitting, swimming, sleeping, feeding in shallow lake.
Call: silent
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Tundra Swan has more concave bill, appearing separated from the eye, usually with yellow on base; Feathered frontal area "U" shaped.

Resources Used

None

Previous Experience

Usually see both Trumpeter and Tundra Swans annually in Colorado
