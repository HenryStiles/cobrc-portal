Description of the Bird

A plump gray shore bird with straight short bill.  Not curved like Dunlin or Curlew Sandpiper.  Red Knot's shape is very different then other shorebirds.  See photo.

Similar Species Discussion

Dunlin and Curlew Sandpiper have down curved bill.  These aren't as plump as Red Knot.
Sanderings are white not gray.
