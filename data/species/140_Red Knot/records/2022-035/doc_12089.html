Description of the Bird

Medium sized stocky shorebird.  Overall gray, pale feather edging on back gave bird scalloped appearance, distinctive dark subterminal band on feathers distinctive for species in juv plumage.  Gray barring on flanks, buffy wash on undersides. Bill black and chisel-like.  Greenish legs.
