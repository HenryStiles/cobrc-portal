Description of the Bird

A medium sized, chunkey shorebird. Brownish grey above. Crown, nape and back solid color, the scapulars and coverts pale terminal edges. Primaries were dark. Medium length , straight, stout black bill. Dark eye and a dark line between the bill and eye. Light supercillium. Off white below with pale legs.
