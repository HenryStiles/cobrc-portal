Description of the Bird

Small finch (essentially American Goldfinch- sized) with bright red cap, yellow bill, whitish underparts with variable flank, rump, and undertail covert streaking, etc.
Behaviors: Foraging along roadside and on open weedy banks of reservoir.
Call: numerous iterations of flight calls heard
Plumage: Fall plumage

Similar Species Discussion

Only real contender for similar species would be Hoary Redpoll, but we saw no birds we considered to be good candidates. Flight call along with suite of field marks only points to redpoll sp.

Resources Used

none- prior experience with species sufficient.

Previous Experience

Quite a bit of prior experience with both redpolls in AK, have seen Common Redpoll in Colorado and upper plains states as well.
