Description of the Bird

Small white finch with streaking along the flanks, prominent white wingbars, male pink wash on the breast female white breast, red cap, and all yellow bill.
Behaviors: Perched high in a tree until they flew off.
Call: Goldfinch like two note call. However more buzzy and distinct from all other finches.
Plumage: Adult
