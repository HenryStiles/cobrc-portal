Description of the Bird

-very similar to a Pine Siskin in markings across back and wings and in size
-definite red patch on top of crown
-whitish breast with small black chin patch
Behaviors: -was present with Pine Siskins and fed and behaved much as they did
-did not always fly away with Siskins when they were spooked
Call: silent
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

-Pine Siskin is very similar from the back and several were present for easy comparison
-definite red patch on crown
