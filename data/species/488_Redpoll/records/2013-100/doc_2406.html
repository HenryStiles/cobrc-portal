Description of the Bird

See photos. Small (basically American Goldfinch-sized) finch with bright red cap, yellow bill, variable side streaking, variable undertail covert and uppertail covert streaking/spotting. Males with variable pink wash on breast, sometimes extending to belly.
Behaviors: Visiting thistle feeders with American Goldfinches and House Finches
Call: many call variations heard
Plumage: winter

Similar Species Discussion

All but one bird was too streaked (flanks, rump, &/or undertail coverts) for Hoary, though one bird fit Hoary traits well and was submitted to the CBRC.
