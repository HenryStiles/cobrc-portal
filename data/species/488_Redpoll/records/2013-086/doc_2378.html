Description of the Bird

Small brown and white finches with streaked sides, white wingbars, yellow bill and red forehead patch. Smaller than house finches.
Behaviors: Behavior was similar to house finches except more nervous and they were feeding exclusively on the thistle sock.
Call: Could not hear call or song.
Plumage: winter

Similar Species Discussion

Smaller than House Finches. Common Redpoll more streaked that Hoary Redpoll.

Resources Used

Saw a Common Redpoll at Bear Creek Lake Park with several other birders - had been reported there several times. Also used Sibley's to confirm ID.

Previous Experience

Male House Finches are larger and have more widespread color.
