Description of the Bird

I saw bird fly into our Choke Cherry tree, and noticed immediately that it was smaller than our house finches. Had also noticed that there was a larger flock of house finches than usual, coming to our tree. Saw the white wing bar, and grabbed my binoculars. Noticed its yellow bill, and the dark spot under the bill. As I recall the pink coloration on the breast was mostly confined to the top. There was some brownish streaking on the white breast.
Behaviors: The bird tried a couple of times to get niger from the feeder, but other birds scared it off. It was very flighty, nervously moving from branch to branch.
Could not get a photo.

No question that this was a Common Redpoll, we were aware of the numerous reports from COBirds, and were aware of what the bird looked like when we saw it.
Call: None heard
Plumage: Yellow bill, red forehead, pink on chest
