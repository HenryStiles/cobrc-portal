Description of the Bird

This bird was in typical adult plumage. Red "cap" on poll and black "mask" around bill to the eyes and under the chin. Bill was yellow. Heavy streaking on sides. Whitish edging to greater coverts. Back was brown with streaking.

Supporting photographs will be emailed seperately.
Behaviors: Sharing a platform feeder with Pine Siskins, American Goldfinches and House finches. Eating niger and sunflower chips.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

This bird was feeding with Pine Siskins, Am. Goldfinches and House finches. It was unmistakable. Too dark for a Hoary.
