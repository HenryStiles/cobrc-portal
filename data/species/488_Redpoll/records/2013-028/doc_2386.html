Description of the Bird

Small finches with dark faces, short stubby bills, dark rufous caps, at least one with pink breast.
Behaviors: Three were perched in the branches of a single deciduous tree for several minutes. Other similar birds were flitting about with similar calls but were obscured by trees and on private property.
Call: rising call similar to AMGO, other high notes
Plumage: At least 1 adult male, other two unknown
