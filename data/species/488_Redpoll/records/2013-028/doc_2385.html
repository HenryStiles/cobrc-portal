Description of the Bird

I scanned the group for some time looking for HORE traits but couldn't see anything definitive. All were small finches, had dark face, short bill, streaked flanks, dark rufous caps. Some had the adult male plumage with rosy hues on the breast.
Behaviors: CORE had been previously reported in the neighborhood and they appeared to be continuing birds (I saw some nearly daily on my walks to/from the bus stop). On this particular day they were clustered in several adjacent trees and not moving around as much as usual, so I was actually able to make a count.
Call: Rising calls similar to AMGO, other high notes and trills.
Plumage: Adult, M/F/Juvenile (latter two indist.)
