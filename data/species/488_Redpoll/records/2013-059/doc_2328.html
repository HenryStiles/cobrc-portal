Description of the Bird

Small finch , streaky flanks and back , red poll , black chin . One bird in this flock of 12 had characteristics of a Hoary Redpoll and has been submitted as a separate report.
Behaviors: Actively feeding in residential front yard from feeders after perching in aspen.
Call: short trills .
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

HORE would be paler , much less streaking on flanks , no undertail streaking , less black areas around bill , less to no rump streaking on pale rump etc.

Resources Used

Sibley , discussion on cobirds re characteristics to separate CORE from HORE .

Previous Experience

Seen CORE several times in CO , more times in NE US .
