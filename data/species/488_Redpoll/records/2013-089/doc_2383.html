Habitat

Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Binoculars and camera
Distance:  varied 10 to 50 feet
Light:  Good but a bit over cast.

Description of the Bird

Similar in size to the American Goldfinch they were with. White and brownish grey with streaked belly, pinkish upper chest. Red cap and yellow bill. Black beard.
Behaviors: Whole flock flying from one side of the Country Road to the other. Back and forth back and forth with American Goldfinch in winter plumage. Seemed to hanging around dried up thistle plants. Seen on 2-6-13.
Call: Mostly quiet
Plumage: Winter

Similar Species Discussion

House finch ruled out because of the red cap and black beard on the birds observed.

Hoary Redpolls also present which lack the streaking on the breast.
