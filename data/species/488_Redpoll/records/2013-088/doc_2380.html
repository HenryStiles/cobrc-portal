Description of the Bird

2 small finches a bit larger than Pine Siskin coming to feeders at Fawn Brook Inn. Brown streaked back. White beneath with brown streaking on flanks. Flank streaking heavier on female. Well defined bright red cap. Male breast with pink wash. Stubby yellow bill. Black chin, lores and forehead. Forked tail.
Behaviors: Skittish. Flying from trees to ground under feeders at Fawn Brook Inn.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: Winter male and female (1 each)

Similar Species Discussion

Smaller than House, Cassin's or Purple Finch with smaller more clearly defined red cap than any of these species.
