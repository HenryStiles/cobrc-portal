Description of the Bird

Tiny finch with red cap, yellow bill that extended well beyond the front of the face, significant streaking on flanks and at least smudgy streaking on undertail coverts.
Behavior: Responded to pishing and playback in large AMGO flock in woodlot near the edge of a town.
Call: not heard calling, but did respond to CORE playback
Plumage: winter

Similar Species Discussion

Hoary Redpoll ruled out by size of bill and amount of flank streaking
