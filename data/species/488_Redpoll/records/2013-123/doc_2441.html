Description of the Bird

A group of redpolls were feeding on weeds at Prewitt, in Washington County. Noted were small finches with red forehead, black chins, streaks beneath. The males were liberally washed in red-rose across chest. Exact breakdown by sex not known. Birds flew off shortly after being spotted
Behaviors: See Description
Call: djit-djit
Plumage: males and females
