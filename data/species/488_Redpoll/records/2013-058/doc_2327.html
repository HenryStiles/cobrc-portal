Description of the Bird

See photos , small finches , red poll , most heavily streaked on back and flanks , streaks on undertail coverts . One individual was considerably paler , with very limited flank streaks , and very limited black around chin and base of bill but photo ( 5 ) not enough to take beyond Redpoll sp.
Behaviors: present at private residence feeders.
Call: Flight calls shorter and not as uprising as Pine Siskin.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

No other finch has this combination of features except HORE . All birds seen were too streaky and brown , streaks on undertail coverts etc except one ( photo 5 ) and not enough to progress any further.
