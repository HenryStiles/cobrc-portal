Description of the Bird

One to four birds present around North Picnic Road at Pueblo Reservoir from December 12, 2012 to January 3, 2013. Photos taken on January 3, 2012. See photos.
Behaviors: Flying around and then landing on old sunflower heads.
Call: jit jit, siskin like noises
Plumage: winter

Similar Species Discussion

Hoary Redpoll -- not frosty enough, to much much streaking, bill too big, etc.

Previous Experience

Saw lots of Common Redpolls this past summer in Alaska. I've also seen a few in Colorado over the years, including November 2012 in El Paso County.
