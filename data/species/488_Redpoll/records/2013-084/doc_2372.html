Description of the Bird

A small finch like bird, dark face, with a yellow bill. Brown streaking on the flanks, dark pink chest, red capped.
Behaviors: They have been feeding at the finch feeders that have black oil sunflower seed and nyjer seed. With other house finches & pine siskins and such. I first saw/photographed Feb 8. I have not seen them in the last 2 days.
Call: chit chit chit followed by a rattling buzzing sound....
Plumage: brown streaks, red capped, pink breast

Similar Species Discussion

The commons are have much more white on them, than that of the abundant house finches, that's what caught my attention, a frosty look to them. So I started watching ever more carefully, even sat outside closer and watched & photographed.
