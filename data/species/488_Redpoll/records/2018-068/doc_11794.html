Habitat

Came to my Thisel feeder many times over a month.

Description of the Bird

A single Common Redpoll came to my feeder from March 6th to April 9th some days several times.  It was observed by my family and birding friends. Red cap, small yellow bill, Black face and throat with broad streaking on flanks.  

Similar Species Discussion

Finches and other Redpolls were eliminated by comparing photos of the bird with pictures of the other species, and pictures of the Common Redpoll.
