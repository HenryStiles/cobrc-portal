Description of the Bird

 

I was recording what I thought were Red Crossbills.  I posted them to eBird.  Nathan Pieplow heard the recordings and emailed me to say they were Cassia Crossbills.  I changed my list to reflect the observation.  I did not see the birds, I only heard them.

 https://ebird.org/checklist/S116272355

You can hear the recordings on the eBird check list posted above. 

The recordings were reviewed and approved by Nathan Pieplow, Matt Young and Tim Spahr. 

Similar Species Discussion

Red Crossbills are the common species at this location.  

Resources Used

I didn't know they were CACR until Nathan Pieplow contacted me.

Previous Experience

I went over the Silverthorne the week before and heard the CACR several times at the Alfred M. Bailey Bird Nesting Area in Summit County, CO.  I could tell the difference between RECR and CACR because the RECR sound higher pitched and more metalic than the lower pitched CACR.
