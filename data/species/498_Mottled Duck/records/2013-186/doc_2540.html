Description of the Bird

Dark, mottled mallard looking duck. Head and neck light brown. Weak brown eye stripe. Head and neck obviously lighter than body. Darker than the 20+ mallards that it was with. Could not see edges of bluish speculum while on ground but got decent looks in flight and could not see any white edge. White underwings contrasted sharply with body in flight and did not appear to have any black on them. Bird was dark-tailed from above. Dark vent. Bill appeared yellowish/olive, different from mallards. Legs orange.
Behavior: Hanging out with 20+ Mallards.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Separation from American Black Duck difficult. I think the light head, clear underwing and no visible border to the bluish speculum are keys (black duck should be more purple.) Mexican Duck would have a lighter body with obvious border to speculum.
