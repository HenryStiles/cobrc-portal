Description of the Bird

Only seen in flight. Tern with long thin black bill with yellow tip. Forked tail. All black top of head. See photos I took.
Behavior: Flying and catching small fish on small pond.
Call: none
Plumage: adult breeding plumage

Similar Species Discussion

The yellow tip on the black bill, eliminates all other terns. The tail was forked, not like a Sabine's Gull.
