Description of the Bird

When heading out to the shore of Manitou Lake my husband and I saw an obvious tern sitting on a snag in the middle of the lake. Upon putting a scope on the bird I saw a medium sized tern with a black bill that had a lighter tip. the cap was all black and formed a slight crest at the back of the head. The birds legs were dark. When the bird took off flying I could see darker under-wing tips.
Behavior: The bird was both perched on the snag in the middle of the lake and also flying around the lake.
Call: I did not hear the bird call.
Plumage: Adult breeding
