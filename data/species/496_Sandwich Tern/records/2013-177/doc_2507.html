Description of the Bird

large black-capped tern, pale mantle, wings and primary tips showing heavy molt with missing feathers in the primaries and secondaries, deeply forked, but fairly short tail, long black bill with yellow tip.
Behavior: Tern-like flight over small pond with frequent dives into the water to catch small prey. At one point it had prey in its bill and started flying over Highway 57 back toward Manitou Lake when it dropped the food item. The tern started to swoop down for the item, but stopped to the relief of all watching as a big pick-up would have flattened it had it kept after its morsel.

After watching the bird for several minutes, it returned to Manitou Lake.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult breeding
