Description of the Bird

Medium sized white tern with long wings and short deeply forked tail . Dark black extends to nape , longish black bill tipped pale yellow. In flight outer 5 primaries show up as a darker gray , rest of wing very pale gray almost white in some light. At rest wing tips just extend beyond tail.
Behavior: Either perched into wind on tree branches in middle of Manitou Lake , or patrolling along adjacent beaver ponds and plunge diving for fish.
Call: None heard
Plumage: adult summer

Similar Species Discussion

No other tern of this size has combination of a long black bill tipped pale yellow in breeding plumage. Gull -billed Tern has thick all black bill
