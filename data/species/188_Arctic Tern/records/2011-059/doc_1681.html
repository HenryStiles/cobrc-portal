Description of the Bird

Medium sized sterna tern with relatively long wings and long tail streamers in comparison with body and head. Black cap and gray body offset white cheeks , shortish dull red bill with blackish tip , upper wings uniform gray , underwings uniform whitish with a narrow black on tips of primaries , white secondaries .On one occasion saw the red color of legs but in general very difficult to see the legs at all in flight. My only views were in flight.
Behaviors: flying over the lake in a strong wind , dipping down to the surface of the water but never saw it it plunge dive. Wing beats strong and deep.
Call: None given
Plumage: adult breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Not Forster's because gray body , not Common because of the relatively smaller head , shorter bill ,longer tail and wings and no broad black tips to primaries not plunge diving, not Roseate because uniform grayish upperwings , not Aleutian because no black bar on secondaries and a red bill not black.
