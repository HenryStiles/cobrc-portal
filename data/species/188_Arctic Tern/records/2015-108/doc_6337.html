Description of the Bird

Receeding back cap, white forehead.
Pale gray carpal bar.
Pale gray upper wing.
Short red legs.

Similar Species Discussion

This tern lacked the dark wedge on the upper wings for a Common Tern.
The white forehead and black receeding cap contrasts with Forster's Tern black eye patches.
Birds of North America Online stated that the juvenile Arctic Tern's legs are red until autumn when they turn black. The Common Tern's legs are a pale orange-pink. This tern has red legs. The scaley back points to a juvenile.
