Description of the Bird

Head dark and strongly patterned with dark collar, dark short upturned bill. Dark eye. Approx. 9.5 inches (based on sizes of much larger American Avocets, Yellowlegs in vicinity). Wings dark with some patterning. White belly. Bright orange, short legs.

In flight, strong white "V" on back. Striking pattern on upper wing. Broad white stripe in tail with black sub-terminal band. Clean white underwing
Behaviors: Foraging for food on mudflat.
Call: none heard
Plumage: Adult non-breeding
