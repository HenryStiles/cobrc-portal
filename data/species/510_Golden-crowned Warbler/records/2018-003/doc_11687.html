Description of the Bird

Small, warbler sized and shaped bird.  Two black stripes on head with a golden yellow stripe between them down the middle of the head. Rest of the face grayish, with a dark eye and a broken white eye-ring.  Upperparts and top of tail a uniform dull green, with no wingbars at all.  Below it was a uniform yellow, that was not really dull, but certainly not bright either.

Similar Species Discussion

No other North American warbler in spring plumage looks like this bird at all.
