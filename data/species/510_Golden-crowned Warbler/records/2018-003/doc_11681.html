Description of the Bird

I was pishing up Wilson's and Yellow Warblers when this bird popped up.  I was stumped for about 5 seconds, until I saw it's head with a yellow-orange center crown stripe, with dark stripes on either side, mostly bright yellow below and olive colored above.  Face was grayish with a difuse lateral eye line and pale lower eye arc.  The bird was about the size, shape and structure of a Wilson's Warbler.  See Photos.

Similar Species Discussion

The head pattern eliminated any other warbler that I am familiar with.
