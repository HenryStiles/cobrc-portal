Description of the Bird

a rosy-finch, about the size of a song sparrow feeding next to it, and larger than the siskens it was with. Black chest, sides, mantle, auriculars and forecrown, same color as its feet. Gray nape contrasted sharply with black auriculars and forecrown. Some pink in wing coverts and belly/vent. Yellowish bill.
Behaviors: After a night of heavy snow, it was feeding with a flock of pine siskins on the ground uner my feeders at my home, where I had cleared the snow away so the birds could get to spilled thistle seeds.
Call: did not call
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

sharp contrast black with gray on nape/auriculars. Black rather than brown chest. Sharp contrast gray crown with black nape.
