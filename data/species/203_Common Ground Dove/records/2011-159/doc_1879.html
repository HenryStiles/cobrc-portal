Description of the Bird

This petite dove has a small, pink-based bill, scaling on the grayish head and breast, tan back and scapulars with dark brown markings, a short gray-brown tail with outer recs black and small white corners. It flight it showed maroon primaries.
Behaviors: This bird flushed from the area marked on Steve Stakoviack's map as this bird's favorite hangout. It had been on the ground.
Call: n/a
Plumage: basic

Similar Species Discussion

Mourning Dove is the only somewhat similar species in range. MODO is a larger bird with a long, pointed tail, longer, more pointed wings.
Common Ground-Dove is more similar to Inca Dove, but that bird would be out of range and has a longer, white-edged tail.
