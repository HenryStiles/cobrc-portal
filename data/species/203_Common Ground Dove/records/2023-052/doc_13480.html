Description of the Bird

What a crazy bird to see here in Colorado let alone in my yard. WoW!! Common ground dove 10-14-23. Came home from the store and seen a bird at the edge of my pond. Initially thought it was a small immuture mourning dove. then it flew up to the split rail fence and I seen reddish color on its wings. Then i new thats not a mourning dove. It then flew to the backyard so i went in locked the dogs in and grabbed my camera. Looked out back and it was feeding under my feeders. went out, and before i could get a pic it flew over to my neighbors roof where i got some pics, even one where you can see the open wings and tail. It then flew back to the yard and I was watching it instead of trying to get more pics, then it flew to a smaller fence in the yard where house sparrows started bothering it then it flew to a tree in the front and that was the last time i saw it. I couldnt tell if it was hanging around with the sparrows or they were bothering it. It did not make any sound.

Similar Species Discussion

At first I considered mourning dove as there is a immature one that comes

To the yard. This was eliminated by the reddish underwing. Size also eliminated 
Collared dove. After seeing the ground dove next to a house sparrow had me 
Realize just how small it was. Not really much bigger then house sparrow
