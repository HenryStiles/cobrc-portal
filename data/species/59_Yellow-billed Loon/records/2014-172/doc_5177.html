Description of the Bird

No calls, Mostly swimming on the surface with dives for food. Large loon with light colored bill, white chin and throat with light buff on flanks of neck. Back looked stripped.

Similar Species Discussion

Common Loon, color and shape of bill discounted the common. This bird was also very large.
