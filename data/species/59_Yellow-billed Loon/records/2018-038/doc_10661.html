Description of the Bird

Large loon with long yellowish bill that was upturned when loon was not feeding. Head angled uplards when not feeding. Size = larger than Common Loon with which it was feeding. Pale head with small darkish auricular patch. Mandibles with space between them when bill is closed. Gave single note call at times when bird was not preening and possibly in response to my presence. 

Similar Species Discussion

RTLO much smaller with small head and thinner bill.
COLO - with double bump on head and body smaller
PALO - with strong contrast between dark and light on neck and dark nape
ARLO - with white crescent on flank showing above water line

Resources Used

None - found previously - good comparison in size and bill structure with COLO with which is was seen feeding 

Previous Experience

I worked in arctic Alaska and saw them in breeding plumage in small tundra ponds 
Seen in CA out of Monterrey on a pelagic trip
Seen about 10 in CO over the years including one in January this year and this is my 3rd at Pueblo Res
