Viewing Conditions

Description of the Bird

see photos – bird had a very pale neck and head, obvious barring on back, but I have seen very pale COLO so did not consider it possible to be YBLO (my only other YBLO was much more distant, see below)
Behaviors: I saw the bird several times during the period and I never saw it leave its smallish hole in the ice, other than to stick its head in the water and forage under water
Call: I never heard this bird vocalize
Plumage: see attached photos

Similar Species Discussion

other loon sp, especially COLO, but see attached photos
