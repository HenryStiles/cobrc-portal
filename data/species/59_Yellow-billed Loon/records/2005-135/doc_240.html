Description of the Bird

A large loon, that was brownish over all, with up turned yellow bill.
Behaviors: Swimming and diving at a reservoir, not really with other birds.
Call: none
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Common Loon - darker overall, gray not yellow bill. Holds the bill straighter, and not up turned.

Pacific, Arcitic, and Red-throated Loons - all smaller overall.

Previous Experience

I've seen Yellow-billed Loons a few times in Colorado at Pueblo Reservoir and Chatfield Reservoir. Also, one in 2005 at St. Paul Island in Alaska.

Seen lots of Common Loons in several states.

Seen lots of Pacific and Red-throated Loons in several states.

Seen one Arctic Loon in Douglas County, Colorado.
