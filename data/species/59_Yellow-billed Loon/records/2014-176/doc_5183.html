Description of the Bird

Loon looking to be a little larger than an adjacent juvenile COLO , head and nape pale smudgy brown , in comparison COLO head and nape was much darker . Pale barring on back . Bill pale with some yellow seen towards the tip , bill color of COLO appeared to be light gray . Diving about every minute in tandem with the COLO . See photos for side by side comparison .

Similar Species Discussion

COLO is eliminated by bill color ,and  light smudgy neck and head .
