Description of the Bird

Two larger loons, light brown back, yellow up turn bills, bigger than nearby Common Loons.  Found eariler in the day by several birders (Al Gardner and Chris Knight photographed one from West Fishing Road, and then later two together by the dam, seen by David Tonnessen and others..  See photo of one bird, both looked the same.  Better photos taken by other people, hopefully they submitted them to the CBRC.  Two birds from January 8, 2022 to February 10, 2022.  Saw only one on February 15, 2022. Saw two again on February 18, 2022 through March 16th.

 

Here are a lot of photos from many people who came to see these birds. 

 Yellow-billed Loon - Gavia adamsii - Media Search - eBird and Macaulay Library

Similar Species Discussion

All other loons are smaller.  Common Loon darker bill, darker over all.
