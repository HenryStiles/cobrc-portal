Description of the Bird

White body, black flight feathers, distinctive heavy bill. Juvenile, first year plumage. Bird was standing on a submerged log at the shoreline during the full observation.  It was observed preening and spread its wing briefly to sun. 

Similar Species Discussion

White ibis - Leg and beak color very different and lacks fully black primaries

American white pelican - pelicans have a shorter neck and a very large, yellow-ish bill.  There was a group of pelicans in the pond during observation so comparison was clear
