Description of the Bird

Chris Knight and I were birding at Valco Ponds, and spotted this immature night-heron.  The all black bill and white spots, were good for a Yellow-crowned Night-Heron and not Black-crowned Night-Heron.  See photo.

Similar Species Discussion

Black-crowned Night-Heron - doesn't have all black bill, and white spots.

Previous Experience

Seen quite a few Yellow-crowned Night-Herons around Pueblo, over the years, both adults and immatures.

Regularly see Black-crowned Night-Herons around Pueblo and other places.
