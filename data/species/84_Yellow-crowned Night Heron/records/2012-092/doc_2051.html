Description of the Bird

Approximately 24 to 30 inches tall. Very thick dark bill, bill ius completely dark. Grayish plumage with some brown visible. Reddish eye. Brownish streaks on white on chest and neck and throat. Yellow legs. White visible on front edges of wings near body. Reddish eye, some yellow on lores and below eye.
Behaviors: The bird was feeding and was moving very slowly while doing this, apparently this is typical of Yellow crowned night herons.
Call: Not heard
Plumage: juvenile (?)

Similar Species Discussion

Considered Great Blue heron but bill was too thick, Considered Black crowned Night-Heron but bill was all dark and streaking on breast, neck, and throat had a high degree of contrast. Considered Little Blue Heron but plumage did not match.
