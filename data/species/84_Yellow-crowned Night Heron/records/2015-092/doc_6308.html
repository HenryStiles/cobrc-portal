Description of the Bird

The night heron was perched in a tree on the waters edge.  I was able to see a frontal view with a white crown, yellow wash on the forehead and white cheek patches.

Similar Species Discussion

Only Black-crowned Night Heron is similar, but this bird did not have a black crown or a white breast.
