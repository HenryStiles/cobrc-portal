Description of the Bird

First seen in flight, then perched on a branch in the water in SW corner of Tabor Lake. Night-Heron with yellow crest along top of head and oval yellow patch on side of face. Also studied thick bill, longish legs, rounded looking head. Compared with adult BCNH.

Similar Species Discussion

BCNH is the only other bird similar in structure. This YCNH had an obviously different head pattern and no black on its back. Several BCNH's were around for comparison, though comparison isn't really necessary in this situation.
