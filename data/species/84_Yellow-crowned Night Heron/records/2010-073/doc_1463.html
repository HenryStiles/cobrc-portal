Description of the Bird

Large heron-like bird. Crown creamy white. White occipital plumes. Yellow forehead. Sides and back of head and upper hindneck black with white stripe on side of head extending under eye. Mantle slate gray. Chin and upper throat black. Gray-blue long heavy bill. Orange iris. Underparts gray. Yellow legs.
Behaviors: Sitting in a tree. I saw this bird fly a few times when flushed. Briefly landed in some cattails.
Call: not heard
Plumage: Adult
