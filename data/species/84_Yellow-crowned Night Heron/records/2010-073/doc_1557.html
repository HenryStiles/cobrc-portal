Description of the Bird

It has a thick black bill. It also has a black head with a white cheek patch and yellow on top of the head with long yellow plumes with a red eye. It had a blue-gray body and neck. Most of the feathers on the back had black on the inside of each feather with blue-gray on the outer edge of each feather.
Behaviors: It was usually just standing at the edge of a small pond on a small island in the middle. I saw it fly once, but then it returned to the back side of that island.
Call: Didn’t hear it
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Red eye eliminates everything except Black-crowned Night-Heron. The black head with white cheek and yellow on top of head eliminate everything.
