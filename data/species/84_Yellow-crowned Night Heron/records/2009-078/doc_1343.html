Viewing Conditions

Optics:  Telescopes

Light:  Good. Sun rising from the east. We were north east of the bird.

Description of the Bird

A short dark night heron with a dark head and white cheek patch.
Behaviors: The bird was seen with dozens of other night-herons - adult and juvenile, herons and egrets. It was in a tree in the middle of the John Martin Res. near mudflats.
Call: N/A
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

Black-crowned Night-heron was eliminated by the white cheek patch, overall darker tones, and shape of the bird when compared to the BCNHs around the YCNH.
