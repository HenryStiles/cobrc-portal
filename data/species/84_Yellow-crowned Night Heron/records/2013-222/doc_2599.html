Description of the Bird


On October 6, I had to stand on my car door, to see the bird in the marsh.  It was a smaller heron, than the more common Great Blue Heron.  It was certaintly a night-heron.  The bird was not a juvenile or an adult.  A second year bird, seemed to fit well with this bird.  See photos.  Seen by lots of people.

On October 19, 2013:  I saw the bird again, with Dave Silverman, Chris Knight, Margie Joy, Pearle Sandstorm-Smith, and Clif Smith.  More photos were taken on this date.

Similar Species Discussion

Black-crowned Night-Heron -- black head and back.  Doesn't really look like a Yellow-crowned Night-Heron is this plumage.
