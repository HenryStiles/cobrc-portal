Description of the Bird

Heron by leg length and bill shape.  Face dark gray with white patch behind eye. White chin. Bill light.  Legs dull yellow. Back gray with hints of brown. Body gray with brown interspersed. Head rectangular in shape.  Crown darker gray than rest of bird. Stationary in small marsh.

Similar Species Discussion

Black-crowned Night-Heron comes closest. BCNH has white sides to face not gray.  BCNH bill is chunkier and longer and tapers to a point.  BCNH has white breast or with brown vertical streaks.
