Description of the Bird

Small , stocky heron , stout billed , either BCNH or YBNH . Bird was in a transitional plumage with the white cheek surrounded by black just beginning to show through indicating YCNH , also stout bill not sharply pointed as in BCNH . wings and body plumage mainly gray little brown juvenile plumage visible.

Similar Species Discussion

Only other heron with this jizz is BCNH and they do not have a white cheek bordered in black .
