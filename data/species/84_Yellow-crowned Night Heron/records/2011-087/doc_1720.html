Description of the Bird

See photo. They make the ID, and I am submitting this predominantly as supporting material.
Behaviors: Was gathering sticks to add to a nest. The nest was on the small island at Glenmere Park, in Greeley. Sharing the colony were multiple BC Night-Herons, Snowy Egrets, and a few Cattle Egrets. At later dates, the YCNH could be refound sitting on the nest. Though once it seemed to garner the attention of a BCNH, for the most part, there was no obvious mate, and I never saw a BCNH on the nest. In late June, when most of the other nests had obvious chicks, the YCNH was still sitting on the nest, rising every once in a while to peck sharply at a couple sticks, or rearrange a stick or two.
Call: a "Qwok" higher in pitch than BCNH
Plumage: adult
