Description of the Bird

The bird was about the same size as all the Black-crowned Night-Herons around it.It had a gray bill. It was black on its throat. From its head all the way to its feet was a gray color. I did not see its feet because it was standing and then sitting in the nest.The very top of its head was a cream yellow color (Bavarian cream). It had a red eye and its lores and eye stripe were black. It also had a white cheek stripe.
Behaviors: When we first saw the bird it happened to be standing. After a little while it sat down and never stood the rest of the time we were there. Overall it was not very active.
Call: I did not hear a song.
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

A Black-crowned Night Heron does not have a black throat and a contrasting white cheek stripe. It also does not have an entirely gray body. The main feature that a Black-crowned does not have is that striking cream yellow crown.
