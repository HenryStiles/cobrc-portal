Habitat

Description of the Bird

I observed a medium-sized, chunky, heron-type bird with a short neck; gray body, black face with a distinctive white patch on its cheek and a yellow stripe along its crown; a few white nuptial feathers extended back from its crown; thick dagger-type black bill.
Behaviors: The night-heron was perched on a dead branch extending over the water on the west side of an island hosting a mixed heron breeding colony. In my observation it did not move much.
Call: none heard
Plumage: adult breeding

Similar Species Discussion

Black-crowned Night-Heron eliminated by the black face, yellow crown, gray body and white face patch.
