Description of the Bird

Stocky night heron shape. Dark brown above with small spots on scaps. Spots on wing coverts also small. Greater wing coverts narrowly edged in whitish along sides. Underparts dark grayish brown on neck and chest with whitish streaking (reverse of young BCNH). Lower belly and undertail coverts pale. Dark crown. Orange-red eye. Cheek streaked moderately with fine dusky marks, no whisker mark as in BCNH. Bill stout and black. Legs yellowish and long with feet and about equal amount of leg protruding beyond tail in flight. Tail and flight feathers contrastingly darker than remainder of wing and uppertail coverts.

Similar Species Discussion

BCNH- has pale on bill. Bill longer and slimmer than this bird. White markings above larger, more prominent. BCNH lacks white edges to greater secondary coverts. BCNH has shorter legs. Underparts (neck and chest) of BCNH are pale with brown streaking, not mostly grayish with white streaking
