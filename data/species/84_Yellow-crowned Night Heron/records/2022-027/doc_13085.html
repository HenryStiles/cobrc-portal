Description of the Bird

I was heading home, from Pueblo to Pueblo West, I was on Goodnight Ave, not far from Pueblo City Park, when I heard that Ed Landi found an aduly Yellow-crowned Night-Heron at Pueblo City Park.  I was able to turn around and go see it along the shore.  I helped other birders see it for a while.  Adult night-heron with yellow crown, clearly not a Black-crowned Night-Heron.  See photos.

Similar Species Discussion

No other herons have yellow on the crown.

Resources Used

none

Previous Experience

Seen many adult Yellow-crowned Night-Herons, including quite a few during April 2022, in both east Texas and Louisiana, seen adults in Colorado a few times, seems like they aren't too unusual around Pueblo.

Seen many Black-crowned Night-Herons in CO and other states.
