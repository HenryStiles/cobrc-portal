Description of the Bird

medium sized, long legged wading bird with the following noted features:

large white cheek patch on a black face;
pale streak on the forehead, crown that was light yellow right above the eyes and then became just off white further back on the crown;
red eye;
stout black bill;
dark blue-gray body and wings;
stubby appearance;
pale, light yellow legs
Behaviors: The bird stood very still on the weedy mud flats of Jumbe Res.
Call: N/A
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion
