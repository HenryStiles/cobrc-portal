Description of the Bird

I spotted the bird on the 16th and it has been re-found over and over for a week and a half.  Gray bird with a darker gray back with the very light gray vertical streaks.  The head is mostly black with the very creamy yellow head strip on top and more buffy white/yellow horizontal stripes on each cheeks.  Thick black bill.  Yellow plume feathers coming out from the back of the head to about half way down the back.  Bright orange legs with a bit of black on them.  Red eye ring around a large black pupil.

Similar Species Discussion

I didn't consider anything else.  It was immediately identifiable. 

Resources Used

Experience.  Prior exposure to the bird in CO, OK, TX, and AR.  Prior to that, I had done my studying in all the major field guides and some online resources.

Previous Experience

I've seen them in several states, including another one at the exact same pond last year.  I am thinking it may even be the same individual.  I've seen them in Colorado, Texas, Arkansas, and Oklahoma (at least - I may be forgetting some place).
