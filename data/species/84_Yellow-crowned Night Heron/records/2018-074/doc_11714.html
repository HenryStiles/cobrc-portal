Description of the Bird

Two obvious night-herons perched in the open in Russion Olives on the western edge of Stalker Pond.

Both predominantly gray and very long-legged with orange eyes and hefty dagger-like bills - these characteristics separate them from the more common BCNH.

Adult had black face with a horizontal white cheek patch and dusky-yellow feathers on the forehead.

Youngster had extensive white spotting on the wings which it kindly showed off while sunning and white streaking on its face.  The proximal portion of the bill was yellow.

As a note, YCNHs have been reported intermittently from this location since 2014 with multiple reports from the spring/summer of 2017.
