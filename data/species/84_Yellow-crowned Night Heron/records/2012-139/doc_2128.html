Description of the Bird

I have a photo. Can I submit the photo.
This information was submitted to eBird on the day of sighting. I just learned of CBRC.
Behaviors: Bird had been sighted a few days earlier that date submitted here flying with a Black-Crowned Night Heron adult on eastern lake at S. Platte Park. It was too far away for me to obtain a detailed photo. On the day the detailed photo was taken the YCNH was seen again with a BCNH adult on the South Platte River. It was a fairly secluded spot. Joe Roller and several other birders tried to find this bird again but I don't there were additional reported sitings, on eBird at least.
