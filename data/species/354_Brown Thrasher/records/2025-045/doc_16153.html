Description of the Bird

Had been sitting in a small chair watching/photographing  many species feeding on a large swarm of midge type flies on the leaves of a Black Hawthorne.  Did not see any species eat the tree fruit. Had been there for over an hour and stood up and was getting ready to leave when I spoted a long-tail weasel at the bottom of the hawthorne and shot a few pictures of it.  Then a few minutes later the Brown Thrasher popped out right in front of me at eye level but stayed halfway hidden.

Bird was rusty brown with large brown spots/streaks on the breast.  Had a slightly curved bill and a bold orange/yellow eye. White and black wingbars. 

Got a few quick camera shots before it disappeared back into the hawthorne
