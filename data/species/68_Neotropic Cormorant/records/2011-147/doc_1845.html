Description of the Bird

Yellowish-brown gular pouch NOT extending up towards, or around, the eye. Base of gular pouch has a white, side-ways "v-shaped" border. The bird had a long tail, longer than the attendant DCCOs around it, and it was smaller than the DCCOs in the area.
Behaviors: Perched on a stump in Cherry Creek reservoir.
Call: none
Plumage: adult nonbreeding
