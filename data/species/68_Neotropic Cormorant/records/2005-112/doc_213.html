Description of the Bird

A dark cormorant, smaller than nearby DCCO. Long tailed relative to DCCO.
Behaviors: sitting on perch in lake
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Double-crested Cormorant, other cormorants. Pelagic Cormorant is the only cormorant comparable in size. Head appeared thicker than neck, and no white patches were visible on flanks eliminated PECO
