Description of the Bird

Small cormorant sitting on sandbar near marina in Cherry Creek State Park.  Noticeably smaller-bodied than neighboring Double-crested Cormorant.  Obvious light patch around gape.

Similar Species Discussion

Double-crested Cormorant:  Was easily eliminated as Neotropic was sitting right next to a DC.

Resources Used

Sibley's, online research such as Whatbird.  Examination of photos of species vs Double-crested cormorant

Previous Experience

None with this species - this was my first sighting.  Had seen plenty of Double-crested Cormorants and had some at same site for direct comparison.
