Description of the Bird

Alongside the Double -crested Cormorant , looked like a two thirds sized cormorant with a proportionally longer tail , and smaller head and bill . It had dark lores , a pale area turning to yellow below the eye at the base of the bill . Breast and rest of body was black as far as I could see although the bird was back light . When swimming alongside the Double -crested Cormorant , the smaller head and shorter bill were characteristic.
Behaviors: Resting on a buoy alongside a DCCO . Also swimming and diving .
Call: None
Plumage: juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Size rules out the other large comorants , Great , Double-crested , Brandt's , Red-faced and size and shape for Anhinga . Pelagic Cormorant is a little larger than NECO but has an extremely thin bill and face is completely blackish.
