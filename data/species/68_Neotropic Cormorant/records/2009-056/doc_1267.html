Description of the Bird

A small cormorant, with two Double-crested Cormorants at John Martin Reservoir, Bent County, Colorado. The bird was quite far away, so I mostly identified the bird by the shape and size.
Behaviors: Sitting on log in the reservoir.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The only other cormorant is Double-crested Cormorant, which there were two on the same log as the Neotropic Cormorant. The Double-crested Cormorants are lot larger than the Neotropic Cormorant.

Previous Experience

Seen many Neotropic Cormorants in Texas, as well as a few in Arizona and Kansas. I've seen a few in Colorado, including at John Martin Reservoir.
