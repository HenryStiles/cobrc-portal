Description of the Bird

Small cormorant with a long tail in proportion to its body. Head was tucked so identification was limited to size and tail length.

Similar Species Discussion

This bird was sitting next to Double-crested Cormorants. DC Cormorant was eliminated due to size and tail length.

Resources Used

While reviewing my photos I noticed the size difference of this bird and thought it was a Neotropic. I sent the photo to Brandon Percival who agreed with my identification.

Previous Experience

I have observed Neotropic Cormorants on a few different occasions in Texas.
