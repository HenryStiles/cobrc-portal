Description of the Bird

Small cormorant associating with DCCO. Browner with proportionally longer tail. Acute gular angle. No yellow in the loral region. I have found a total of 2 vagrant NECO in Indiana before this one, and have experience with this species. Photos were obtained and subsequently attached to this (https://ebird.org/checklist/S66594139) eBird checklist.

Similar Species Discussion

No other NA cormorant species combines the previous features and pattern of vagrancy as NECO. Direct comparisons to DCCO made ruling out DCCO easy.

Resources Used

Sibley's, Audubon Birds app (to confirm)

Previous Experience

I have found this species before out of range (see https://ebird.org/checklist/S44576053). This would be the 4th time I have seen a vagrant NECO.
