Description of the Bird

Lovely female acitvely diving and catching a few crayfish. An absolute stunning way to end a day of birding in the mountains. Small mostly black duck, roughly the same size as a nearby Ruddy Duck. White spot behind eye and white in front of the eye extending to the all dark bill. 

Similar Species Discussion

No other species are of that size and color. Although I did first assume it was a scoter until the Ruddy Duck popped up next to it!

Previous Experience

Extensive knowledge of Colorado waterfowl. Only other time I had seen HADU was in Iceland!
