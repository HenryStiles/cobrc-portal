Description of the Bird

***NOTE***  Year should be 1901, form did not allow for dates before 1975.

I have not personally seen the specimen, just photos which are attached.  The photos show a dark duck with white in front of the face, a white dot behind the eye, and a white line towards the top of the head.  The back has some white and rusty colored areas.  The belly appears to be completely dark.

This species should be added to the Larimer County List.
