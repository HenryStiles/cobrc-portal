Description of the Bird

A gull with white primaries, gray back, and smaller than the nearby Herring Gull.

Similar Species Discussion

The white primaries eliminated the dark primaried gulls. The gray back eliminates an albino. It was smaller than the nearby Herring gull, so not a Glaucous Gull.

Resources Used

The photos I took, while not very sharp and poor resolution, still showed the features. These photos were compared to Sibley's guide. Also this gull was identified as an Iceland in Cobirds.
