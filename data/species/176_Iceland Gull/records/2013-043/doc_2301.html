Description of the Bird

An overall pale gull, with evenly pale primaries, coverts, tail, mantle and rump. Slightly large than Ring-billed Gull, considerably smaller than Herring Gull with smaller head and bill.
Behaviors: feeding on dead fish in the mud of a drained lake
Call: none
Plumage: 1st cycle

Similar Species Discussion

Thayer's is very similar, but darker with contrasting darker primaries and tail
