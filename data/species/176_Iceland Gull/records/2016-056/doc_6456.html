Description of the Bird

Bird was first spotted by David Dowell out on ice. It was clearly paler and more petite than nearby Herring Gulls. Head appeared delicate and bill very small when c/w nearby HERG. Body size smaller than HERGs but definitely closer in size to HERGs than RBGUs.  Eye nearly white. feet pink. Bill mix of bright yellow and pallid yellow with orange-red gonydeal spot. Wing pattern clearly shown in photos, within range of Kumlien's ICGU but not THGU.

Similar Species Discussion

Thayer's Gull -- almost always dark eye (and probably never white), more dark on wing tips, typically blacker on wing tips
