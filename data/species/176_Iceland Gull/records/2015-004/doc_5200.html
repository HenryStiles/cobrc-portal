Description of the Bird

2nd yr. Worn wing coverts whitish and freckled with medium brown fine spotting throughout. Tertisls pale brown and mottled throughout. Primaries same color as tertials and edged in pale grayish white. Body medium to light brown fading into whitish head and neck bill yellow- pink with black tip. Very tip seemed to have small pale spot. Smaller than nearby HERG. Structure mid range for THGU but easily also within range of Iceland. Feet bright pink. Eye rather pale. Back gray, paler than same aged HERG. In flight underwings entirely pale like typical ICGU. Upperwings pale brown. No secondary bar. Outer 4-5 primaries with dusky tips running part way up outer webs. Tail had narrow pale brown band. Remainder of tail and UTC white with pale brown mottling.

Similar Species Discussion

Similarly aged THGU would have darker eye, darker back, solid tertials. Though wing tips darker than average for second year ICGU, well within range of such. Tail with rather narrow and pale band for what one might expect in THGU
