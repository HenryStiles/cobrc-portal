Description of the Bird

A pale, cream-colored larus, similar in size to California Gull, noticeably smaller than herring Gull, with a small head and bill. Almost completely con-colorous, but with very slightly darker primaries and tail band, but still very pale tan. Dark bill and pinkish legs.
Behaviors: Flying, swiming, feeding and chasing other gulls around the lake.
Call: none
Plumage: 1st cycle

Similar Species Discussion

Thayer's Gull is very similar, but even the palest THGU has darker primaries, darker tail band and dark centered tertials. Bird was too small for GLGU or GWGU.
