Description of the Bird

Seen in flight flying over with other gulls. Gull larger than Ring-billed and California Gulls, smaller than Herring, Glaucous, Glaucous-winged Gulls. Lighter wing tips than Thayer's Gull. See photos.
Behaviors: Flying over with other gulls
Call: none
Plumage: first year

Similar Species Discussion

Thayer's -- darker wing tips, Glaucous much bigger with two-toned bill. Glaucous-winged Gull much bigger, thicker bill.
