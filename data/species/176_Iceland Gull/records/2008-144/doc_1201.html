Description of the Bird

White wing tips, white body, white head. Has some light brown markings on the body and has some smudgy gray on the head. Mostly white belly and chest, but it still had some smudgy light gray colors. Light brown and white lines on the undertail coverts. Dark eye. Pink legs. A little over half of the bill was pink, mostly the base of the bill. It also had a small pink tip to the bill. The pink on the bill was still quite dark.
Behaviors: Mostly sitting on the ice, but occasionally would fly around and occasionally sit on the water.
Call: Didn't knowingly hear it
Plumage: 1st Winter

Similar Species Discussion

Glaucous Gull - Larger and heavier gull, shorter white primaries, large bicolored bill (with the pink being bright pink), not as much light gray smudgy color on head and body
Thayer's Gull - Brown to black primaries (darker than body) and brown back
