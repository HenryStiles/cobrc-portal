Description of the Bird

The bird was resting most of the time. It finally stood and stretched its wings, revealing primaries with limited dark (not black) on outer webs, inner webs all white. All white body and tail, mantle gray similar to nearby Ring-bills. Some dark streaking on head. Yellowish iris (hard to tell from distance), yellow bill with red gon spot, angle subtle. Pink feet. Slightly larger and more robust than Ring-billed.
Behaviors: Sleeping and resting on ice shelf a few feet from a group of Ring-billed gulls
Call: None given
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

In adult plumage, Iceland Gull wing pattern is quite unique.
