Description of the Bird

Originally spotted and photographed by Todd Miller. He thought it might be a male Yellow-rumped Warbler, but when reviewing his photos, he got excited because he didn't know what it was.  I looked at the photo and identified it as a male Magnolia Warber.  We searched for the bird and shortly thereafter it popped up in some willows near the creek.  Our entire group, except one, got to see it.
