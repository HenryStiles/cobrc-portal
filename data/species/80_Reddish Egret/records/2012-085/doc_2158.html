Description of the Bird

A dark heron like bird, that was dancing around, with the wings lifted, going back and forth along the edge of the pond. The size was smaller than Great Blue Herons. The dancing around, eliminated Little Blue Heron. See photos taken by David Chartier.
Behaviors: The bird was dancing around, with the wings lifted, going back and forth along the edge of the pond.
Call: None
Plumage: dark morph juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Great Blue Heron much larger and doesn't dance around.

Little Blue Heron, adult, doesn't dance around.
