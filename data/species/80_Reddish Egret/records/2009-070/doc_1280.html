Description of the Bird

Bird appeared to be an immature bird. The bill of the bird was very dark and only one color, the coloring on head and neck was rufous color, but lacked the long shaggy plumes. The body of the bird was gray. I was unable to see the leg color of the bird due to the depth of the water it was in.
Behaviors: During the time that I observed the bird it was mostly standing very still without it’s wings spread. A couple of times it did walk around and display the sudden movements this bird is know for such as a suddenly change direction.
Call: none heard
Plumage: dark morph possible juvenile

Similar Species Discussion

Little Blue Heron-smaller bird, bi-colored bill all ages, darker colored body.
