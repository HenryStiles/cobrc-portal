Description of the Bird

Smaller heron then Great Blue Heron. Reddish feathers on head and neck, with grayish body. Bill mostly black, with a little lighter color at the base. See photos.
Behaviors: Seen in flight and walking and dancing along the shore.
Call: none
Plumage: immature, second year

Similar Species Discussion

Little Blue Heron - wouldn't have the reddish head and neck.
