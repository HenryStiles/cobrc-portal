Description of the Bird

-Slender egret with fairly long neck
-White morph (all white) with dark lores, dark long heavy straight bill, and dark legs
-Observed the bird changing locations in the shallow water but not feeding
-The bird was observed by others on the 20th, and several observed the bird in a nearby location on the 22nd
-The observers on the following day (the 22nd) observed the distinctive "canopy feeding"
 

Similar Species Discussion

-not as large as a Great Egret and did not have the yellow bill
-longer and more slender than a Cattle Egret and with a longer bill 
-a little larger than a Snowy Egret and did not have the Snowy's yellow lores or feet
