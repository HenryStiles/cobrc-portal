Habitat

Large shallow lake on the plains with exposed alkaline flats in places and reeds to the water's edge in other places. The bird was in front of one of the reedy sections.

Description of the Bird

Tall thin white bird with long thin legs and neck
Behaviors: The bird was standing in shallow water, running back and forth then suddenly stopping hopping turning and spreading its wings.
Call: none
Plumage: white morph

Similar Species Discussion

Even at a huge distance in poor light the bird seemed to be a large white egret. Conditions prohibited me from seeing legs, bill or any plumes, however the behavior was distinctively that of a Reddish Egret.
