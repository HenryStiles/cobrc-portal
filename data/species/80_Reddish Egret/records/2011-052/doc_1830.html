Description of the Bird

A medium sized egret dancing around and canopy feeding. Two toned pink and dark bill. See photo and video.
Behaviors: Dancing around and canopy feeding.
Call: none
Plumage: adult white morph

Similar Species Discussion

All the other white herons/egrets aren't known to dance and canopy feed like this bird was doing. The two toned pink and dark bill eliminates immature Little Blue Heron, Great Egret, Snowy Egret, Cattle Egret.
