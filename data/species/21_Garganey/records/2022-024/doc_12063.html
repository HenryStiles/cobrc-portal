Description of the Bird

Handsome male duck swimming with Blue-winged Teal at far north of Boyd Lake. Warm brown patterned head with curving white stripe over eye extending to back of head, pale gray flanks, stripes of black, white and grey scapulars visible while swimming. Dark bill.   It was very easy to pick out and photograph this duck late in the day.  

Similar Species Discussion

Blue-winged Teal have different colored and patterned head.
