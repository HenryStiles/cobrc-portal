Description of the Bird

Larger than there neighboring HERG, bigger chested, thicker billed, shorter winged. Uniform in "lightness" as seen from a lateral view while perched. The primaries were no darker than the tertials nor the body. Rather pale, pale enough so to make me think ICGU for a moment. Both birds had gray backs that were similar in darkness to the brown body. The patterning seemed smudgy. When the bird flapped we could see uniformly colored wings (no dark on primaries, secondaries) and a white and dark patterned (barred or perhaps spotted dark on white background) rump and solid pale smoky tail -- the tail, same darkness as remainder of body. Bill all black. 

Similar Species Discussion

To large, short-winged, heavy billed for THGU or ICGU
GLGU would have bicolored bill, etc. GLGU x GWGU would be paler yet, with some pink at base of bill, typically paler primaries than tertials/wing coverts.

GWGU x HERG -- should have some dark on dorsal surface of wing. Typically some of HERG shape is evident: smaller head and/or bill, longer wings, less smudgy plumage.
