Description of the Bird

HERG sized Larus gull, with heavy bill dark bill, pale at base. Uniforned light grayish brown mantle, wing coverts and wings including primaries. Lighter head, neck and breast with minimal, subdued streaking. Pale pinkish legs and dark iris.
Behaviors: hanging around with other gulls, occasionally swiming, flying and foraging
Call: none
Plumage: 2nd cycle
