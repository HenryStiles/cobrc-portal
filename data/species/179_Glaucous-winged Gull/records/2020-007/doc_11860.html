Description of the Bird

adult breeding: white head with minimal brownish smudging on face, heavy yellow bill, gray back with gray wingtips perhaps slightly darker than back, clean white underparts, pink legs and feet. Observed at close range in flight and at rest.

Similar Species Discussion

Adult Glaucous would have a bright yellow eye and unmarked white wingtips. Adult Western would have black wingtips.
