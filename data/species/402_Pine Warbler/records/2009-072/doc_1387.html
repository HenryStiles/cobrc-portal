Description of the Bird

A warbler with yellow throat, white underparts, green back, long tail, two white wing bars, thin somewhat long bill. See photo.
Behaviors: singing from the pine trees
Call: singing loudy, loud thrill somewhat like Chipping Sparrow
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow-throated Vireo - has thicker bill, lacks green back.
