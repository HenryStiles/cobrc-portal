Description of the Bird

[Photos will be more definitive descriptive than my text. Aaron Wald has dozens of more photos, if desired]

Yellow, warbler-like bird, feeding at our backyard feeders on a snowy morning (6 " snow on the ground). Elevation 7,500' at our feeder in Evergreen, CO, 80439. Ponderosa pines dominant in our area.
Behaviors: Single pine warbler fed at both suet at seed (Chickadee cheer) feeders. Seen morning of Dec 31, 2010; appeared for 10's of minutes, disappeared and returned an hour later. Perched on trunk and branches above feeders (at about 20').
Call: None
Plumage: Winter
