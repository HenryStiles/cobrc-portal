Description of the Bird

I saw a dull warbler, long tail, two wing bars, brownish colored back, light colored throat, belly and underparts. Dark cheek patch with spectacles. Look at photos.
Behaviors: In pine trees with Yellow-rumped Warblers.
Call: none
Plumage: immature

Similar Species Discussion

Long tailed warbler with wing bars. Eliminates other warblers that looks similar. Others have short tails.
