Description of the Bird

A warbler hanging out in the pines, up high. The bird had a thin warbler bill. The bird was quite dull colored underneath, with white wing bars, all green back (no streaks), long tail. Dark cheek patch, with somewhat of an eyering around each eye. See the photos which show all field marks of the bird.
Behaviors: Staying high in the pine trees.
Call: giving a warbler chip, that was somewhat loud. Similar to Townsend's Warbler, though not as loud.
Plumage: first year

Similar Species Discussion

The all green back, long tail, wing bars, eye ring, ear patch, light colored bill, and dull color elimnates all other species
