Description of the Bird

I found a warbler was a little yellow on the throat and white belly and under tail coverts. It had a long tail, two white wing bars, greenish back without streaking. Yellow spectacles. Dark cheek patch. See photos.
Behaviors: Staying the pines with Yellow-rumped Warblers.
Call: none
Plumage: immature
