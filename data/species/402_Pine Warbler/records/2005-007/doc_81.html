Description of the Bird

A warbler with bright yellow throat, green back, two white wing bars on both sides, long bill, yellow spectacles around each eye.
Behaviors: The bird was in trees and came to a bird seed feeder.
Call: heard long chip several times.
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Yellow-throated Vireo - has a different bill shape, and doesn't give warbler like chip notes, also, the rump and mantle color are different.
