Description of the Bird

Juvenile found May 25, 2008 in mature stand of Cottonwoods about 30 feet off the ground.

While this is not an unusal bird for the state of Colorado the location of this juvenile is very unusal. The location was about 22 miles Northwest of Limon, Coloardo.

The bird was a dark brown on the back and a lighter buffy color in the breast. The owlet had the classice white face patch extending from just above the bill to over each eye.
Behaviors: Sitting on a branch about 30 feet in the air.
Call: none
Plumage: Juvenile
