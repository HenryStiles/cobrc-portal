Feeders at a house in a pine forest.

Description of the Bird

Wings about the same length as the tail. Wings and tail is black and a white spot at teh end of the tail. A black bill with the base of the lower mandible red. Rows of green spots on the breast and chin of the bird. A white belly and stripe above and below the eye. A black stripe coming down from the eye. A green back and a brownish-green head.
Behaviors: Came into the right feeder at Bill and Kim Fisher's House.
Call: Didn't hear it.
Plumage: Probably an adult

Similar Species Discussion

Female Blue-throated Hummingbird and female Broad-billed Hummingbird - Has a line from the eye, but it is not black, it is gray. Larger than a White-eared Hummingbird. Doesn't have green spots on the throat and breast, the throat and breast is gray.
