Description of the Bird

I am very new, but I've seen Hummingbirds upclose before. This bird, and the male I've seen here too, are small; VERY small, even for Hummingbirds. They had been here a couple days when I finally had a camera in my hand when I heard her go to a feeder. I snapped the shot and she was gone. Both continue to frequent my yard and the feeders here daily. As a matter of fact, the female may have been the one I had to capture in my garage. Too bad I had no idea what I had a hold of, perhaps I’d have taken a better look. She was quite tired and I tried to get her to take a slurp of sugar water I dripped onto my finger from a feeder, to no avail. The male hovered in front of my face as I held her; she was calling. When she wouldn’t drink, I simply released her.
Behaviors: Both birds are shy...I've taken photos of hummingbirds before; these don't seem comfortable with me moving around...even from inside the window.
Call: Quiet
Plumage: Green head/back, pale undersides

Similar Species Discussion

The bird is so very tiny. Others viewing the photo comment on the short bill and short tail.
