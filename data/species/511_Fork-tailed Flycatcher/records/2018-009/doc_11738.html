Description of the Bird

Black head down to below the eye.  Completely white on the front from below the bill to the tail coverts.  Blue upper back and dark lower back and wings.  Two very long black tail feathers that showed very clearly both when the bird was perched and especially when it was flying. Very typical flycatcher behavior of chasing after some prey item and then returning to the same perch, or to some other position with a good view.

Similar Species Discussion

There is nothing very similar.  Not the right colors or plumage to be a Scissor-tailed Flycatcher.  Tail very much longer than a Magpie.
