Description of the Bird

A medium sized chunky shorebird with BRIGHT orange legs. Smallish head and when sitting or standing still appeared to have no neck. When walking or active the neck appeared to be long and thick. Steep forehead with a very flat crown. Medium thick, sturdy and slightly decurved black bill. The head was light brown with a dark mask over the eyes and a light gray patch above the bill. The upperparts were boldly patterned; black centered feathers with buffy edges.The underparts were mostly white except for the upper breast which was light brown with an incomplete brown and black breast band. The flanks had some brownish and black spotting. Undertail coverts appeared to be white.
Behaviors: Mostly feeding or sitting along the edge of the reservoir.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
