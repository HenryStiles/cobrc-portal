Description of the Bird

Small warbler sized bird. Gray upperparts with slight black streaking. White underparts with yellow chin, throat, and breast. Yellow supercilium with white at rear. Yellow crescent under eye, black lores. Black streaking along flanks, two white wing-bars, and extensive white on tail.
Behaviors: Most of the time singing from pine tree.
Call: As described in Nat. Geo., "rapid, accelerating trill."
Plumage: adult
