Description of the Bird

Small warbler sized bird. I believe this is a male but could be wrong. Gray upperparts with slight black streaking. White underparts with yellow chin, throat, and breast. Yellow supercilium with white at rear. Yellow crescent under eye, black lores. Black streaking along flanks, two white wing-bars, and extensive white on tail.
Behaviors: Feeding with a small flock of Yellow-rumped Warblers, moving rapidly through area.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
