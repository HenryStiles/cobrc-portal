Description of the Bird

In comparison with adjacent Horned Larks , size was a little shorter and compact , short tail , fairly long primary wing projection . Overall coloration was a pale sandy brown , pale supercilium , pale brownish auricular patch , underparts and flanks off white and unstreaked with ( from photo ) faint blackish band across upper breast. Three chestnut median coverts also visible . In flight showed an upside down black T on a white tail. Long hind claw also noted.
Behaviors: Feeding on the ground alongside the dirt access road to the reservoir in company with Horned Larks . Made a couple of short bouncy flights before landing and commencing feeding on small seeds on the road.
Call: dry rattle call
Plumage: winter

Similar Species Discussion

Only other longspur with similar black on white tail pattern , CCLO is darker overall with streaking on breast if female and if male much more extensive black on breast and belly and black and white median coverts not chestnut. SMLO underparts are completely buffy with some streaking , different tail pattern , LALO has dark borders to the auricular , different tail pattern , rufous in the greater coverts and tertials , and streaking on the breast and flanks.
