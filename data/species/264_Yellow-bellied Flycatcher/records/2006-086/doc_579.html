Description of the Bird

Empidonax flycatcher with very green head and back, dark wings with two whiteish/yellowish wing bars. Yellow throat, yellow underparts, yellowish eye ring.
Behaviors: Photographed in the hand, and seen high up in the trees above the banding station at Chico Basin Ranch.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The two "Western" type flycatchers do not have such a green head and back, the eye ring is more tear droppy. These birds would be bigger.
