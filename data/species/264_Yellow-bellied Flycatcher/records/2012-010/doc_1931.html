Description of the Bird

I found and photographed a small and compacted flycatcher with yellow throat, green back, almond eye ring, black wings, with wing bars. I sent photos to Chris Wood, and he agreed that this bird was a Yellow-bellied Flycatcher.
Behaviors: Staying low, near the Arkansas River, in Rock Canyon in Pueblo.
Call: none
Plumage: fall plumaged

Similar Species Discussion

Western Flycatchers have bigger tear dropped eye rings, one was present at the same time, it was bigger and browner on the back, compared to this bird which was green backed.

Resources Used

Chris Wood looked at my photos to make sure it was what I identified it as.

Previous Experience

Seen a couple in Colorado. Also, quite a few in New Hampshire in summer time.
