Description of the Bird

Large bird with obvious large decurved blackish bill. Redish orange eyes. Long rounded tail. Wing brown. Median coverts tipped with white, forming two slight wing bars. Sides of neck and head brown. White patch on cheek. Chin and center of throat white with brown submalar stripe. Flanks more cinnamon brown. Other underparts dull buffy white. Some spotting on chest and breast. Dark brown legs.
Behaviors: Eating at backyard feeder
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
