Description of the Bird

Orange eyes,slightly smaller than grackle, thick curved bill, thin wing bars, gray in color with spotting on chest, breast spot, and short wings.
Behaviors: The bird seemed to stay on the ground for MOST of the visit until he flew to the neighbor's lilac bush that hangs into the southern part of the yard. He moved or walked on the ground flipping things over in the garden before moving to the water feature for a drink. He then ate sunflower chips that were scattered under the feeder.
Call: None
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Bendire's Thrasher has yellow at base of bill and more yellowish eye. I also looked at every possible field guide and web comparing my photos with Bendire's.
