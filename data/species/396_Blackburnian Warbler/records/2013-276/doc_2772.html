Description of the Bird

Clear yellow throat and breast, white belly.
Two very strong white wing bars on blackish wings.
White sides to tail.
Face orange-yellow w/black through eye. Black cap, yellow on face, almost around nape.
Behavior: Initially with yellow-rumps. Then we saw it across the stream, alone. It crossed back and disappeared into some Russian olives.

Similar Species Discussion

Other warblers ruled out by
        Orange face;
        White wing bars; and
        White sides to tail.
