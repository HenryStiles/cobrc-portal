Description of the Bird

Dendroica warbler with an orange throat , orange supercilium , dark auriculars , dark cap , black flank streakings on pale breast , white wing panel and white braces on dark back.
Behaviors: Came in to Chickadee tape , very active feeding high in Russian olive or cottonwood tree.
