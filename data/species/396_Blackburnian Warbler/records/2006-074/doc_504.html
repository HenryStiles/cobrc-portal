Description of the Bird

Small warbler. Crown and nape black. Stripe above eye, sides of neck, chin, throat and neck bright orange. Wide black streak through eye. Thinner streak below eye connecting at the back of the eye to form a black patch. Uunderparts pale yellow lightening to almost white at the lower belly. Under tail coverts white. Sides and flanks streaked with black. Black back streaked with some white. Broad white wing patch.
Behaviors: Foraging high in a cottonwood.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
