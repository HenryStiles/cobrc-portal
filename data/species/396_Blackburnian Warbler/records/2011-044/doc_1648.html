Description of the Bird

The bird was a orange , black and white warbler. The birds head was orange, more intense on the chin and throat. It had a black bill and eyes. There was a black eye stripe, black auriculars and a black neck stripe that continued on to the lateral side of the chest. The black stripe broke up into streaking on the flanks. The eye stripe contained an orange lower eye arc. The black crown was divided by an orange patch. The belly and undertail coverts were white. The wings were dark with a large white patch. The back and top of the tail were not seen well.
Behaviors: Foraging in the top of a cottonwood tree that was just beginning to leaf out.
Call: none heard
Plumage: breeding
