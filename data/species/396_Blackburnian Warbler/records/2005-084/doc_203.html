Description of the Bird

Dendroica Warbler with orange throat fading to white at belly. White from mid-belly to undertail. Black mask, with orange supercilliam. Dark lores and orange crescent below eye. Black streaking in flanks. 2 white wingbars showing no significnt differnce in thickness. Dark back with white braces.
Behaviors: Actively feeding on insects.
Call: none
Plumage: probable first year male

Similar Species Discussion

TOWA is superficilly similar. Orange throat eliminated all warblers.
