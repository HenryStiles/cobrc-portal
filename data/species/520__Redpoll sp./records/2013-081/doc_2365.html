Description of the Bird

Pale lightly marked, stub-billed redpoll, with puffy head and small poll. Large nearly unmarked pinkish rump patch. Multiple fine streaks on vent. See photos
Behaviors: At feeders with other redpolls
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Bird had very Hoary like aspect but also had those troubling undertail covert streaks.
