Description of the Bird

Adult seen soaring at a distance over a period of over 25 minutes; broad-winged, short-tailed, and blackish overall, recalling Black Vulture; one bold white band on tail, most obvious from above; flight feathers slightly paler than underwing coverts; bill yellow; soared on flat wings; flapping shallower and circles tighter than Black Vulture, recalling Red-tailed Hawk.
