Description of the Bird

Similar Species Discussion

First I considered the other black raptor with white in the tail. This bird was too sticky and broad winged, as well as only the one broad tail band compared to three white bands on zone-tailed. The brownish wash in the back and inner flight feathers also pointed away from zone tailed. Zone-tailed also has a longish tail, this bird had a very short tail. A dark red-tail was also eliminated via that broad white band in the tail, other feathers meantioned for ZTHA as well as body and head structure as well as the yellow legs etc. The banded tail and broad wings also eliminated Harris’s Hawk etc.
