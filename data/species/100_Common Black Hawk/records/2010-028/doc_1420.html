Description of the Bird

A buteo that was very black, with short tail, larger than Broad-winged Hawk, shorter tailed than Zone-tailed Hawk. See photos.
Behaviors: Sitting in cottonwoods near ponds at Fountain Creek Regional Park Nature Center.
Call: none
Plumage: sub-adult

Similar Species Discussion

Zone-tailed Hawk - longer tail, more white bands.
