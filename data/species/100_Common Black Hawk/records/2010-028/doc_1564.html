Description of the Bird

Very black colored hawk. It had a short tail with long legs, which makes it not look like a Buteo (which it isn’t). It has bright yellow legs and a bright yellow bill with a dark tip. It is an all black bird, even black eyes. The tail (which isn’t shown in my photo) had some thick bands. First from the end of the tail was a thin stripe of white, when a thick black band, and finally a thick white band.
Behaviors: All I saw the bird do is sit in a tree and look around.
Call: Didn’t hear it
Plumage: Adult

Similar Species Discussion

The tail with the thick black and white bands eliminates all the other North American hawks except Zone-tailed Hawks, but with only 1 thick white and 1 thick black stripe eliminates Zone-tailed Hawk and it also eliminates Great Black-Hawk. Also, with the all black body eliminates everything except Zone-tailed Hawks, but the short tail finally eliminates Zone-tailed Hawk.
