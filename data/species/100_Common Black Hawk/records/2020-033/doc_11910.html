Description of the Bird

Bird was discovered by Kevin Ash earlier that same day.   It was perched below the dam of Manitou Lake on the rocks next to the water.   It moved downstream (refound) and was perching on the bank by a pond formed by a beaver dam.   It was seen to be eating crayfish.
