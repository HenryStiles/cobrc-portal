Description of the Bird

Initially found by Ada Jones and Linda Chittum on 09/21/2022. The bird was observed flycatching from various dead branch perches along Q 3/4 and a pond on private property.The bird was a kingbird type with a large black bill, dark eye and legs.Head was dark gray from the gape straight back to the nape. The nape, back, wings and upper and lower tail were also drak gray. The throat and upper breast were white with the white extending along the side of the neck toward the nape. The lower breast, belly and undertail coverts were a lemon yellow shade.

Similar Species Discussion

Consideration of Eastern, Cassins and Gray were quickly eliminated because of coloration and bill size.
