Description of the Bird

A shorebird with black legs and long wings, that was not that was about an inch or two bigger than the nearby Least Sandpiper. The bird had a black bill and buffy front and white underneath. See photos.
Behaviors: On the rocks along the Arkansas River with a Least Sandpiper and Killdeer.

Plumage: adult in winter plumage
