Description of the Bird

Adult seen amongst flock of RBGU on North side of Reservoir. Very large, bulky gull, far larger than nearby Ring-billed and California Gulls. Dark mantle and wings, heavy, yellow bill with a red splotch on lower mandible. No streaking around eye. White nape. Blocky head. Thick, white neck and breast. Eyes appeared dark. Hard to see the color of legs. Lesser Black-backed Gull eliminated by size, color or back, eye color, bill size, and streaking around eye and nape. Never heard. Viewed for a long period of time through binoculars and scope.

Similar Species Discussion

Lesser Black-backed Gull eliminated by bill size, body size, no streaking around nape, mantle color, and leg color
