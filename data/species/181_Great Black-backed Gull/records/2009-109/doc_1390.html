Description of the Bird

Large gull with black back and light pink color legs. Much larger than all the other gulls around. See photos. This bird has been documented every year since it was first found in March of 1993 at Pueblo Reservoir.
Behaviors: Sitting on tires, also swimming in the water, and standing on the shore.
Call: none
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Nothing is as big as a Great Black-backed Gull, with light pink colored legs and dark black back.
