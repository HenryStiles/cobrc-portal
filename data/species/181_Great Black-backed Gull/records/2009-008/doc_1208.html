Description of the Bird

A large gull that was bigger than all others gulls near it (Herring, Ring-billed, Lesser Black-backed.)Using a scope and at the distance we were at, the bird's head appeared all white and the eyes pale. The bill was very large and long, all yellow with a red gonydeal spot.

The neck and underparts were white, the mantle and upper side of the wings were very dark and blackish. All white tail. Pink legs.

We only saw the bird standing but could see white tips to the primaries.
Behaviors: Standing on ice.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

The size of the gull compared with other large gulls around it, size and color of the bill, very blackish mantle, white head, pink legs and age (adult) make it very easy to rule out other species of large dark mantled gull.
