Description of the Bird

Very large gull that dwarfed Ring-billed gulls next to it and was even distinctly larger than nearby Herring Gulls. The bird had black and white checked pattern on its back, not brownish, and black wing tips. The head was quite white with a seemingly small eye and huge all black bill with some lightening toward the base. The breast was white with fine black streaking and the rump was white. It had thick pink legs that has especially thick ankles.
Behaviors: Roosting on ice and in open water with a large group of smaller gulls near a landfill.
Call: na
Plumage: first winter

Similar Species Discussion

Huge size, huge black bill, black and white pattern rule out any other possible gull.
