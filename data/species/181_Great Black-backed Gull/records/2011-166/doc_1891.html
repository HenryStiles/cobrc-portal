Description of the Bird

Large white-headed gull with faint streaks on head and nape. Bill large, yellow with red gonys spot. In flight very slow wing beats. Mantle blackish, darker than nearby Lesser Black-backed Gulls. White tail seen in flight. White spots on P10 and P9 at least and black on primary tips contrasting slightly with back color.
Behaviors: Mostly sitting at edge of water on tires of the South Shore marina at Pueblo Reservoir.
Call: heard in flight and noticeably different from other gull calls at Pueblo Res but nothing I can describe.
Plumage: adult winter

Similar Species Discussion

Kelp Gull with yellow legs, not dull pink legs of GBBG.
