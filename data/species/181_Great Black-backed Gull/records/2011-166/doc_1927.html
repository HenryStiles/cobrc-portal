Description of the Bird

Two giant white-bodied, black-backed gulls with pink feet that towered above nearby Herring Gulls. Eyes pale, but not as bright as those of Herring Gull. See attached photos
Behaviors: Roosting with 1000 or so other gulls
Call: Not Heard
Plumage: adult

Similar Species Discussion

Combination of large size, black back, bulky bill, pink legs eliminate all other gulls
