Description of the Bird

Sparrow sized bird with gray head, white eye ring. Grayish white spot in front of eye, white submoustachial stripe. Dark malar stripe. Back brownish gray with slight grayish streaking, wings brown. Mostly white underparts with gray on flanks and gray streaking on breast. Tail dark blackish brown.
Behaviors: This bird was with a mixed flock of sparrows on the ground in grass and occasionally going to a gravel road. Continuously jerked tail upwards.
Call: not heard
Plumage: adult
