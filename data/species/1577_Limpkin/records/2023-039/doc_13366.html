Habitat

Optics:  10x binocs and x60 telescope.

Description of the Bird

Large wading type bird. Mostly brown plumage overall with white streaks on the head, neck and extending down onto the body. Long, decurved bill that was yellow at the base.  Brown eye and dark legs.

Similar Species Discussion

There is no other species that looks remotely like a Limpkin, so none were considered.

Previous Experience

Have seen this species many times in Florida, Central America and South America. Last seen one at Ramah Reservoir in June 2023.
