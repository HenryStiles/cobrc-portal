Description of the Bird

Similar Species Discussion

Great Blue Heron and egrets: Their bodies are a light build with a longer neck and slim bill

Night-Heron: Their bill is shorter and thick

Crane: Their neck is longer, smaller bill, and much taller 

Bittern: Different coloration and smaller bill 

Previous Experience

I have seen many Herons, Night-herons, and Egrets in my 27 years of birding here in Colorado and in Florida. I have seen one Bittern here in Colorado. I have seen a handful of Limpkins at various times in Florida.  Cranes are abundant in the fall in Bosque Del Apache in New Mexico, which I have been to a few times, along with seeing them in Florida.
