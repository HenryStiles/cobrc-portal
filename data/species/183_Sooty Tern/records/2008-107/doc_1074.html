Description of the Bird

A large tern with jet black back and bright underparts. The bird had white area above the eyes, black cap, with then black line from the bill to the eyes. Underwings were white with darker trailing edges to the wings. The tail was dark and forked.
Behaviors: Flying around the lake.
Call: none
Plumage: adult alternate

Similar Species Discussion

Bridled Tern: Underwing pattern different, mostly white and back color not as dark.

Resources Used

Sibley Guide to make sure which species it was.

Previous Experience

Seen many Sooty Terns in Florida at the Dry Tortugas and one at the Boliver Ferry in Texas. I haven't seen Bridled Tern.
