Description of the Bird

Bird was silent.  It flew around one of the small ponds, occasionally landing on the gravel road.  If I tried to approach more closely, it would fly briefly, and land on the road again.

Similar Species Discussion

Forster's and common terns are eliminated by bill color (yellow) and white patch on forehead above bill.
